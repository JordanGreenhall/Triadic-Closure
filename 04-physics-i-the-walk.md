# Physics I — Domain Entry and the Walk

Physics is entered as a subdomain of mathematics: the terminal mathematical object (document 02, Part B) arrives whole as From, and the domain is selected by its own threefold constraint — **self-transforming / invariant / coherent** structure — which, read from inside, is threefold causation (From-causation, With-causation, This-causation). Physics adds no new mode of being; realizability is realizability and actuality is actuality across all of mathematics. What differentiates physics is its particulars: which characteristics its actuals carry, hence which lineage its realizability-field develops along. (Full method: document 03.)

## 1. The registration rule (what counts as a physical standing)

Status: **Registered as a control rule.** A candidate structure has framework-native physical standing only when all five conditions hold:

1. **From-whole inheritance** — reached from the inherited prior object as a whole, not from a handle selected because mature physics wants that handle.
2. **Physics-domain constraint** — self-transforming / invariant / coherent.
3. **Vertical constitution stated** — what in it is From, With, and This, with no office treated as an external base.
4. **Physics-subdomain participation** — a rule for how it belongs to the self-transforming/invariant/coherent lineage, not an unregistered formal resemblance.
5. **Status and boundary declaration** — which content is framework-derived, which is mathematics-as-mathematics, which is empirical observation, which mature-physics content is comparison only.

If any condition fails, the candidate may still appear, but only as: mathematical comparison, empirical observation, conditional result, Conjectured, or Open. Load-bearing claims come from exactly **three permitted bases**: the framework's derived stack; mathematics as mathematics; empirical observation explicitly marked as observation (real measured numbers supplied via the translation bridge — the framework drives the concept, the world supplies only the number). Mathematics may supply formal consequences once the framework has licensed the mathematical object; a formal resemblance never licenses a mature physics standing by itself.
