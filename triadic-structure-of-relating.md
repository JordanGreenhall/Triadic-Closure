# Triadic Structure of Relating

This page is the current M1 control for the mathematics gate and first mathematical content developed in Part 9.2 of [The Triadic Structure of Relating — Canonical Foundation](triadic-structure-of-relating-rev-canonical.md). The canonical treatise carries the derivation; this page governs its status, warrant, scope, and downstream use.

## M1 boundary

M1 includes:

- the held/registered seam;
- the selected gate into mathematics;
- mathematical identity as exhausted by registration;
- boundary-registration at D1;
- the unmarked dyad, its non-collapse, and exchange-equivariance;
- the narrow recognition of weak discernibility.

M1 does not establish D2 symmetric-holding results, unordered cardinality, counting, integers, modules, continuum, involution, terminality, physics, or chemistry.

## Governing adjudication

| Claim | Live status | Warrant and exact reach |
|---|---|---|
| Every determinate holding has a held/registered seam. | **Registered** | **Forced by dependency.** A basis registers character without exhausting the individuation and occurrence of what it holds. |
| The seam supplies an available gate into mathematics. | **Registered** for the exhibited gate | **Selected.** The gate cuts from held individuation toward registered character. Its availability is forced; choosing this domain boundary is not uniquely compelled, and alternative gates from the same ground remain legitimate. |
| Mathematical identity is exhausted by registration. | **Registered** within the selected boundary | A content is admitted when no basis registers a difference relevant to its identity. The boundary excludes event-particularity, which-taking, embodied occurrence, and designation among registrably identical positions. It necessarily retains vertical form, conditionedness, and orientation. |
| Isomorphism-invariance, the lineage-event / lineage-structure distinction, and portability follow from the boundary. | **Registered** at the displayed reach | Event-history is excluded while registrable order-type remains. Portability means the gate is available wherever holdings stand; it does not derive every mathematical theory or show that every domain instantiates every mathematical structure. |
| D1 registration is boundary-registration. | **Registered** | **Forced by construction** within the exhibited gate. There is no internal mathematical basis before the first mathematical content. |
| The first mathematical content is one unmarked dyad together with distinct orientation-form. | **Registered** | **Forced by construction within the selected boundary.** The dyad has internal two-ness, mutually exclusive positions, and identical position-character. Orientation belongs to the act's vertical form and does not privilege either pole. |
| Registered identity does not collapse the dyad. | **Registered** | **Retorsion-secured and forced by office-construction** at this reach. A cut cannot identify its own sides without denying the distinction it deploys; With holds the distinguished positions in multiplicity. The dyad is one content whose internal articulation is itself registered. |
| Position labels are computational scaffolding; constructions must be exchange-equivariant. | **Registered** control rule | Any result that changes when `+/-`, `0/1`, or equivalent labels are exchanged has spent designation excluded by the boundary. |
| The dyad exhibits weak discernibility. | **Registered and Sealed** at the exact mapping | **Recognition.** The positions are discerned by an irreflexive relation while sharing every monadic character. The name carries no further philosophical or mathematical surplus here. |

## Interpretation rule

When Part 9.2 calls the unmarked dyad “forced,” the force is conditional on the selected mathematics boundary. The boundary itself is an exhibited selection over a universally available seam. These are different warrant claims and must not be collapsed.

The older phrase “sealed as recognition” is read through the current two-axis vocabulary: **Recognition** names the warrant route; **Registered and Sealed** names the status of the exact weak-discernibility mapping. It does not seal every doctrine associated with that inherited term.

## Downstream use

Downstream mathematics may inherit only the M1 object and controls stated here: the selected registration boundary, boundary-registration, the unmarked dyad, orientation-form, non-collapse, and exchange-equivariance. No ordinary mathematical primitive, completed totality, continuum, linear structure, pairing, or theorem follows merely from calling this domain mathematics; each requires its own walked unit.

Executable checks are evidence only at their stated finite scopes. They do not supply the warrant for this gate or license the name mathematics. See [Verification Companion](verification-companion.md).
