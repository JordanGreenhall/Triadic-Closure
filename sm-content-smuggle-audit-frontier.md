# Standard Model Content Smuggle Audit and Cleanup Frontier

This page audits where Standard Model or mature-physics content is currently doing
load-bearing work without being fully established from one of the three permitted
bases:

1. the Foundational stack itself, built in order from retorsion/premises and then conjectured, registered, sealed, etc. according to Framework rigor.  For example the full suite of the From, With, This closure, the entire floor, etc. are all available;
2. mathematics as a domain (for example, anything that is part of "mathematics" may be used);
3. empirical observation, explicitly marked as observation (for example, any empirical observation may be used - but be careful not to smuggle in unregistered physics content.  If we haven't yet established "the electron" as a framework registered concept, then the measure of the "mass of an electron as an empirical event can only be understood as the "measure of some kind of particle".

A physics name may be used as framework-native only to the degree its content has been Registered inside the framework. A particle name such as `electron` may be usable if registered, but its spin, chirality, gauge representation, charge value, Yukawa origin, or decay role may not be imported unless each is separately registered or explicitly empirical. The major work of this frontier is properly Registering content that is necessary for downstream work (e.g., the content of "color".)

## Authority note

The current control layer already warns against overclaiming. This audit sharpens
the anti-smuggle frontier: the highest-risk errors are not usually false claims;
they are premature use of mature SM content downstream of partially registered
essential-form claims.

**Current support:** [P1](physics-registration-theorem.md) now PASSES as the canonical expenditure and dependency-map control. **Cleanup work:** no P1 control debt remains. Each mapped consumer must still construct every genuinely additional phase, carrier, operation, or property it spends and register inherited names property by property at the owning unit. It must not be asked to re-prove inherited structure merely because the domain is called physics. A downstream failure is handed back locally; it does not reopen P1 unless the boundary rule or map itself is incomplete.

## Bottom-line diagnosis

The framework has a strong essential-form spine. The main discipline failure is
not that SM content appears; empirical physics and sealed recognition are allowed.
The failure is that mature SM content sometimes migrates from orientation or
comparison into load-bearing derivation before its framework-native content has
been registered.

The most fundamental cleanup sequence is:

physics registration -> amplitude/readout -> epsilon/SU(3) bridge -> spin/chirality
-> charge/module -> particle identities -> mass/decay numerics -> spectrum/Higgs/Yukawa.

Do not attempt downstream numerical particle claims until the upstream bridge for
the named objects and their licensed properties is explicit.
