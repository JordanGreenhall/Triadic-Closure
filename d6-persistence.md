# P6 — Persistence, Stability, and the Decay Boundary

This is the canonical P6 record. It separates:

1. the generic D6 phase-form;
2. which physical constructions actually satisfy it, at what scope and conditions;
3. the familiar names persistence, bound state, stability, metastability, and decay;
4. the additional requirements for domain-grade persistence, universal stability, observed rates, atoms, spectra, and a stable-closure hierarchy.

Walk order is not epistemic authority. A result first articulated in a later unit may establish or constrain P6 whenever its constitutive content satisfies the P6 criterion. What may not be spent backward is genuinely additional structure on which that result depends.

## Unit boundary

P6 owns:

- invariant-preservation across an exhibited D5 variation-field;
- recognition of every physical construction, wherever articulated, that satisfies that criterion;
- the persistent-composite criterion;
- structural stability and metastability criteria, conditional on exhibited perturbation and transition structure;
- the structural distinction between persistence and decay;
- the boundary between escape weight and observed decay rate;
- identity-continuity through preserved invariants.

P6 does not by itself supply later gauge, particle, mass, atomic, chemical, or quantitative structures. But later constructions may bear evidence for P6 if they exhibit a closure, its identity-constituting invariant, a declared variation-class, and preservation or return across that class. Their later location neither disqualifies nor upgrades them.

## Authority and applicability adjudication

The canonical foundation governs the generic D6 phase-form: given a D4 invariant and a standing D5 field of admissible variation, D6 consists in carrying that invariant coherently across allowed passages through the field.

The older physics walk added exact `3+1`, atoms, confinement, the particle spectrum, and Higgs claims. Those do not survive merely as assertions. Exact `3+1` is governed by P4; the others require their own constructions.

Two later constructions are already constitutively applicable to P6:

1. **Modeled baryon closure.** The singlet/complete-closure class is preserved and restored under two declared perturbation/return flows. This witnesses scoped conditional physical persistence and stability.
2. **Electron bare repeat.** The color-trivial electron closure is re-selected as the same standing per step; the bare repeat is Registered as the native mass ruler `m_e = 1`. This witnesses scoped conditional persistence at the per-step recurrence class. It does not by itself establish perturbative stability.

The correct discipline is neither forward-only exclusion nor unrestricted backward import. Ask:

> Does the result exhibit the P6 invariant and preservation structure, or does it require genuinely additional ontology that is being smuggled into P6?

Recognition may run backward through the written walk. Constitution does not.

## 1. D6 phase-form

Let a physics construction supply:

- a D4 invariant `I`;
- a D5 variation-field `V` whose members and passages are explicitly admitted by the relevant physical construction;
- a standing closure `x` whose lineage-related presentations can be compared under `I`.

Then `x` persists over `V` exactly when every admitted passage in the declared scope preserves the registered invariant by which `x` is re-identified.

Persistence is preservation across allowed variation, not a substance enduring inside a time-container. P5 supplies finite succession and re-resolution; P6 asks whether an invariant survives the declared class of those passages.

The generic D-series secures this form and dependency order. It does not guarantee an unrestricted or domain-complete physical population.

## 2. Physical P6 witnesses

Physical P6 occupancy is claim- and scope-specific, not all-or-nothing.

### 2.1 Modeled baryon closure

- **closure:** gauge-equivalence class of complete color-singlet triads;
- **invariant:** complete closure / singlet registration under the selected color-overlap structure;

- **preservation/return:** tested starts and perturbations flow or return to the singlet while octet and decuplet components drain toward it.

Within that model and those flows, persistence and perturbative stability are Secured conditionally. Baryon/bound-state language is Registered only for the displayed core and inherits every gauge, closure, and modeled-dynamics dependency.

### 2.2 Electron bare repeat

- **closure:** the color-trivial electron standing;
- **invariant:** the bare repeat, the same closure recurring as itself;
- **variation-class:** per-step self-reselection in the mass/self-closure construction;
- **preservation:** each step re-selects the same color-trivial standing, supplying the framework-native normalization `m_e = 1`.

Within that recurrence scope, electron persistence is Secured conditionally and the native ruler registration is available. This is not yet a perturbative stability theorem: no broader perturbation/return class is exhibited merely by recurrence.

These witnesses do not establish:

- the earliest or ontologically most basic persistent closure;
- persistence under every physically relevant passage;
- universal confinement dynamics;
- the totality of persisting physical closures as one domain-grade D6 field;

Accordingly, the earlier blanket statement “physics D6 occupancy is Open” was too strong. The correct record is: **scoped physical occupancy exists; domain-grade occupancy, basicness, broader stability, calibration, and the closure hierarchy remain Open.**

## 3. Persistent composite and bound-state core

A composite satisfies P6 when its constitutive relation remains registered across the declared allowed passages. The constituents need not remain featureless or motionless; the identifying invariant must survive.

The inherited name **bound state** is Registered for this persistent-composite core, not Registered–Sealed. Mature bound-state language often carries a specified Hamiltonian, normalizable state, spectral threshold, localization, binding energy, asymptotic channel structure, and lifetime.

The modeled baryon satisfies the persistent-composite criterion at its reported flow scope. No claim about atoms, nuclei generally, or all hadrons follows without their own exhibited structures.

## 4. Stability

Persistence and stability are distinct.

- **Persistence:** the invariant is preserved across declared passages.
- **Stability:** under a declared perturbation class, perturbed states return to or remain within the invariant-preserving class.

Given perturbation, comparison, and transition/return structure, this is a Secured conditional classifier. **Stability** is Registered for that core.

The modeled singlet satisfies it for the tested perturbations and flows. The electron bare repeat supplies recurrence but not, by itself, a perturbative return theorem.

P6 does not generically supply a topology, universal dynamics, basin measure, attraction rate, energy minimization, or stability of every familiar standing. A fixed point is not automatically an attractor, but an actually exhibited return dynamics must not be ignored because it appears later in the written walk.

## 5. Metastability and decay

Given an exhibited passage structure, a standing is metastable when it preserves its invariant over a restricted passage-class but admits at least one allowed escape passage into a differently registered closure-class.

Decay, at P6 scope, is loss or reconfiguration of the prior invariant through an admitted escape passage. **Metastability** and **decay** are Registered for this structural core.

Products, conservation bookkeeping, mechanisms, barriers, and rates require their owning constructions. Later decay results may instantiate P6 regardless of P-number, but all genuine dependencies remain binding.

## 6. Escape propensity versus decay rate

An escape set may receive a relative weight only when an applicable weighting construction supplies a measure over admitted alternatives. P6 adds no weighting theorem, and any use inherits the positive-pairing and frequency debts of the weighting page.

A rate or lifetime additionally requires:

- a transition rule or hazard per step;
- a demonstrated frequency bridge;
- clock or step calibration;
- relevant scale and coupling data.

Thus structural escape-accessibility may be established conditionally, while generic decay rates remain Open unless a later owning construction supplies these further structures.

## 7. Identity through succession

Continuity of identity means continuity of the registered invariant across lineage-related passages. **Identity over time** is Registered for that invariant-continuity core. It does not imply that all properties remain unchanged or settle every metaphysical criterion of identity.
