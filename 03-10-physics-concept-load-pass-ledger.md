# Frontier Items 3-10 — physics concept load and native-derivation pass ledger

## Governing rule

Work backwards from the established physics concept's actual job. A framework loop
must not ask a physics concept to do more than that concept does in physics. Its
job is to produce the clean framework-native equivalent for the capacity actually
being used. If the framework uses a mature-physics fact not yet native, the loop
must derive/register that fact or mark the local use empirical/conditional/quarantined.

This ledger is not a proof. It is the pass-condition packet for looping agents.
Each loop must measure actual downstream usage before claiming PASS.

## Item 3 — Epsilon / SU(3) bridge

### 3.1 Fixed nonzero alternating complex 3-form / epsilon / determinant

- **Physics/math concept:** A fixed nonzero alternating 3-form on a complex 3-carrier; determinant/epsilon tensor as the invariant volume/closure form.
- **Actual physics job:** Provides the invariant antisymmetric color-singlet/volume structure; its stabilizer gives special linear structure, and with Hermitian pairing gives compact color `SU(3)`.
- **Current framework use:** Used to turn triadic office closure into a primitive fixed closure-floor; supports color singlet, baryon complete closure, group-volume terms, and the route to `SU(3)`.
- **Native pass condition:** Derive from framework primitives that full vertical ternary office-registration yields a scalar-valued, primitive, fixed, nonzero, alternating 3-form on one same-kind carrier. Must exclude symmetric cubic, vector-valued mediation, role-sorted carriers, projective-only volume, and operator-valued alternatives.
- **Fail condition:** The loop appeals to determinant/epsilon because `SU(3)` color uses it in physics, or treats vanishing-on-coincidence / alternation as obvious rather than derived.

### 3.2 `SU(3)` as color group

- **Physics concept:** Compact `SU(3)` color gauge symmetry.
- **Actual physics job:** Classifies color transformations preserving the color inner product and determinant/volume; supplies color representation structure for quarks, singlets, octets, etc.
- **Current framework use:** Used for color, quark/lepton split, baryon singlet, group volume, confinement/With-to-This checks, and proton mass terms.
- **Native pass condition:** Once the fixed alternating form and Hermitian pairing are native, prove the mathematical stabilizer step: `Stab(ε) = SL(3,C)`, pairing-preserving intersection gives `SU(3)`, and minimality gives dimension 3. State whether this is native physical registration or conditional mathematics.
- **Fail condition:** The SM color group name carries proof work before the carrier, fixed form, and pairing have been licensed.

### 3.3 Color / three colors / color singlet

- **Physics concept:** Color charge with three color states; color-neutral singlet.
- **Actual physics job:** Internal degree of freedom for quarks; singlet condition marks observable color-neutral hadrons.
- **Current framework use:** Used to distinguish quark-like interior resolution, baryon closure, proton identity, and color-sector mass terms.
- **Native pass condition:** Show that framework interior triadic distinction requires three same-kind internal alternatives and that complete closure corresponds to the invariant/singlet condition. Must distinguish empirical color labels from native closure roles.
- **Fail condition:** “Three colors” or “singlet” is imported as a familiar SM fact rather than derived from triadic interior closure plus Item 3/4 gates.

### 3.4 Group-volume terms, especially `Vol(SU(3)) = 2π⁵`

- **Physics/math concept:** Haar/group-volume of compact `SU(3)` under a chosen normalization.
- **Actual physics job:** Mathematical measure of the compact group, not by itself a mass derivation.
- **Current framework use:** Supports closure-inherited metric and proton With-This term / mass-ratio work.
- **Native pass condition:** Derive the exact normalization, show why this group measure is the framework-native closure payment being used, and state dependencies on native `SU(3)`, metric, and measure normalization.
- **Fail condition:** Numerical group volume is used as a physical payment without deriving why that mathematical measure is the framework quantity.
