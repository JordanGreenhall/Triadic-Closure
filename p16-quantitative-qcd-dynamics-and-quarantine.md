# P16 — Quantitative QCD Dynamics and Quarantine

## Controlling specification

P16 contains exactly:

1. the invariant pairwise color-conditioning operator form;
2. its representation-theoretic spectrum and sector decomposition;
3. structural confinement and the tested singlet-attractor result;
4. the boundary separating these from quantitative QCD dynamics.

P16 does not normalize masses, baryon energetics, proton/neutron splitting, decay, electroweak structure, generations, or the particle spectrum.

## 1. Invariant pairwise operator form

Given the P9/P10 `SU(3)` carrier and Hermitian structure, an invariant pairwise interaction on two color factors is affine in

`T_i · T_j = sum_a T_i^a T_j^a`.

The form is a downstream consequence of the established color symmetry and invariant theory. It may not be used upstream to prove the `SU(3)` bridge.

Standing:

- invariant pairwise operator form — Secured conditionally on the P9/P10 carrier; Registered;
- additive offset and overall scale — not fixed by the symmetry form;
- physical coupling magnitude — Open and values-side.

## 2. Representation consequences

For three fundamental color factors,

`3 tensor 3 tensor 3 = 1 + 8 + 8 + 10`.

This decomposition and the corresponding eigenvalue structure of the pairwise Casimir operator are Sealed representation mathematics once the `SU(3)` representation is admitted. The singlet sector is the color-neutral complete-closure sector.

P16 does not infer observed hadron masses, level spacings, cross sections, or running couplings from the decomposition.

## 3. Structural confinement

The framework licenses a narrow structural use of **confinement**:

- a color-resolved factor is not itself a complete color closure;
- the triad-spanning singlet is a complete color-neutral closure;
- under the two exhibited conditioning flows, the singlet class is fixed and tested perturbations return to it.

Therefore:

- structural colored non-closure — Registered;
- singlet complete-closure — Registered;
- tested-flow singlet attractor — Registered, not Sealed;
- universal uniqueness over every admissible dynamics — Open.

This scope does not import flux tubes, a linear potential, an area law, a mass gap, or universal dynamical confinement.

## 4. Quantitative QCD quarantine

The following remain external comparison material and may not do framework-warrant work:

- the magnitude and scale dependence of `alpha_s`;
- beta functions and asymptotic freedom as framework derivations;
- flux-tube or string-tension mechanisms;
- linear confinement potentials and Wilson-loop area laws;
- lattice-QCD numerical dynamics;
- empirical binding-energy decompositions;
- hadron masses, radii, form factors, scattering amplitudes, and cross sections;
- the QCD scale and dimensional transmutation.

These are not proof obligations for the narrower structural claims. Conversely, the structural claims may not be expanded to include them through the shared word **confinement**.
