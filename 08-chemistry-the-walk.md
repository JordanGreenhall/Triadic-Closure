# Chemistry — The Walk

Chemistry begins at the residue left by atomic closure. Physics closes color and charge, but a neutral atom may retain a shell edge. Chemistry is the domain of **shell-edge closure-completion**. Its first content is the bond; its recursion is catenation; carbon is the physics D7 provisioner at the grade stated below.

The inherited physics object enters whole: the D6 atom, exact 3+1 spatial structure, spin and Pauli holding, the U(1) valence axis, the closure measure and mass hierarchy, the realizability-weighting law, and the spatial-texture conditions carried by G1. Chemistry does not re-derive those structures and does not treat them as optional tools.

## 5. The shell-edge residue and carbon

Charge closure ends at neutrality but may leave the shell edge open. The successor domain inhabits that residue. Valence is the residual shell-edge; the bond is its completion.

A successor domain capable of recursion requires a provisioner with:

1. multiple simultaneous edges;
2. balance between giving and receiving;

\[

Hydrogen caps one edge; boron remains pre-midpoint; nitrogen and oxygen tend toward completion; silicon and germanium carry the same formal edge-count at larger, more screened scales and therefore weaker catenation.

Carbon is selected by octet midpoint plus smallest bridging scale. It is the catenation-maximizer in the company of hydrogen as cap and oxygen and nitrogen as hetero-completers.

**Grade:** carbon as the physics D7 provisioner is Registered-candidate.

## 9. D1 — the bond

The first chemical This is the minimal joint standing in which two residual shell edges terminate one another.

### 9.1 Pair capacity

One shared spatial mode under antisymmetric holding has capacity exactly two. The Lewis pair is therefore a theorem at the stated parent grade. Occupancy one is admitted, so \(H_2^+\) is a real but weaker bond rather than a contradiction.

### 9.2 Ownership

The pair belongs to the composite closure. Individual ownership is held interior texture; the composite completion is what registers. Covalent sharing and transfer are not two substances but positions within one apportionment continuum.

### 9.3 Bonding and antibonding

One standing over two centers has two linear combination modes. The symmetric mode relieves maintenance; the antisymmetric mode places a node between centers and adds load. Both modes are realizable; occupancy determines the standing. \(He_2\) is excluded both because helium has no residual shell edge and because four electrons fill both modes with no net relief.

Exactly two modes is a two-center result. For \(n\) centers there are \(n\) combination modes.

**D1 standings:** bond, pair capacity, held ownership, and the two-center mode fork are Registered-candidate at their inherited conditions.

# Part C — Passage Dynamics

## 13. Activation barriers

A passage partially opens prior closures before new ones close. Partial opening is unrelieved maintenance, so a minimal path between relieved basins generically has a nonnegative maintenance ridge.

Two limiting classes are distinct:

- **no-opening:** outstanding edges simply close; de-closure is zero and the barrier is exactly zero;
- **compensated concerted passage:** opening and closing overlap, but exact synchronization is itself constrained, so the residual barrier is generically small and nonzero.

Barrier existence and sign are Registered-candidate with G1 carried. The no-opening zero-barrier limit is Registered-candidate exact. The concerted synchronization residue is Conjectured-strong; magnitude is Open.

## 14. Rate form
