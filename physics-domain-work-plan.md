# Physics Domain Work Plan

This is the hinge-first work plan for the physics domain. It is a dependency plan, not a publication sequence and not an epistemic ladder.

## Working rule

Every task must run the applicable P1–P7 controls, architectonic clarification, inherited-content and population survey, three moves per stage, essential/mature split, two-axis claim record, adversarial pass, and propagation.

Traditional physics may orient and check. It may not silently determine the framework result.

## Walk order and applicability

The numbered walk records a route of articulation. It does not determine when reality becomes true or what evidence may bear on an earlier phase.

A later result may satisfy or constrain P6 or P7 when it actually exhibits the relevant constitutive content. Distinguish:

- **later recognition:** already-applicable content made explicit later;
- **later construction:** genuinely additional structure whose dependencies remain attached.

For P6, the current worked witnesses are baryon persistence/stability, electron recurrence/ruler, and neutron metastability/decay. For P7, later chemistry work may identify the actual successor boundary, D1 unit, and provisioning characteristics. Document position neither weakens nor strengthens these claims.
