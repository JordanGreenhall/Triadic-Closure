# Physics Domain Work Plan

This is the hinge-first work plan for the physics domain. It is not a publication outline. It is a dependency plan for making the wiki coherent and for preventing mature physics results from being reverse-fitted into the framework.

## Working rule

Every task below must run:

1. Architectonic clarification of the relevant presupposition.
2. Physics-walk checklist: Part 0 From in full; A frame; B instruments; C population; D grade; E adversarial pass.
3. Three moves per stage: dependency position, bottom-up combination, grade/dependency record.
4. Essential-form/mature-form split.
5. Domain-grade pacing: a specimen is evidence, not proof that a D-phase governs the relevant whole stock.
6. Final standing: Open, Conjectured, Registered, Registered and Sealed, or Defended posit, with the warrant route recorded separately.

Traditional physics may orient the search and supply empirical checks. It may not supply the metaphysical basis or silently determine the result.

## Priority 1 — Load-bearing and under-sealed

### 1. Seal or weaken the realizability-weighting law

Current status: Registered.

Why it matters: it underlies Born, collapse/actualization, F8/F9, and gravity-as-coherence-participation.

Work items:

- Prove or weaken the frequency=weight identity: coherence-participation is actualization-tendency, not merely monotonically related to it.
- Prove exact `C = |ψ|²` from the Hermitian pairing, not merely canonical self-pairing.
- Derive one non-physics instance end-to-end, preferably Boltzmann, without pattern-matching.
- Re-run adversarial check: could a domain's coherence-measure generate a monotone transform rather than identity? If no, show why from framework primitives.

Possible outcomes:

- Registered and Sealed: all three gates close.
- Registered: one or more gates remains argued but not proved.
- Conjectured: if frequency=weight cannot be secured and the law becomes a strong pattern rather than a framework result.

### 2. Record the F9 standing-state discharge

Current status: Registered and Sealed for the standing-state choice.

Why it matters: the conserved-flow stationary amplitude is the hinge beneath gravity's participation field and the linear degree tilt.

Work items:

- Patch result pages that still call conserved-flow a motivated choice.
- Record the ledger discharge: σ is the amplitude of the conserved accumulated standing; the adjacency eigenvector is a growth mode and fails conservation.
- Preserve the distinction: this closes the F9 standing-state choice, not the downstream strong-field/full-gravity gates.

Possible outcomes:

- Completed when `mathematization-F9-done.md`, result summaries, and this control layer agree.

### 3. Audit the SU(3) / gauge bridge claimed by the ledger

Current status: Registered, with ledger-claimed seal requiring adversarial audit.

Why it matters: exact gauge structure, quark/lepton split, confinement, and chiral matter downstream depend on it.

Work items:

- Start from the ledger's later claim, not the older open-bridge wording: the closure-floor is claimed to be the alternating form; continuity, pairing-preservation, and det=1 are claimed forced.
- State the exact theorem target:
  `full vertical ternary office-registration + accumulated complex pairing-bearing interaction content + role-faithful same-kind mediation + minimality => primitive nonzero alternating 3-form on C^3`.
- Explicitly test countermodels:
  - `C^2` role-sorted ternary functional;
  - Pauli/operator `End(C^2)` representation;
  - vector-valued ternary mediation;
  - symmetric cubic/trilinear invariant;
  - projective volume-line rather than fixed volume-form.
- Prove or deny seven bridge requirements: same-carrier, scalar-valued, complex multilinear, alternating, nonzero, primitive, fixed/normalized.
- If the ledger proof survives these countermodels, promote to Registered and Sealed; otherwise downgrade to Registered-relative-to-bridge.

Possible outcomes:

- Registered and Sealed: epsilon bridge closes and SU(3) follows.
- Registered: SU(3) follows relative to an explicit bridge premise.
- Conjectured/Open: if countermodels survive.

### 4. Strengthen the full gravity equation beyond weak-field

Current status: weak-field conditional model Conjectured; full Einstein route Conjectured and conditional.

Why it matters: gravity is a major claimed bridge from framework to mature physics.

Work items:

- Extend F10 from weak-field static g00-sector to spatial metric sector and 3+1 tensor.
- Derive or explicitly locate the second-order premise from framework content rather than relying only on Lovelock's theorem.
- Test strong-field / nonlinear regime on the conditioning-network.
- Clarify the relation between local degree, conserved-flow perturbation, curvature, and stress-energy without background density smuggling.
- Keep `G` as Open unless the network coupling coefficient is computed.

Possible outcomes:

- Registered and Sealed for full gravity form if second-order/full tensor/strong-field gates close.
- Promote the weak-field bridge only after deriving the carrier-depth/proper-time rule rather than selecting it.
