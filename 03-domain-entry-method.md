# The Domain-Entry Method

A successor domain is entered by clarifying its inherited presuppositions, receiving the prior object whole as From, identifying the successor’s selecting constraint, deriving its first content, and walking its D-series without importing mature vocabulary. The method is Registered. The cross-domain handoff mechanism is Registered-candidate as the generalization of one exhibited crossing.

## 1. Clarify presuppositions before use

A mature discipline operates with foundational notions it normally does not examine. Architectonic entry cannot borrow those notions in their ordinary form, because doing so either makes the derivation circular or generates phantom conflicts caused by the borrowed ontology.

For each presupposition:

1. state the ordinary concept and the work it performs;
2. identify what that concept already presupposes;
3. separate its essential capacity from its mature accretions;
4. reconstruct the essential capacity from the inherited framework stock;
5. quarantine every remainder until it is separately earned.

### 1.1 Metric

A metric is not an arena-substance. It is accumulated registered distinction: a rule for comparing how much determinate difference a domain’s contents bear. Each domain owes its own metric from its own registered quality.

### 1.2 Causation

Causation is not an external push between already complete things. It is the inside-face of domain constraint:

- From: oriented dependency and succession;
- With: mutual holding and co-conditioning;
- This: invariant standing through the passage.

### 1.3 Individuation

Individuation is not a hidden numerical thisness behind registered character. A domain must state what distinctions its boundary registers and what merely held differences it excludes.

### 1.4 Possibility and actuality

Possibility is not a second inventory beside actuality. Admissibility is boundary permission; realizability is availability from S; actuality is a realized standing. Demands that presuppose two separately existing substances—possible and actual—must be tested for dissolution before they are accepted as work.

## 2. The inherited whole

The prior domain enters as **From**, whole and constraint-bearing. Every established feature of the inherited object binds the successor. It is not a menu of tools.

For physics, the inherited mathematical object includes rule-given complex structure, J, the two exchange sectors, the pairing, rho-coherence, conservation of registered distinctions, discrete grading, and rational linear composition. Any physical construction that silently drops one of these is incomplete.

## 3. The domain constraint

The successor domain is selected by its own constraint. That constraint is not merely an admission test; read from inside, it is the domain’s intrinsic causal architecture.

The physics constraint is threefold:

- **self-transforming:** contents bear their change-generator;
- **coherently held:** contents stand in mutual relation rather than as isolated trajectories;
- **invariant-bearing:** something persists across the transformation.

The corresponding threefold causation is From-causation, With-causation, and This-causation. Coherence, locality, and field are therefore present from the domain cut rather than manufactured downstream.

## 4. The four images at D1

At the first content of every domain, survey:

1. the marked triad—the domain’s From, With, and This capacities;
2. the From-flattening, whose signature is ordered/asymmetric;
3. the With-flattening, whose positions are interchangeable;
4. the This-flattening, whose positions stand in parity but are non-interchangeable in kind.

A proposed flattening whose symmetry character does not match its office is misidentified. The survey may require the whole early walk before each image is fully readable; it must not be forced into premature mature names.

## 5. Derive the first content

D1 is the minimal actual that satisfies the inherited whole under the successor constraint. It is not selected by familiarity or prestige. The construction must show:

- what distinction first stands;
- how the inherited object is borne in it;
- how the domain constraint holds it;
- what is excluded;
- why no weaker content satisfies the same dependencies.

A mature noun is attached only after this construction. “Particle,” “field,” “bond,” and similar names are recognitions, not primitives.

## 6. The three moves at every stage

### Move 1 — Dependency position

Determine what capacities become possible at this phase and what later structures will spend them. This orients the search without reverse-fitting a mature result.

### Move 2 — Construction from the standing

Combine only what already stands. Enumerate the admissible compositions that matter to the dependency load, exhibit the actual construction, and reject every route that spends excluded content.

### Move 3 — Grade and record

For every result state:

- its live status and warrant route;
- its exact scope and inherited conditions;
- the mature surplus not yet earned;
- what later claims may spend;
- what remains open and where it belongs.

Move 1 without Move 2 reverse-fits. Move 2 without Move 1 explodes into aimless combinations. Move 3 without the first two is bookkeeping. Together they orient, construct, and delimit.

## 7. The D-series

The generic phases are:

- D1: first distinction/content;
- D2: relation or multiplicity;
- D3: recursion or self-composition;
- D4: admissible passages and invariants;
- D5: mature domain field;
- D6: persistence;
- D7: successor-provisioning selection.

The phase form is generic; occupancy is domain-specific. A later phase may be absent. Every claimed occupancy requires construction and trace.

## 8. Crossing a domain gate

The general handoff mechanism is:

> **The successor domain inhabits the residue the predecessor’s closure did not consume, and its D7 element is the one that maximizes the successor’s recursion.**

Operationally:

1. identify the unconsumed residue of the predecessor’s closure;
2. state the successor constraint selected by that residue;
3. derive the successor’s D1 unit as the minimal completion-act;
4. identify the successor’s D3 recursion;
5. select the predecessor’s D7 provisioner by maximal support for that recursion, including necessary ambient partners.

In the exhibited physics-to-chemistry crossing, atomic charge closure ends at neutrality but leaves a shell edge. Chemistry is shell-edge closure-completion; the bond is its D1 unit; catenation is its D3 recursion; carbon is the provisioner at the grade stated in document 08, with hydrogen as cap and oxygen and nitrogen as hetero-completers.

The generalization has one fully exhibited instance. It does not by itself seal the chemistry-to-biology continuation.

## 9. Disciplines that travel with every entry

- **Structure native, magnitude located:** derive the structural form; identify coefficients and numerical values at their actual source.
- **Mathematics may be used as mathematics only after the mathematical gate:** it cannot retroactively certify the foundation.
- **Empirical observations enter explicitly:** the framework supplies the concept; observation supplies the number.
- **Named external theorems may be invoked at their exact scope:** invocation is not framework derivation.
- **No smuggled demands:** test whether the requested distinction survives the framework’s ontology before treating it as owed.
- **No theory completion by editorial inference:** a genuine unresolved conflict remains open in document 09.
