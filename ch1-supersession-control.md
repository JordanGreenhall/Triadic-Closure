# CH1 Supersession Control

This page records the claim-specific succession for the first chemistry unit.

## Earlier formulations

- The supplemental chemistry walk treated shell-edge completion as an already-crossed gate and used `Registered-candidate` language for the bond primitive.
- The semantic inventory retained the bond as the strongest framework-native reconstruction but left parent integration unresolved.

## Current governing formulation

- X1 governs whether the physics-to-chemistry gate is established: it is not.
- CH1 governs the bond primitive: a bond is one composite joint standing.
- The bond form is Conjectured-strong as the selected candidate D1 form and Unregistered as an established occupant.
- CH2 capacity, occupancy, ownership, and mode claims are not part of CH1.

## Execution rule

Use X1 for atomic/shell ground, gate validity, nonemptiness, scale separation, and carbon/handoff standing. Use CH1 for the candidate bond form. Do not revive the supplemental source's already-crossed-gate reading or mechanically convert `Registered-candidate` into Registered. Do not use X1's unfilled antecedents as a substantive refutation of the joint-standing form.
