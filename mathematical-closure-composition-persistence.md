# M2 — Closure, Composition, and Persistence Machinery

This document is the canonical M2 result. It formalizes signature-distinguished recursion, unmarked composition, module and pairing structure, rule-given scalar extension, conservation, and persistence limits. Lorentzian metric and spacetime claims are physics-lineage results and do not belong to M2.

## 1. Vertical recursion and horizontal variety

A position-only ternary carrier does not formalize the offices adequately. For the plain endofunctor `D(X) = X^3` in `Set`, with no observable difference among factors beyond position, the terminal coalgebra is degenerate: all states are bisimilar to the unique state of the singleton carrier. Position labels cannot carry office content.

Use a three-sorted, signature-distinguished carrier instead. The sorts record conditioning-, holding-, and arrived-signatures. This preserves the difference among office roles, but pure vertical recursion remains rigid: with sort as the only observable, it supplies one recursively presented solution per sort, not a universe of mathematical variety.

Variety is therefore horizontal. Different formations, lineages, registered characters, and compositions accumulate through the build; the vertical survey types every formation without generating its historical stock.

## 2. Unmarked combination

Let `J` act by exchanging two occurrences of the same summand.

Over the integers, decomposition into the `J`-fixed and `J`-negated parts has an index-two obstruction: recovering `(1,0)` from symmetric and antisymmetric components requires division by two. Thus a tag-free eigen-decomposition is not available for every integral pair.

Over the rationals, where `2` is invertible, every pair `(x,y)` decomposes uniquely:

`(x,y) = ((x+y)/2,(x+y)/2) + ((x-y)/2,(y-x)/2)`.

The first summand is `J`-fixed and the second is `J`-negated. Their distinction is registered by the action of `J`, not by marking one copy as intrinsically first.

This earns additive same-summand composition at rational grade. At integer grade, oriented ordered combination remains available, but the full unmarked eigen-decomposition does not.

**Standing:** Registered by elementary algebra. The result does not yet attach boson/fermion, particle, or physical exchange-statistics names.

## 3. Modules and pairing form

Free modules on registrably distinct generators provide a carrier for additive composition. A bi-additive, non-degenerate, `J`-compatible pairing can be constructed on such modules.

The following must remain separate:

- existence of a compatible pairing form is Registered;
- non-degeneracy can be imposed as an admissibility condition on the selected objects;
- positivity, Hermiticity, topology, completion, and dimension require their own constructions;
- numerical pairing values and scale are selections unless a later lineage derives them.

A pairing form therefore carries structural comparison and coupling capacity without supplying a physical probability measure or quantitative constants.

## 4. Rule-given scalar extension

The mathematics boundary admits content whose identity is exhausted by registered character. Within that selected boundary, an arbitrary rule-free subset of the rationals supplies no registered presentation and cannot be spent as a constructed content.

Rule-given real presentations are available. One adequate construction uses downward-closed rational cuts with no maximum, provided by a rule. Order is inclusion/entailment of presentations; arithmetic is defined by composition of rules; suprema exist schematically for rule-given bounded families when the union is itself rule-given. Rule-given complex numbers follow as pairs with the usual rule-defined operations and conjugation.

The standing is deliberately local:

- rule-given real and complex arithmetic is Registered within the selected mathematics boundary;
- unrestricted completed-continuum totality is not licensed by this construction;
- “predicative analysis” is a recognition label and contributes no warrant;
- any downstream use of impredicative or choice-heavy functional analysis owes an expenditure audit at the point of use.

## 5. Closure and recursive composition

Formal closure has two registers:

- vertical closure is the signature-typed, baseless co-constitution of the offices;
- horizontal closure is a formed unity whose components and lineage can be traced through the build.

A completed horizontal closure may be taken at unit-altitude for a further formation. That turn-over is the formal recursion used by M2. It does not erase the prior components and does not convert the new unit into an ontologically independent substance.

Coinductive language is an available recognition for baseless vertical recursion. Category-grade coalgebraic finality, universal terminality, and full `rho` coherence laws are not established merely by using that language.

## 6. Conservation

M2 inherits the monotone ledger rule: a distinction that has been Registered remains part of the standing unless an explicit later identification is earned. This is conservation of registered distinction.

It is not yet conservation of energy, probability, charge, mass, norm, or measure. Each named conservation law requires:

- the quantity to be constructed;
- the relevant passage or symmetry to be specified;
- invariance across that passage to be shown; and
- any empirical recognition to be separately graded.

**Standing:** conservation of Registered distinctions is Registered as a framework ledger rule. Named mathematical or physical conserved quantities remain Open until separately constructed.

## 7. Persistence machinery

Persistence is statable when an invariant is preserved across a standing field of allowed variation. At formal D6 grade this yields a carrier for persisting closures and admissible passages among them.

M2 establishes the form only. It does not establish:

- that a particular domain occupies D6;
- a category-grade set of coherence laws for the whole field;
- physical time, endurance, stability, bound state, or atom;
- categorical finality; or
- a successor-provisioning D7 occupant.

Those are domain occupancies or later recognitions. One persistent specimen cannot establish domain-grade persistence.
