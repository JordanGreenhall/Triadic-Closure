# Physics II — Internal Structure and Matter

The internal gauge architecture is **SU(3) × SU(2) × U(1)**. Its structural sources and the chiral/vectorial character of the couplings are derived; exact charges, mixing, symmetry breaking, masses, and generation count retain their stated open or values-side status. Technical derivations are collected in `appendix-internal-structure-derivations.md`.

## 1. Same-kind carrier and alternating closure

The three interior moments inhabit one same-kind complex carrier \(V\cong\mathbb C^3\) with common addition, scalar multiplication, comparison, and a non-degenerate Hermitian pairing. Dimension three is inherited from the primitive interior triad under the This-flattening and minimality. The closure verdict is scalar and is represented by a fixed nonzero alternating 3-form.

Three contributions close exactly when they span \(V\):

\[
\varepsilon(m_1,m_2,m_3)=\det[m_1\ m_2\ m_3]\ne0.
\]

The complex-linear stabilizer of the alternating form is \(SL(3,\mathbb C)\); imposing preservation of the Hermitian registration structure gives

\[
SL(3,\mathbb C)\cap U(3)=SU(3).
\]

The carrier theorem is **Registered** with named residuals. Native SU(3) is **Registered, not Sealed**; the stabilizer step is Sealed as mathematics.

## 2. Gauge sources and coupling character

The three office-sources are:

SU(3) is the pairing-preserving, determinant-one symmetry of the interior closure. No single interior moment closes independently; neutral closures are the complete triad and the moment–antimoment pair.

Gauge-source completeness is a **Defended posit** inherited from the exactly-three office ceiling. It does not fix electroweak breaking, the mixing angle, photon/W/Z particulars, or hypercharge.

## 3. Chirality

For the minimal spinor carrier, helicity is

\[
h=\sigma\cdot\hat p,
\]

with eigenvalues \(-1\) and \(+1\). Rotations about the propagation arrow are a registered symmetry. Because \(h\) is nondegenerate on the two-dimensional carrier, its commutant is

\[
\operatorname{Comm}(h)=\operatorname{span}\{I,h\}.
\]

A transverse Pauli component would register an additional direction and violate no-privileged-frame. An oriented coupling cannot reduce to the orientation-blind identity. Coherent attachment requires idempotency, and the nontrivial idempotents are

\[
P_L=\frac{I-h}{2},\qquad P_R=\frac{I+h}{2}.
\]

Pure-projector chirality and parity violation are therefore **Registered**. Which projector is selected remains the one free handedness selection. The commutant closure is **Registered-candidate**.

## 4. Quark and lepton

Every elementary fermionic closure carries the interior triad in germ. It stands relative to that interior in one of two elementary ways:

- **Lepton:** the interior triad closes within the elementary closure; the role is color-singlet and independently standing.
- **Quark:** the closure stands as one unresolved interior moment; external completion is required, giving color and confinement.

The split is binary. Standing as two moments is already composite. The same closure-floor that yields SU(3) therefore yields the quark/lepton distinction and the two routes to color neutrality.

The structural integer/fractional pattern is a consistency result: a complete closure stands at integer grade, while one resolved interior third stands fractionally. Exact charge values are not derived by this result.

## 5. Flavor and mark geometry

After color closes, three non-interchangeable marks remain. No mark is privileged, so the marks form a cyclic order with equal lengths. The fixed line of the real \(\mathbb Z_3\) representation is excluded by no-privileged-frame, giving

\[
e_u+e_d+e_s=0.
\]

Equal pairwise overlaps then imply

\[
e_u\cdot e_d=e_d\cdot e_s=e_s\cdot e_u=-\frac12.
\]

Flavor is the marking of the three This-aspects. A quark is marked color. The three-ness of flavor is inherited from the three interior aspects under marking, not separately imposed. The mark metric is **Registered, not Sealed**.

## 6. Proton and neutron

The proton and neutron share one color-singlet closure. Their distinction lies in the mark configurations

\[
P=2u+d,\qquad N=u+2d.
\]

The spin gate is common and cancels in the difference. With \(u\cdot d=-1/2\),

\[
|P|^2=|N|^2=3,\qquad P\cdot N=\frac32,
\]

so the mark-access angle has \(\cos\theta=1/2\) and Born-squared access weight \(1/4\). This access overlap is distinct from the normalized isospin ladder matrix element.

The relief-valve mechanism fixes the sign of the splitting. The proton presents an outward valence edge that can be satisfied by an external inverse partner; the neutron bears the corresponding load internally. Mass is internally borne maintenance, so the neutron is heavier. The mechanism and sign are **Registered**.

The quantitative proposal is

\[
f_{np}=\frac{1}{2\pi}+\frac14\left[1-\frac{1}{(2\pi)^2}\right],
\qquad
\frac{m_n-m_p}{m_p}=\frac{f_{np}}{3\pi^4}.
\]

The receipt-complement factor remains **Conjectured**; the complete magnitude therefore remains Conjectured.

Neutron decay is structurally the inverse opening of closure into With plus From. The antineutrino as a From–With receipt remains Conjectured.
