## 0. Standing rule

D4 is the invariance phase: the structure preserved across the passages the lower phases generate. As at D3, the discipline is to force only what D4 reaches from D1–D3 materials, carry the rest as explicit forward-dependencies with their later homes, assert no final group, lock no result.

## 2. What D4 establishes (forced from I1–I6)

**(E1) Invariance is conservation under the flows.** The dynamical branch is self-transforming with a conserved invariant (the branch selection S). Each admissible generator in u(M) (I5) generates a flow; each flow conserves its own generator as the invariant of that flow (I4, generalized). So the generator algebra u(M) *is* the algebra of conserved quantities of the branch: invariance at D4 = conservation under the generated flows. [Forced from I4 + I5. The conservation is the Hamiltonian-side fact "generator = conserved observable"; it does not pass through an action integral — see O1.]

**(E2) Sector-membership is a conserved invariant.** The exchange split (I3) is preserved under the flows: a content's sector-membership — whether it lies in the symmetric or antisymmetric sector — is conserved across self-transformation, because conservation (E1) applies to the exchange structure as to any registered structure. [Forced from E1 + I3.]

**(E3) A conserved abelian charge exists.** The trace (abelian phase) part of u(M) (I5) generates a flow whose conserved quantity (E1) is a scalar charge. Its existence is forced; its *quantization* is not (see O3). [Forced from E1 + I5.]

**(E4) The non-abelian remainder carries conserved quantities of its own.** The trace-free part of u(M) likewise yields conserved quantities under E1. Their algebra is the non-abelian remainder's algebra; its rank and the specific group remain open (dim M unfixed, O2 of the D3 document). [Forced as structure; specific content open.]

## 3. What D4 does NOT establish — forward-dependencies, named

**(O1) The action/Lagrangian/variational structure is not forced at D4.** Conservation at D4 is established Hamiltonian-side (generator = conserved observable, E1) and requires no action integral. The variational apparatus — S = ∫L dt, differentiable trajectory variation, Noether's theorem as a derivation of conservation — presupposes trajectory, integration, and a Lagrangian, none supplied by D1–D4. Noether-style derivation of conservation would additionally be circular here, since it presupposes the action framework. The variational formulation, where it appears, is a later or equivalent presentation, not a D4 forcing. Carried forward; possibly a recognition rather than a derivation. [This is the precise boundary: D4 forces conservation, not the action principle.]

**(O2) Spin-statistics is not reachable at D4.** The symmetry-type of the closure-reading (whether the mediation carrier is built on the symmetric or antisymmetric sector — the open O2 carried from D3) is, in established physics, fixed by the spin-statistics connection: identical-fermion antisymmetry tied to spin, which requires Lorentz invariance, locality, and spacetime dimension. D4 has none of these — it has orientation, flows, conservation, but no metric, locality, or dimension (those are the D5 base stratum). Therefore D4 does **not** fix the exchange sector and does not discharge the D3 O2. What D4 contributes is E2: sector-membership is a conserved invariant — the quantity spin-statistics will later tie to spin. **O2 relocates from D3 to D5 (the spacetime/base stratum), where its premises first become available. It is not discharged at D4 and is not selected at D4.**

**(O3) Charge quantization is not forced at D4.** E3 gives a conserved scalar charge, but discreteness of its values requires the abelian symmetry to be compact, which is a topological fact about the symmetry fixed by the base geometry (D5), not by the bare flow. Charge exists at D4; charge quantization is a D5 forward-dependency.

**(O4) The specific gauge group remains open.** As at D3: the form (abelian ⊕ non-abelian, now seen as conserved-charge structure) is established; the dimension of M and the sector-choice (O2) are unfixed, so the specific group is not determined at D4.
