# P8 Same-Kind Internal Carrier

P8 establishes the carrier required before the alternating closure-form and native SU(3) construction of P9. Its exact scope is: one same-kind carrier, carrier-specific deployment of M6's selected complex extension, primitive scalar closure verdict, fixed verdict scale, Hermitian registration pairing at the inherited M6 grade, and dimension exactly three.

P8 does not derive the complex scalar field from physics, upgrade M6's selected extension into a forced or unrestricted complex continuum, or derive the alternating form, epsilon/determinant, connectedness, irreducibility, SU(3), color-singlet consequences, U(1), or Standard Model representations. Those belong to M6, P9, and later units as specified below.

## 1. Mature status

Status: **Registered carrier theorem, not Sealed, conditional on the explicitly selected M6 complex extension and its rule-calculus closure conditions**.

This condition is substantive. M6 supplies the standard quadratic extension only as a selected construction at its conditional rule-presented grade. P8 may deploy that extension on the internal carrier; it may not report complex scalars as physically forced independently of M6 or omit the selected-calculus condition.

The earlier “redirect ledger only / no carrier theorem yet” state is superseded. The carrier exists at a usable framework grade. Its remaining debts are explicit seal residuals and inherited scalar-grade conditions, not grounds for collapsing the result to Open.

## 2. Gate A — one carrier

The three internal moments are same-kind: they are registered under one shared character and stand within one With-field.

A model with three isomorphic fibers requires a gluing datum. If the datum is registrable, it introduces an additional internal distinction or frame-selector not supplied by the same-kind basis. If it is unregistrable, the fibers are framework-identical and collapse to one carrier. Therefore the minimal same-kind construction is one carrier, not role-sorted slots or independently fibered copies.

Standing: **Registered**.

Seal residual: formally classify every possible registrable gluing datum and show that none escapes the frame-selector objection.

## 3. Gate B — deployment of the selected complex extension

A real three-dimensional carrier is coherent. One-carrier, scalar-verdict, dimension, and the later alternating-form argument do not by themselves force complex scalars. P8 therefore does not manufacture `C` from the internal triad.

The load-bearing scalar provenance is M6. Relative to a selected rule calculus closed under the operations used, M6 constructs the selected quadratic extension `C_C`, conjugation, and the corresponding non-degenerate Hermitian scalar extension. This is the maximum scalar grade P8 may inherit.

P8's additional work is carrier-specific deployment. The This-flattened internal triad is symmetric in standing but non-interchangeable in kind. That registered orientation-content is carried faithfully by the selected complex representation rather than erased by treating the carrier as merely real. Thus P8 selects and preserves M6's available complex orientation-content on the one same-kind carrier.

The cyclic route is corroborative and seal-directed, not the provenance of `C_C`: three equally standing, non-interchangeable moments with no privileged element admit the current cyclic reading; its faithful real realization is a two-dimensional rotation, equivalently a complex line. This supports the complex deployment but does not independently construct the complex field.

Standing:

- M6 complex scalar availability: **Secured conditionally and Registered** for the displayed selected-extension core;
- P8 carrier-specific complex deployment: **Registered, not Sealed**, conditional on that M6 selection and calculus closure.

Primary seal residual: formally establish that the internal orientation-content requires the selected complex deployment rather than a fully adequate real realization, without using the later SU(3) center. Formalizing the cyclic reading is one route to that seal; it is not a prerequisite for the current Registered provenance.

The appearance of `Z3` as the center of SU(3) is downstream recognition only and contributes no P8 warrant.

## 4. Gate C — primitive scalar closure verdict

The carrier must support a verdict on whether the internal triad closes as one. Closure status is prior to any vector or operator produced by a closure: a vector-valued or operator-valued output is successor content whose production presupposes that the closure has already occurred.

The primitive closure verdict is therefore scalar-valued. Vector and operator realizations may represent or consume the verdict downstream, but they do not replace its primitive type.

Standing: **Registered**.

Seal residual: sharpen the dependency claim into a complete exclusion of mixed scalar-plus-operator primitive verdict models.

## 5. Gate D — fixed verdict scale

The scalar verdict carries a magnitude. Once that magnitude registers a distinction, conservation of registered distinctions prevents arbitrary rescaling in subsequent use. P8 therefore supplies a fixed verdict scale rather than a merely projective scalar line.

This does **not** yet supply a fixed alternating 3-form, determinant, `det = 1`, SU(3), or the identification of a residual phase with U(1). Those require the P9 alternating-form construction and P11 gauge-source analysis.

Standing: **Registered** for fixed scalar normalization at the carrier interface; the particular closure-form remains P9.

## 6. Gate E — Hermitian registration pairing

M6 already supplies a non-degenerate Hermitian extension of the M5 pairing, conditional on the same selected closed rule calculus used in Gate B. P8 does not independently derive Hermitian structure from the internal carrier.

P8 deploys that inherited Hermitian core as the carrier-specific registration and coherence-comparison form. The carrier deployment preserves conjugation and real-valued self-comparison. The argument that a complex-bilinear symmetric form would select a preferred real structure is corroborative support for this deployment, not a replacement for the M6 provenance.

Where the amplitude/readout construction is spent, the pairing supports nonnegative self-participation at that construction's exact scope. Neither M6 nor P8 thereby supplies unrestricted positivity, Hilbert completeness, probability semantics, or an unrestricted physical inner-product space.

Standing: **Registered for carrier-specific deployment, conditional on M6's selected extension and calculus closure**. Non-degeneracy and Hermitian algebraic type inherit M6's displayed core; positivity inherits the exact readout scope.

## 7. Gate F — dimension exactly three

Dimension three is grounded upstream of P9.

The interior distinction is the This-flattened image of the primitive triad. It presents three non-interchangeable moments. Under the selected M6 complex deployment of Gate B, each moment is carried on one complex line. Minimal same-kind carriage therefore gives

`V_int = C_C^3`.

When the selected calculus is understood, the familiar shorthand `C^3` may be used for this registered core. It must not be read as the unrestricted classical complex vector space or as scalar structure independently forced by P8.

This route depends on the exactly-three office result, This-flattening, same-kindness, and Gate B's selected complex deployment. It does not depend on the existence of a nonzero alternating 3-form. The later theorem that such a form requires dimension at least three is corroboration, not P8's warrant.

Standing: **Registered, conditional on Gate B's M6 scalar grade**.
