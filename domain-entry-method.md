# Domain Entry Method

This is the general domain-entry method, not merely a physics preface. It states how Triadic Closure moves from the foundational / mathematical object into a successor domain without borrowing that domain's unconscious metaphysics.

## Mature status

Status: **current governing method** for domain entry.

Any domain-specific reconstruction must first clarify its presuppositions architectonically. For physics this includes metric, causation, individuation, possibility, actuality, particle, field, space, time, and force. The point is not to solve the domain's inherited phantom problems on their own terms; it is to dissolve them by rebuilding the presuppositions from the framework.

## Procedure

1. Name the domain's unconscious version of a presupposition and the smuggle it carries.
2. Refuse that version as load-bearing.
3. Rebuild the concept from the framework toolkit: offices, registration, sameness/difference, quantity/quality, J, rho, and the modal triad.
4. Ground the rebuild in a concrete example.
5. Check that the apparent problem dissolves rather than requiring an imported answer.
6. Deploy the clarified concept from then on.

## From-whole and domain constraint

When entering a successor domain, the From is the full prior object, inherited whole. It is not a buffet of optional handles. Every characteristic of the prior object constrains the successor-domain walk.

The successor domain is selected by its own constraint. For physics the constraint is self-transforming / invariant / coherent structure. Read from inside, that same constraint is threefold causation: From-causation, With-causation, and This-causation.
