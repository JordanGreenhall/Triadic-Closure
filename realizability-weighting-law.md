# Realizability Weighting Law

At a realizability fork with candidates `k`, actualization is weighted by

> `P(k) = C(k) / Σⱼ C(j)`

where `C(k)` is the coherence-participation of candidate `k`, measured by the domain's own coherence measure. The universal law supplies normalization; the form of `C` belongs to the domain.

## Quantum instance: R5 and Gleason

In quantum physics the registered carrier supplies a complex Hermitian pairing. The noncontextuality premise required by the Gleason route is grounded in the held/registered distinction:

- orthogonality is registrable distinctness, so a complete orthonormal basis is a complete admissible fork;
- a candidate's participation is the registered relation between the standing and that candidate, while the other basis members are held context and cannot alter that relation;
- the total over a complete fork is the fixed participation of actuality-as-such and is independent of the held decomposition.

Therefore the candidate assignment is a nonnegative frame function with a basis-independent total. In complex dimension at least three, Gleason's theorem gives

> `C(e) = <e, rho e>`,

and for a pure standing

> `C(e) = |h(sigma,e)|^2`.

The dimension-two caveat remains local to this theorem. The full R5 derivation and the access/readout distinction are stated in [Amplitude / Readout Theorem](02-amplitude-readout.md).

## Status

The realizability-weighting law is **Registered**. The R5/noncontextuality grounding is **Registered-candidate**; the exact quadratic Born instance runs through that stated joint rather than assuming basis-independence. It is unconditional with respect to the dissolved `frequency=weight` demand.

## The frequency=weight demand is Dissolved

The former demand to derive that long-run frequencies match the weights is a category error, not an open gate.

A categorical derivation would make actualization non-chancy and destroy the explanandum. A probabilistic derivation would already use the weight-to-frequency relation it was supposed to establish. Both versions presuppose that weight and degree-of-actualization are two different things requiring a bridge. The framework does not make that bifurcation: degree-of-coherence-toward is degree-of-actualization.

Therefore `frequency=weight` is **Dissolved**. It is not a condition on the Born instance, mass, flavor and neutron results, atomic binding or spectrum, chemistry gate-crossing, or decay propensity.

## Remaining work

A non-physics end-to-end instance, naturally the Boltzmann case, remains useful seal-polish. A native typicality theorem and reproducibility theorem may be developed as consistency results, but neither is a condition on the weighting law.

Absolute decay or transition rates still require the local possibility-space and rate structure of the process. That values-side requirement is distinct from the dissolved `frequency=weight` demand.
