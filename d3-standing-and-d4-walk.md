# PART I — What is standing after D3

D3 is the turn-over: D2's relationships become relata, and standing structures are brought into mutually-constituting relationship (the two D3 questions — is there determinate With-content relating them, or Thises composing them into a new unity). The dead-center operation is composition.

## Sealed at D3 (bidirectional content-identity, no remainder, test run explicitly)

- **Composite-This.** A closure of held/coupled constituent Thises into a unity that is itself a self-transformer (its own invariant, generator, coupling-slots). Forward: the object is exactly such a closure. Backward: "composite entity properly understood," stripped of the Cartesian assembly-of-pre-existing-bricks picture (the constituents are co-constituted in the closure, not pre-existing), carries no more. The walk thereby becomes **self-similar**: a composite is a This and re-enters D1/D2/D3 composition at its own level. *Seals.* (The seal is on the general composite; "bound state" = the persistent subcase, deferred to the persistence stage.)
- **Post-P3 mode-multiplicativity claim.** P3 does not supply a tensor/product composite construction. Conditional on that later operation and its domain, this source proposes that the mode-charge (±1) composes as the **product** of independent constituents' modes. Its standing belongs to the owning composite unit and is not upgraded by P3's exchange-mode registration.
- **The Hamiltonian's free + interaction decomposition.** Composition forces the total generator to split into the parts' own generators (free) and their mutual-conditioning (interaction/binding): H = H_free + H_interaction. *The decomposition seals* (the existence and meaning of the split, in essence). The interaction *content* — which couplings, the constants, the vertices — is **not** sealed; it is later articulation.

## Registered at D3 (identification made, not sealed — remainder or deferred content present)

- **Gravity-in-essence** [forced]. The mutual conditioning (With-causation, symmetric co-determination) between the Hamiltonian (energy) and the three spatial-degree structure: energy conditions the spatial co-standing structure and the spatial structure conditions the energy-distribution. Forced — it is exactly the D3 operation on two D2 structures. It is the **first force to stand**, before the gauge forces, because it needs only structures already standing, and it couples **universally to energy** (everything with energy participates) precisely because it *is* the energy-geometry mutual conditioning, not a coupling to a special charge. Registered in essence; the specific gravitational law (the Einstein-equation content, Lorentzian curvature dynamics) is later articulation.
- **Post-P3 force/field/matter proposals.** P3 supplies no force-carrier, field, matter, bulk, impenetrability, spatial-extension, or unlimited-superposition result. The historical assignments in this source require their own physical-role constructions and retain the standing of those owning units; P3's holding theorem cannot be cited as their warrant.
- **Spacetime 3+1 germ** [registered]. Time (oriented metric) and the three space degrees (unoriented metric) compose into one metric structure whose signature is the orientation-difference (one oriented, three unoriented). The full Lorentzian metric is later articulation.
- **Momentum and angular momentum** [registered]. By the symmetry-conservation link: spatial-translation homogeneity → conserved momentum; rotational isotropy → conserved angular momentum. Concrete at D3 as conserved quantities of composites (including **orbital** angular momentum from a composite's spatial arrangement). The **intrinsic** (spin) angular momentum — mode-tied — is *not* here (it is the spin-statistics deployment-signature).
- **Composite conserved totals** [registered]. A composite's conserved quantities are built from its constituents' invariants plus binding (conservation forbids the invariants vanishing in closure).

## Not yet at D3 (named missing dependency and home stage)

- **Quarks** — require confinement (can-only-exist-bound), which requires the specific strong coupling, which requires **internal-charge structure**. Home: the internal-charge stage.
- **Specific gauge forces (weak / strong / electromagnetic)** — require differentiated charge-types and gauge groups, i.e. **internal-charge structure**. Home: the internal-charge stage.
- **Atoms / molecules** — require a specific (electromagnetic) force plus stability. Home: after internal-charge + persistence.
- **Spin (mode-tied)** — requires the spin-statistics bridge (Lorentz / 3+1 deployment). The *orbital* rotation-character is present; the mode-tie is not.

## The pivotal gap

Nearly every "not-yet" — quarks, the gauge forces, atoms — is blocked by the **same single missing dependency: internal-charge structure**. Gravity is the exception that proves the rule: it arrives at D3 *because* it couples to energy (which everything has) rather than to a not-yet-differentiated charge. The most-unlocking next target is therefore internal-charge structure — exactly where the D2 lead pointed (the non-interchangeable mode-pairing three, bb/ff/bf, as the candidate for internal/charge degrees, as opposed to the interchangeable spatial three).

# PART II — The D4 Walk

## Frame (Checklist Part A)

A1 both axes — vertically office-constituted; horizontally the fourth stride, standing on the full D3 standing. A2 modal — what does D3's standing make realizable next, given the accumulated actuals constrain the next actual. A3 inherited — all of D1–D3 + Part 0 as constraint.

**Placement.** The D-series rhythm: D1 the first This; D2 With (relationship of Thises); D3 the turn-over (relationships become composite relata, self-similar recursion begins). D4 is the **invariance phase** — what is preserved across the passages the lower phases generate. Where D3 produced composites and their relationships, D4 asks what is *conserved and invariant* across composition and transformation: the structure that holds steady while the lower phases vary. This is the phase of conserved quantities as a system, symmetry, and the invariants that label kinds.

## Move 1 — dependency position: what should we be looking for at an invariance phase?

Candidates whose dependencies are plausibly satisfied by D4's standing:
- **The closing invariance-algebra** — the conserved quantities (energy, momentum, angular momentum, charge-germs) not as a list but as a *coherent closed system* (they close under their mutual relations). Depends on: the conserved quantities (D3/D2) + ρ-coherence. Plausibly satisfied.
- **Casimir-invariants as kind-labels** — the invariants that label *which kind* a This is (mass-germ from energy-momentum, the mode as a ℤ/2 label). Depends on: the invariant-characteristic + the closing algebra. Plausibly satisfied.
- **Noether-structure in essence** — the link between a symmetry (a direction of no-distinction) and a conserved quantity, as a *systematic* fact rather than case-by-case. Depends on: the symmetry-conservation link (established) + the invariance phase making it systematic.
- **Internal-charge structure** — the pivotal gap from D3. *Does it arrive at D4?* This is the key Move-1 question for D4 (below).
- **CPT-germ** — the discrete invariances (C from the conjugation/paired sector, P from spatial reflection, T from J/orientation-reversal). Depends on: J + sectors + spatial degrees. Plausibly satisfied in germ.

## Move 2 — combination from the standing: what do the actuals compose into at an invariance phase?

**conserved quantities × ρ-coherence → the closing invariance-algebra [registered].** The conserved quantities (energy, momentum, angular momentum) are not independent; brought into mutual relationship under ρ they must *cohere* — and coherence here means they **close** (their mutual relations stay within the system; an unclosed set would generate constraints outside itself, violating coherence). So the conserved quantities form a closed algebra, the closure forced by ρ-coherence, not assumed. (Essence; the specific algebra — its exact relations — is dimension/signature-dependent, later.)

**invariant-characteristic × composition → Casimir kind-labels [registered].** Across all the transformations a This undergoes, its invariant is what is preserved — and the invariant *labels its kind* (same invariant = same species). At D4 this becomes systematic: the conserved invariants are the **labels of irreducible kinds** — the mode (ℤ/2), the mass-germ (from conserved energy-momentum), are Casimir-type invariants that say *what kind of self-transformer* a This is. (Essence; specific values are deployment/selection.)

**symmetry × conservation, made systematic → Noether-in-essence [registered].** D2/D3 used the symmetry-conservation link case by case (spatial translation → momentum, etc.). D4, the invariance phase, makes it a *systematic* structure: every symmetry (every direction of no-distinction in the standing) has a conserved quantity, and these are exactly the closing-algebra's elements. The systematic link is the essence of Noether's theorem — established here Hamiltonian-side (symmetry ↔ conserved generator), *without* the action/variational apparatus (which presupposes trajectory-integration not forced; it is a later or equivalent presentation, possibly recognition).

**modes-pairing-three × the invariance phase → internal-charge structure? [the pivotal test].** The D2/D3 lead: the two sealed modes give three *non-interchangeable* symmetric pairings (bb, ff, bf) — the wrong kind for space (interchangeable) but the candidate for *internal* degrees (non-interchangeable orthogonal qualities, exactly the character of charge/internal dimensions). Does the invariance phase activate these as **conserved internal charges**? Test: an internal charge is a conserved quantity of an *internal* symmetry (a no-distinction direction that is not spatial). The mode-pairing-three are non-spatial qualitative directions; if they carry a symmetry (a direction along which no distinction registers) then by the symmetry-conservation link they carry conserved **internal charges**. The invariance phase is exactly where conserved quantities of symmetries are systematized — so D4 is the natural home for internal-charge structure to *begin*. *Provisional Move-2 result:* the internal-charge structure plausibly *begins* at D4, as the conserved charges of the internal (non-spatial, mode-pairing-derived) symmetries — but whether the specific internal symmetry group (the gauge group) is fixed here is a separate, harder question (it needs the specific structure of the internal directions, likely later). So: **internal-charge in essence (conserved internal charges of internal symmetries) is a candidate D4 arrival; the specific gauge group is not.** This needs the careful per-step treatment, flagged as the live D4 work.

**J × sectors × spatial reflection → CPT-germ [registered].** C (charge-conjugation) from the conjugation/paired sector (0.3); P (parity) from reflection of the spatial degrees; T (time-reversal) from J/orientation-reversal itself. All three are J/sector/orientation-sourced — present in germ at D4. The exact CPT theorem (their combined invariance) needs the Lorentz/locality deployment, later.

## Move 3 — grades and dependency record

**Registered at D4:** the closing invariance-algebra (closure forced by ρ-coherence); Casimir kind-labels (invariants labeling species); Noether-in-essence (systematic symmetry↔conserved-quantity, Hamiltonian-side); CPT-germ; **internal-charge in essence** (conserved charges of internal/mode-pairing symmetries — provisional, the live D4 result to firm up).

**Dependency record updated** — now standing: the closing invariance-algebra, Casimir kind-labels, Noether-in-essence, CPT-germ, and (provisionally) internal-charge-in-essence. **The gap narrows:** the specific internal symmetry group is now the pivotal remaining gate (it unlocks the specific gauge forces, quarks, and atoms).
