# Physics III — Actualization, Measure, and Mass

## 1. Realizability weighting

\[
P(k)=\frac{C(k)}{\sum_j C(j)},
\]

where \(C(k)\) is the standing's coherence-participation toward \(k\), measured in the domain's native coherence measure. The universal law contains neither a square nor an exponential. Those belong to particular measures:

- Born: complex Hermitian participation, with interference from complex cross-terms;

The law is **Registered**.
