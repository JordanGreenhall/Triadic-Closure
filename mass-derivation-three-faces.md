# Particle Mass from the Three Faces of the Closure

The framework's current full statement on how particle mass is determined. Mass is
read off the closure structure of a standing, not posited. A closure is
(From x This x With) with three mutually-constituting faces; mass is that one
closure read through its three pairwise relations. This page assembles the
derivation, grades each stage, and marks the open joints precisely.

**Canary, kept in view throughout.** With the electron bare repeat fixed as the
native mass ruler (`m_e = 1`), the exact With-This color-seating factor is
`6 pi^5 = 1836.1181`. It is one factor within the canonical proton/electron ratio,
not the ratio itself:

> `m_p / m_e = 6 pi^5 [1 + c(3 pi^4)^-2]`, with `3/2 <= c <= 9/4`.

The measured ratio places `c approximately 1.6076`, equivalently
`epsilon_FW approximately 1.8825 x 10^-5`. The bracketed From-With structure,
its positive sign, second-order scale, and coefficient bounds are Registered.
Only the exact internal selection of `c` within the forced bracket remains Open.
The discipline of this page is to derive the structure, record clean negatives,
and refuse to reverse-fit the number.

**Canonical reporting rule.** Whenever the proton/electron mass ratio is stated, report the complete expression `m_p / m_e = 6 pi^5 [1 + c(3 pi^4)^-2]`, with `3/2 <= c <= 9/4`. The standalone `6 pi^5` is the exact With-This color-seating factor only; it must never be reported as the complete mass ratio. The bracketed form, positive sign, second-order scale, and coefficient bounds are Registered. Only the exact internal selection of `c` remains Open; the measured ratio places `c approximately 1.6076`, equivalently `epsilon_FW approximately 1.8825 x 10^-5`.


## The principle

Mass is the maintenance of a closure. To be a physical closure at all (past the
With-gate) is to be re-selected onto its attractor every step; that perpetual
self-re-selection *is* the mass (see [with-to-this-closure](with-to-this-closure.md) and
[mass-as-self-closure](mass-as-self-closure.md)). Maintenance-of-closure and mass are the same object.
There is no closure that additionally "provides" mass — the distinction between a
color closure and a mass-giving color closure is a phantom. [Registered.]

Consequently masses are not free parameters; they are read off *which closures a
standing maintains* and *the measure of each*, in relation to a native ruler. For
mass, that ruler is the electron bare repeat: `m_e = 1`.

## The structure being measured

A closure is (From x This x With), three faces, each of which *is the relationship
of the other two*. So no face is measured alone; the closure is read through three
pairwise relations, and any one face re-enters wherever the other two meet. With
the established flattenings (This -> color, From -> time, With -> space), the three
readings are:

- **With-This** (space ^ color): the interior seated in the exterior arena — the
  *spatial* self-maintenance.
- **From-With** (time ^ space): the advance trapped into recurrence — the
  *temporal* self-maintenance.
- **This-From** (color ^ time): the energy-mass relation, where mass appears as a
  *variable*, not a product.

These are not three masses to sum; they are one closure read three ways.

## With-This: the spatial closure (2 pi^5 -> 6 pi^5)

The most developed component.

**The interior (This-face) is color, gathered.** The triad *taken as This* gathers
its three moments into one interior closure. For a baryon this is the color
closure: three quark colors gathered by the alternating form (the determinant)
into one color singlet, one joint frame in C^3 — one determinant, one closure, not
three. [Registered; the one-closure reading is settled.]

**Its measure is the closure-space measure `2 pi^5`.** See [closure-inherited-metric](closure-inherited-metric.md) for the full construction. The quantity measured is the normalized space of moment-standings, not an abstract operator group. A first unit moment in `C^3` contributes `S^5`; a second unit moment in its orthogonal complement `C^2` contributes `S^3` by restriction of the same pairing; orthonormality plus `det=1` forces the third moment with no remaining freedom. Therefore

> `Vol(closure) = Vol(S^5) x Vol(S^3) x 1 = pi^3 x 2 pi^2 = 2 pi^5`.

The measure is invariant under the closure's `SU(3)` no-distinction action, and its scale is fixed by self-pairing equal to one. The abstract trace metric gives `sqrt(3) pi^5`, but no operator direction is measured in the closure-space construction. The same exclusion appears algebraically in the determinant-balancing direction `lambda_8 = diag(1,1,-2)/sqrt(3)`: after the first two moments stand, `det=1` makes the third phase a forced completion rather than an independent freedom. [Registered.]

**The electron's interior is 1, and the electron bare repeat is the native mass
ruler.** By the identical construction the electron's color is the trivial rep;
its SU(3) orbit is a single point (0-dimensional, verified). No S^5, no
completion — a point, measure 1. Its From-With repeat is the minimal stable
positive mass case: elementary, rest-capable, color-trivial, and minimally
contextual. Therefore `m_e = 1` framework-natively. Both particles carry the full
color sector; in the proton it produces a value (resolved triad, 2 pi^5), in the
electron it produces no additional color value (trivial, 1). [Registered for the
trivial interior and the ruler choice.]

**Spatialization gives the factor 3 additively (-> 6 pi^5).** With-flattening
*separates* the triad into three independent degrees (space), where
This-flattening *gathers* it into one (color) — opposite handlings of the same
triad, which is exactly why color is one closure and space is three. The
With-This seats the one gathered interior into the three independent exterior
degrees. The seating operation is now closed: three full equal bearings of the
one color closure are summed,

> 3 x 2 pi^5 = 6 pi^5.

Two guards against the dead factor-of-three still apply: (i) this 3 is the
exterior *separation*, never the color count; (ii) it applies to the This-face
being seated, not to the whole standing — so the electron, having no nontrivial
color This (trivial color, no value produced), is *absent* from the With-This pair
and receives no spatial factor at all, not by exemption but because there is no
interior there to seat.

**Why the join is addition, not product or distribution.** Three operations were
a priori live: product, distribution, or addition. Product is excluded by the
one-closure result: `(2 pi^5)^3` would require three independent color closures,
but the baryon has exactly one gathered color closure — one determinant, one
singlet, one seated This. There is nothing to multiply. Distribution is excluded
by symmetric full occupation: a distributed conserved source thins across its
arena and profiles from a center; the proton is not a point source merely located
in space, but a filled body whose color closure is borne isotropically through its
spatial arena. Full symmetric occupation means full bearing per independent
spatial degree, not a divided share. With product and distribution excluded, the
only available operation for multiple full bearings of one object is summation.
Isotropy fixes the equal weighting: each of the three With-flattened degrees bears
the same full `2 pi^5`.

Checked that space is not already inside 2 pi^5: that measure is built from C^3
with no spatial coordinate; it counts the triad *gathered*, while the spatial
three is the same triad *separated* — gathering is precisely the operation that
does not hold the moments apart, so the separated three is genuinely unspent in
2 pi^5. No double-count. [2 pi^5: Registered. The factor-3 spatialization and the
additive seating join giving 6 pi^5: **Registered**.]

## From-With: the temporal closure, and the chiral residual

The From-With is the advance trapped into a standing — c (the medium-level
From-With: succession-medium and spread-medium locked at unit ratio) *closed into
recurrence*. The temporal self-maintenance.

As a measure: the **From** contributes the bare *repeat* — selfness-as-recurrence,
"the same again." This is pure, one identity recurring, = 1, and it is exactly
shared: proton repeats, electron repeats, "again" is "again." On the bare-repeat
level the From cancels exactly.

The residual is the second face, not a flaw in the first. The seating
(With-This) gives the exact color-seating factor:

> 6 pi^5 = 1836.1181.

This is not the proton/electron ratio by itself. The canonical ratio is
`m_p / m_e = 6 pi^5 [1 + c(3 pi^4)^-2]`, with `3/2 <= c <= 9/4`.
The measured ratio places `c approximately 1.6076`, equivalently
`epsilon_FW approximately 1.8825 x 10^-5`, with positive sign because the
proton carries exterior coupling the electron lacks.

Two resolutions were tested and excluded:

1. **Closure geometry: drag as boundary-measure.** Computed like-for-like — the
   same exterior operation applied to the proton's resolved This (`2 pi^5`) and
   the electron's trivial This (`1`), then differenced. Every member of the
   boundary-of-`6 pi^5` family lands a full decade high:

   > (2 pi^5 - 1) / (6 pi^5)^2 = 1.81 x 10^-4

   against a target of `1.88 x 10^-5`, a structureless gap of about `9.6`. The
   residual is not in the closure-return / sphere-volume family. Clean negative,
   like-for-like comparison done.

2. **Metrology seam.** Excluded. The measured ratio is a ratio of two
   free-particle rest masses pinned independently to about nine figures: electron
   via bound-electron g-factor in H-like `28Si13+`; proton via Penning-trap
   cyclotron frequency against `12C`. Binding-energy and reduced-mass corrections
   are applied and removed to recover free-particle masses, leaving no residue in
   the ratio. There is no `10^-5` definitional convention between "the measured
   ratio" and "a pure ratio." The door is closed.

What the residual is: the independent physical decomposition matches the two
faces structurally. The proton's mass is about `91%` non-perturbative binding
(the With-This color seating) and about `9%` bare-quark/Higgs (chiral), while the
electron's mass is entirely its Yukawa coupling (the From-With chiral face). So
`6 pi^5` is the color-seating face — the dominant piece by the physical
accounting itself — and the residual is the proton's small admixture of the
From-With chiral face: the same register that constitutes the electron's entire
mass, and the register the framework has not yet rendered numerically.

The residual is therefore physical, located, and consistent with the independent
decomposition — not noise, not convention, and not a defect in the seating.

**Status.** The `6 pi^5` With-This color-seating factor is exact and Registered. The full canonical ratio is `m_p / m_e = 6 pi^5 [1 + c(3 pi^4)^-2]`, with `3/2 <= c <= 9/4`. The bracketed From-With form, sign, second-order scale, and coefficient bounds are Registered; only the exact internal selection of `c` remains Open. The proton/electron residual (`1.882 x 10^-5`) is this face's admixture in
the proton, not a separate quantity. The combination law is multiplicative;
additive assembly is excluded for the full mass relation, though at this scale the
numerical difference is tiny.

## This-From: no contribution

The This-From is the energy-mass relation (E^2 = m^2 + p^2; at rest E = m), the
phase-relation between the time-reading and the space-reading. Mass appears here as
a *variable being related*, not as a product of the relating. So This-From accrues
no factor — it is where mass *shows up as a term*, not where it is *made*.
[Registered, conceptually.]

## Assembly and the ratio

Proton: With-This produces the Registered color-seating face `6 pi^5`; From-With
contributes the chiral-face admixture; This-From carries no factor. Electron:
With-This produces no nontrivial color value; From-With constitutes its entire
mass as the chiral face; This-From carries no factor.

The mass relation is therefore not a simple additive sum of independent pieces.
The correct assembly law is **multiplicative**: the From-With face scales the
standing rather than adding a second lump beside the color seating. In the
proton/electron comparison the observed small surplus is the proton's tiny
From-With chiral admixture over the electron-ruler normalization:

> m_p / m_e = 6 pi^5 [1 + c(3 pi^4)^-2]

with

> epsilon_FW = c(3 pi^4)^-2,    3/2 <= c <= 9/4.

The measured ratio places `c approximately 1.6076`, equivalently `epsilon_FW approximately 1.8825 x 10^-5`.

Numerically this gives the same visible residual formerly written as
`6 pi^5 + residual`, but that additive shorthand is now deprecated because it
mislocates the open object. The open object is not a separable drag quantity. It
is the numbering of the From-With chiral face, which sets the electron's
mass-as-such and the proton's residual together.

## The neutrino

A massless fermion is pure advance — a single chiral moment running at c, never at
rest, its From-With *open* (nothing to trap the advance against). The neutrino sits
near this limit: lacking the second chiral moment (the sterile right-handed
partner that, fully unengaged under every generator, is not individuated and so
does not stand — see the survey-injectivity result in [quark-lepton-split](quark-lepton-split.md)), it
has nothing to close its From-With against; its advance cannot trap into a rest
standing. So it stays near pure From — near c — and its This-From rest-content is
near zero. The neutrino is light not by a small mass but by a near-absence of the
temporal closure itself. [The missing-partner result: Registered. The From-With
reading of it: **Conjectured**, directional, not canonical.]
