# Cosmological Two-Mode Budget Reading

## Mature status

Status: **Interpretation-grade; not a derivation, prediction, or independent confirmation.**

The present cosmological budget is dominated by two entries, conventionally written `Ω_m` and `Ω_Λ`. The framework permits these to be read as the two cosmological conditioning modes:

- `Ω_m`: the transitive budget entry, carried by matter and radiation through `T_{μν}`;
- `Ω_Λ`: the reflexive budget entry, carried by the metric-proportional self-holding term `Λg_{μν}`.

At the geometric-response level, this is the same two-part division represented by the Einstein term and the cosmological metric term permitted by the Lovelock form.

## Level distinction

The reading is by **cosmological role**, not by constituent essence.

A localized mass is itself a reflexive self-closure. Once localized masses are assembled into cosmological matter, however, their background-budget role is borne through `T_{μν}` as transported and mutually conditioned content. They therefore appear in the transitive budget entry. `Λ` is reflexive at the scale of the cosmological whole: the uniform residual self-holding not localized into the contents already counted in `T_{μν}`.

This level distinction prevents double-counting and avoids the false claim that matter is intrinsically non-reflexive.

## What the reading does and does not say

The reading supplies a coherent interpretation of why the observed budget is naturally organized around a matter entry and a cosmological-self-holding entry. It does **not** derive:

- the observed values of `Ω_m` or `Ω_Λ`;
- their present ratio or the coincidence of their present magnitudes;
- the historical evolution of either entry;
- framework-native Friedmann dynamics or `w(z)`;
- cosmological two-mode exhaustiveness.

Radiation and other transported source components belong to the transitive family even when separately itemized in the empirical ledger. Exact background flatness remains a separate Registered-candidate result conditional on cosmological two-mode exhaustiveness and G1; this interpretation does not strengthen that premise.

## Use rule

Use this page only to interpret the empirical budget after the structural results have been established. Do not cite the observed `Ω_m`/`Ω_Λ` partition as proof that the two conditioning modes are exhaustive, and do not convert the interpretation into a numerical prediction.
