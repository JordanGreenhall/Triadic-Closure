# P7 Manifold Recovery and Local Continuum

P7 is the spatial-carrier recovery unit defined by the normalization program. It asks how the discrete physics network supports the local continuum and manifold structure spent by later spatial, kinematic, and gravity results. It is not D7 successor passability and does not own the physics-to-chemistry handoff.

## 1. Unit boundary

P7 includes:

- graph/spatial dimension;
- criticality or gaplessness sufficient for a continuum limit;
- global frame-freedom;
- smoothness and rotational invariance of the recovered spatial sector;
- exact local Lorentz at the recovered local continuum;
- the scope of the G1 debt.

P7 excludes gauge structure, internal carriers, SU(3), matter taxonomy, mass, gravity dynamics, and successor-domain handoff except where they consume the recovered spatial carrier.

## 3. The G1 narrowing

The manifold debt is **spatial-sector only**.

The internal sector is vertically constituted and does not require recovery from a spatial graph. Consequently:

- the same-kind internal carrier;
- the Hermitian pairing used in the internal readout;
- the amplitude/Born construction;
- the internal mass-measure spine;

owe G1 nothing merely because they are expressed mathematically over continuous carriers.

A downstream claim inherits G1 only when it actually spends spatial distance, surround, direction-continuum, rotational smoothness, momentum-as-continuum direction, or manifold structure. Do not attach G1 globally to all later physics.

## 4. Relation to P4 and P5

P4 establishes exact `3+1` only as one oriented From direction plus three With-spatial degrees. P5 establishes finite link/step propagation, `c = 1`, the direct-bound null relation, and the conditional Lorentz theorem on a given carrier.

P7 supplies the recovered spatial carrier on which P5's mature local-continuum and local-Lorentz readings may be spent. P7 does not re-prove P4 dimensionality or P5 propagation.

## 5. What remains open

- A fully native seal of spatial smoothness without the named universality import.
- The precise selected rule calculus and closure properties for every analytic continuum operation spent downstream.
- Global manifold topology and global Lorentz structure beyond the local result.
- Quantitative crossover scales between the discrete substrate and the recovered continuum.

Exact local Lorentz is not listed as an open task: its defended-posit ceiling is the strongest available warrant for exactness from the discrete base.

## 6. P7 disposition

P7 **passes as a split manifold-recovery unit**. Graph dimension, criticality, and frame-freedom are Registered. Smoothness and rotational invariance are Registered, not Sealed, through the named universality import. Exact local Lorentz carries the defended-posit exactness ceiling and is Sealed on a given recovered manifold through P5 plus Alexandrov–Zeeman.

## 7. Use rule

Apply G1 only to spatial-carrier expenditures. Do not use P7 as a global caveat on internal structure. Do not use D7 successor passability or the physics-to-chemistry gate as P7 doctrine; those belong to the generic D-series and the later X1 interface unit respectively.
