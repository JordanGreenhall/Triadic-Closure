# With-to-This Closure as Realizability Collapse

How a previously-standing With (a holding among constituents) is closed over by a new This. The result answers, in framework-native terms, why such a closure is forced rather than merely available, why it is category-level, and why it occurs only at the closure-form's arity. The mechanism is a Born-type collapse of the mutual-conditioning realizability field onto its unique attractor. Worked and checked on the baryon (three-quark color) case.

## The question

A standing With becomes a new This at the baryon: three quarks, mutually conditioning through color, close into a color-neutral standing that is then a relatum for the next stratum.

The sharp question is how this is possible at all. In mathematics a holding closes by evaluation: given three vectors and the alternating form, the determinant is the value of the form on them. In physics the same closure-form is present, but the three color-moments are dynamic and re-selected each step. The closure therefore cannot be a one-time evaluation; it must be produced through the realizability-to-actuality structure.

## The mechanism

The field of mutual conditionings among the constituents is a realizability field: the coherent constraint that standing actuals place on the next actual. Each step this field must produce a next actual. Selection is weighted by coherence-participation, the realizability-weighting law.

A configuration on which the closure-form is non-zero is a complete closure, and a complete closure is maximally self-participating: it re-selects itself as a coherence fixed point. The realizability weighting therefore tilts selection toward the complete-closure fork; once there, the closure self-reselects and persists. **The new This is the Born-attractor of the With-field.**

### Evaluation versus collapse

The mathematical closure is massless because it is read once; the physical closure is massive because it is re-collapsed every step, and persistent re-collapse is self-closure. Same closure-form, two modes: evaluate (static, massless) versus flow-to-fixed-point (dynamic, massive). Mass is the maintenance.

### Why a whole category

The closure-condition is category-level, not element-level. The field does not collapse onto three specific colored moments; it collapses onto the gauge-equivalence class of triad-spanning configurations. What actualizes is being-a-complete-closure as such, which is why the baryon is a kind and a new relatum-type for the next stratum.

### Why at a certain level

The closure-form has an arity, and the closure fork first exists only when the field can span it. Below triality-zero there is no complete-closure fork and no standing can actualize. At triality-zero the fork appears, the field flows to it, and the This forms. Confinement and baryon formation are one event.

## Computation

Using the pairwise color-overlap operator `H = Σ_{i<j} T_i·T_j` on `C³ ⊗ C³ ⊗ C³`, the spectrum has eigenvalues `−2` (singlet), `−1/2` (octets), and `+1` (decuplet). Under both binding-coherence and conditioning-degree flows, tested random starts flow to the singlet. Perturbations return to the singlet while octet and decuplet perturbations drain to it. The singlet is therefore the unique tested attractor and the only closed fixed point.

- The With-to-This closure is a Born-type collapse of the mutual-conditioning realizability field onto its unique tested attractor.
- That attractor is the complete-closure singlet.
- The closure is forced under the tested dynamics, category-level, and first available at the closure-form's arity.

## Seating corollary

When one completed This is borne through three registrably distinct With-separated holdings, each holding bears the whole closure. Coherence-participation is additive over registrably distinct holdings, so a closure of measure `mu` contributes

> `mu + mu + mu = 3 mu`.

Treating the three holdings as one joint bearing would make re-selection blind to registered structure and violate additivity. The factor three is therefore a corollary of whole-closure maintenance plus additivity, not the survivor of an inventory of imagined joining operations. For the color closure, `mu = 2 pi^5`, giving the exact With-This factor `6 pi^5` in [Particle Mass from the Three Faces of the Closure](mass-derivation-three-faces.md).

## Boundary

This result fixes which configuration closes and that the closure is forced. It does not determine the magnitude of the resulting closure's self-bearing. That measure belongs to [Particle Mass from the Three Faces of the Closure](mass-derivation-three-faces.md).

This page alone must not be read as computing the proton/electron mass ratio. The complete canonical ratio is

`m_p / m_e = 6 pi^5 [1 + c(3 pi^4)^-2]`, with `3/2 <= c <= 9/4`.

The standalone `6 pi^5` is the exact With-This color-seating factor within the ratio, not the full ratio itself.
