# With-to-This Closure as Realizability Collapse

How a previously-standing With is closed over by a new This. The result addresses, within one tested baryon model, why a complete-closure configuration is selected rather than merely available, why the result is category-level, and why the modeled closure first appears at the closure-form's arity.

Walk order is not epistemic authority. Although this mechanism is articulated after the generic P6 phase-form, its exhibited invariant and return dynamics are directly applicable to P6. It supplies a scoped, conditional physical persistence and stability witness.

## The question

A standing With becomes a new This in the modeled baryon case: three quark-color factors, mutually conditioning through the selected color interaction, close into a color-neutral standing that may then serve as a relatum for later structure.

The sharp question is how this is possible at all. In mathematics a holding closes by evaluation: given three vectors and the alternating form, the determinant is the value of the form on them. In physics the same closure-form is proposed, but the three color-moments are dynamic and re-selected each step. The closure therefore cannot be a one-time evaluation; it must be produced through the realizability-to-actuality structure.

## The mechanism

The field of mutual conditionings among the constituents is treated as a realizability field: the coherent constraint that standing actuals place on the next actual. Each modeled step produces a next state under the selected flow. The proposed selection interpretation uses coherence-participation weighting and therefore inherits every condition and debt of [Realizability Weighting Law](realizability-weighting-law.md), including the Open frequency bridge.

A configuration on which the closure-form is non-zero is a complete closure. Under the tested flows, the complete-closure singlet is maximally self-participating and re-selects itself as a fixed point; perturbations in the tested state space return to it. Within that model, **the new This is the tested attractor of the With-field**.

Because the invariant and perturbation/return class are explicit, this is not merely a candidate for P6. It is a scoped P6 instance:

- persistence: the complete-closure/singlet invariant is preserved under the declared flow scope;
- stability: tested perturbations return to the singlet class;
- bound-state registration: licensed for that persistent-composite core.

A fixed point would not suffice without the specified return dynamics. Here the return dynamics is actually reported, so it must not be discounted merely because the result appears later in the written walk.

### Evaluation versus collapse

The mathematical closure is read by evaluation. The physical model repeatedly re-selects the closure configuration. The proposed mass interpretation identifies the maintenance burden of such repeated self-closure with mass. That interpretation belongs to [Mass as Self-Closure](mass-as-self-closure.md); this page supplies no absolute scale and no basic stable-ruler result.

### Why a whole category

The closure-condition is category-level rather than tied to three named color vectors. The modeled field selects the gauge-equivalence class of triad-spanning singlet configurations. What actualizes in the model is being-a-complete-closure as such, which supports treating the baryon as a relatum-type.

This does not establish every mature property of baryons or prove that the category persists under every physically relevant passage.

### Why at a certain level

The closure-form has an arity, and the complete-closure fork first appears when the field can span it. Below triality zero there is no singlet closure fork in the selected representation. At triality zero the fork appears and the tested flows reach it.

The stronger equation “confinement and baryon formation are one event” is licensed only for the modeled closure/non-closure mechanism. Universal QCD confinement dynamics, running, flux tubes, hadron spectrum, and every higher-body alternative remain with their owning units.

## Computation

Using the pairwise color-overlap operator `H = Σ_{i<j} T_i·T_j` on `C³ ⊗ C³ ⊗ C³`, the spectrum has eigenvalues `−2` (singlet), `−1/2` (octets), and `+1` (decuplet). Under the two representative binding-coherence and conditioning-degree flows reported in the source work, tested random starts flow to the singlet. Perturbations within those tested flows return to the singlet while octet and decuplet components drain toward it.

This supports the singlet as the unique tested attractor for the modeled operator and flows. It is finite/model-specific evidence, not a theorem over every admissible realizability dynamics.
