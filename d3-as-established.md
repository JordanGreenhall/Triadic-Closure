# D3, As Established

### What the walk has at D3 — stated to the boundary of what is proven, no further. Nothing here is locked: the theory is not adjudicated until D7. This document records what D3 holds cleanly and names, as open forward-dependencies, exactly what it does not.

---

## 0. Standing rule for this document

D3 is an intermediate stage. It establishes some structure and leaves other structure to later stages. The discipline here: state what is forced at D3 from D1/D2 materials; carry what is not yet forced as an explicit forward-dependency with its likely later home; assert no final group, lock no result. The status of color is not settled at D3 and this document does not settle it.

## 1. Inherited materials (from D1/D2, taken as established)

- **(I1)** Complex linear physical state-space; superposition available. [D2 continuous flow requires it.]
- **(I2)** Nondegenerate positive-definite Hermitian pairing h. [D1 registration + probability-readability.]
- **(I3)** Constitutive orientation/involution J, J² = id, with exchange split into eigensectors λ = +1 (symmetric) and λ = −1 (antisymmetric), both inhabited. [D1 bifurcation.]
- **(I4)** Interaction-events exist as physical actuals; unitary reversibility constrains transformations. [D2.]
- **(I5)** D3 demand: interaction-events compose into higher-order closures of the same physical kind.

## 2. What D3 establishes (forced from I1–I5)

**(E1) A horizontal mediation carrier exists and is distinct from the vertical offices.** D3 same-kind composition (I5) requires a carrier M of mediation alternatives by which interaction-events compose. M is horizontal; From/With/This are the fixed vertical forcing functions that constrain M, not directions within it. No transformation mixes the offices. [This is the structural separation that prevents the category error of acting on the offices.]

**(E2) M inherits the D1/D2 structure.** M carries: complex linearity (I1), the Hermitian pairing h (I2), unitary-reversibility constraint (I4), and the exchange split (I3). Hence admissible frame transformations of M preserve h: before any D3-specific closure constraint, Aut(M, h) ⊆ U(M). [Forced from I1–I4.]

**(E3) D3 requires a closure-reading of complete mediation frames.** Same-kind composition (I5) requires a structure that registers when a complete frame of mediation contributions composes to one same-kind closure. That a closure-reading exists is forced by I5. Its specific form — value-type, arity, symmetry — is **not** fixed at D3 (see §3).

**(E4) The non-abelian/abelian split of the generator algebra.** The generators of admissible transformations lie in u(M); u(M) splits canonically as the trace part (an abelian phase, identifiable with the D2 overall-flow generator) plus the trace-free part (the non-abelian remainder). The *form* "abelian phase ⊕ non-abelian remainder" is forced; the rank and dimension of the non-abelian remainder depend on dim M, which is not fixed at D3 (see §3). [Forced as form; specific group open.]

## 3. What D3 does NOT establish — forward-dependencies, named

These are not failures; they are structure not yet reachable with D1–D3 materials. Each is carried forward with its likely later home.

**(O1) The dimension of M.** dim_ℂ M is not forced at D3. It would be forced by the form and arity of the closure-reading (E3), and those are not fixed at D3. In particular, the closure-reading's *symmetry type* — whether it is symmetric or antisymmetric under exchange of two contributions — is undetermined at D3, because both exchange sectors (I3) are inhabited and nothing at D3 selects which sector the mediation carrier is built on.

**(O2) The closure-form's symmetry, and its dependence on statistics.** Whether the closure-reading is alternating (→ would force dim M ≥ 3 and, with exact preservation, SU(3)) or symmetric (→ permits lower dimension and a different group) depends on the exchange-statistics of the composed events. In established physics the analogous fact — color's totally-antisymmetric singlet — is fixed by the identical-fermion antisymmetry of the composed quarks, which is fixed by the spin-statistics connection, which is a spacetime-and-locality result. The framework's analogue is therefore expected to be fixed not at D3 but at the stratum where orientation/locality/dimension are available (D4/D5). **D3 carries this as a forward-dependency on the spin-statistics structure, not as a selection to be made now.**

**(O3) The specific gauge group.** Consequently the specific non-abelian group of E4 is open at D3. It is fixed only once O1/O2 are discharged downstream. D3 asserts the *form* (abelian ⊕ non-abelian) and no more.

## 4. The clean D3 statement

At D3, established: a horizontal mediation carrier M exists, distinct from the vertical offices (E1); it is complex, carries a positive-definite Hermitian pairing, and is unitary-constrained (E2); a closure-reading of complete mediation frames exists (E3); and the generator algebra splits as abelian-phase ⊕ non-abelian-remainder (E4).

Open, carried forward: the dimension of M (O1); the symmetry type of the closure-reading, expected to be fixed by spin-statistics at the spacetime stratum (O2); and therefore the specific gauge group (O3).

No group is asserted at D3. No result is locked. The status of color awaits the discharge of O1–O3 at later stages, and the theory as a whole is not adjudicated until D7.

## 5. Adversarial pass (run before submission)

*The author's own hostile read, recorded so the reviewer can check it rather than re-derive it.*

- **"E1 smuggles the carrier."** Does a mediation carrier distinct from the offices actually follow from I5, or is it posited? — I5 demands same-kind composition of interaction-events; composition requires something composed-through; that something is not the offices (the offices are constitution, not the events being composed). The carrier's *existence* follows; its *structure* is only what E2 inherits. No dimension or group is claimed for it here. Survives, provided the reviewer grants that "composition requires a carrier" is not itself a selection — it is the content of I5.
- **"E4's split is the old U(3)→SU(3) move in hiding."** No: E4 claims only u(M) = trace ⊕ trace-free, which is true for any u(n) and asserts no n. It does not extract SU(anything); it names the form. The det=1 step is explicitly NOT taken here (it would require the alternating closure-form, which is O2, open). Survives.
- **"O2 is a dodge — you couldn't force the sector so you deferred it."** The deferral is checked against external structure: in established physics the antisymmetric color singlet is genuinely fixed by fermion statistics, not at the color-group stage but by spin-statistics (spacetime). So deferring the sector-choice to the spacetime stratum is the *correct* dependency order, matching how the result is actually fixed, not an evasion. The honest risk: if the framework cannot derive spin-statistics at D4/D5, O2 becomes a genuine selection (or a lock to actual). D3 does not prejudge this; it names the dependency. Survives as a named open, not as a closed claim.
- **"You're claiming less than before — is this retreat?"** It is not a retreat to a weaker claim about the same object; it is the correct claim about what D3 alone reaches. The earlier error was concluding a *negative* lock ("SU(3) not forced, period") from a *local* inability ("not forced at D3 from D3 materials"). This document makes neither the positive nor the negative lock. That is the scrub.

**Adversarial verdict:** the document asserts only E1–E4 and carries O1–O3 as named opens. It locks nothing. The one place a reviewer can still press is E1 (carrier existence from I5) and E3 (closure-reading existence from I5); both are claimed as content of the I5 demand, not as selections.
