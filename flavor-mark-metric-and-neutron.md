# Flavor, the Mark-Metric, and the Neutron Mass Constraint

> **Supporting-source notice.** P14 governs flavor and mark geometry, P20 governs the proton/neutron relation, and P21 governs inverse closure and decay. This page retains detailed derivation and proposal material but is not an alternative canonical owner. Its claim table and local P21 subsection state the surviving grades.

## 0. Scope

This page deposits three linked results worked since the neutron consideration:

1. a framework-native **derivation of flavor** (why there are three flavors, which three,
   and where they live), replacing the imported flavor-SU(3) reading;
2. the **mark-metric** `u·d = −1/2`, forced by the no-privileged-frame argument on the
   three marks — the keystone the earlier draft could only posit; and
3. a candidate route by which the mark-metric may constrain the neutron mass, using equal
   proton/neutron spin contribution only as a selected comparison premise: the
   aggregate configurations have normalized overlap `1/2` and squared overlap `1/4`,
   while P20 keeps the physical access-weight interpretation Conjectured.

It supersedes the A₂/120° keystone of the prior neutron draft. The electron remains the
native mass ruler; all masses are ratios to it. No Standard-Model structure is imported as
ground; SM language is the downstream dialect used only to check against measurement.

The bracketed form, positive sign, second-order scale, and coefficient bounds are Registered. Only the exact internal selection of `c` remains Open; the measured ratio places `c approximately 1.6076`, equivalently `epsilon_FW approximately 1.8825 x 10^-5`.


## 1. The derivation of flavor (color → marks)

This recapitulates the walk exactly, adding and subtracting nothing.

**1.1 The J polarity — the two modes.** This paragraph is governed by [P3](boson-fermion-and-holding-statistics.md). The triad is taken up into the recursion; at the
boundary the bare distinction is two-valued: +/−, symmetric/antisymmetric. Once "particle"
is refused as a primitive, this polarity-fork is the two coherent modes of the minimal
This's intrinsic holding-capacity: **boson = the symmetric-mode self-transformer; fermion =
the antisymmetric-mode self-transformer.** This is the marked (vertical, J-level)
distinction; it survives the stripping of mass, charge, color, and spacetime. At this point
there are fermion and boson in essence and nothing else — no kinds, no quarks, no leptons.
**[Registered–Sealed for the inherited exchange-mode core; a later carrier's sector-purity and role are not supplied here.]**

**1.2 The flattened triad — common to both modes.** Beneath the grading sits the primitive
triad, taken up the recursion, read in its three flattenings by office-signature:

- **From-image → ordering (time), asymmetric;**
- **With-image → quantity (space), interchangeable;**
- **This-image → quality (color), non-interchangeable.**

Every closure bears the flattened triad regardless of grading-mode. **[Registered — the
office-signature reading of the three flattenings.]**

**1.3 Color as a bare requirement.** The This-image is the requirement of qualitative
distinction: three non-interchangeable aspects. The requirement is **bare** — it demands
that the three differ and supplies no means of engaging that difference. Non-interchangeability
says "these must be distinct"; it does not say how they are told apart.

**1.4 Marking engages the requirement.** To engage the bare-but-required distinction the
three aspects must be **marked**, and the marks must differ — that is the whole content of the
requirement. Three distinct marks: **u, d, s**. Now the bare distinction can be engaged:
color-sub-u, color-sub-d, color-sub-s. The passage from color to flavor is exactly this
marking. **There are three marks because there are three aspects to engage** — the
three-foldness of flavor is the same three, now marked, not a separately-forced count.
**[Registered — flavor's three-ness is color's three-ness under marking; this is the result
that registers flavor, which was previously parked empirical in the particle-identity ledger.]**

**1.5 The quark.** A quark is a *this* on the basis of this qualitative distinction: it stands
as **marked color** — color bearing one determinate mark. It is one color, one mark; it does
not hold the triad, it holds a determinate value in it. **[Registered.]**

**1.6 Closure to color neutrality.** The marked color-standings close. The closure is color's
own doing: the three non-interchangeable aspects gather into the singlet — color in balance,
color in equilibrium. The closure achieves **color neutrality**: not the absence of color, but
color brought to balance in closure. (Trivial color — none taken — gives the **lepton**; the
full triadic gathering gives the **baryon**.) **[Registered — consistent with the registered
color singlet, Items 3–4, 9.]**

**1.7 The marks remain.** The marks were applied to engage color's distinction. Closure brings
color to equilibrium and says nothing about the marks. So the marks are simply **there** —
present, standing on the closed baryon, having nothing to do but be there. Closure balanced
color; it did not touch flavor. Flavor needs no survival story: color did not die, it came to
balance, and the marks neither served the equilibrium nor were consumed by it. They remain.

**Standing.** The baryon has achieved color neutrality in closure — color in balance — and it
maintains, simply by their being-there, one of the combinations of flavor markings: **uud,
udd, and the rest**. The marks are present on the closed, color-balanced baryon as its
determinate qualitative configuration, distinguishing this closure from that one.

> **Consequence carried forward.** P14 positively distinguishes the registered proton and
> neutron roles by their marks — the With/flavor face — on a shared color-neutral closure.
> This does not establish that marks exhaust every physical difference between the roles.

## 2. The mark-metric: `u·d = −1/2` is forced

The marks are determinate values in the color carrier (Item 4), so they carry that carrier's
registered **Hermitian pairing** — the metric is inherited, not newly imported. The question is
the value of the pairing between distinct marks. It is forced.

**Inputs, all native:** three marks (§1.4); they engage a distinction that is
**non-interchangeable** (oriented) with **no privileged element** (no aspect is special).

**2.1 ℤ₃, equal magnitude.** Non-interchangeable + no-privileged-element on exactly three = a
cyclic order with no distinguished member = **ℤ₃** acting on the marks, `e_u → e_d → e_s → e_u`.
A cyclic symmetry of order 3 preserves length, so the three marks are **equal-magnitude** (no
separate assumption — the symmetry carries it).

**2.2 No fixed line ⇒ sum zero.** A real ℤ₃ representation splits as trivial (a fixed axis)
⊕ a 2-dimensional rotation. The fixed axis is the direction of `e_u + e_d + e_s` (it is the
unique ℤ₃-invariant direction). A globally fixed internal line is a privileged,
separately-addressable internal frame-element — exactly what **no-privileged-frame** forbids
(the same move that forced irreducibility in Item 3). So the trivial part is absent:

```
e_u + e_d + e_s = 0
```

**2.3 The value.** With equal magnitude (normalize `|e|² = 1`), pairwise-equal overlaps (by
ℤ₃ symmetry, call it `p`), and sum zero:

```
|e_u + e_d + e_s|² = 3 + 2(3p) = 3 + 6p = 0   ⇒   p = −1/2
⇒   u·d = u·s = d·s = −1/2
```

Equivalently: three equal vectors summing to zero with equal pairwise angles are coplanar at
120°. **The 120° is forced by non-interchangeability + no-privileged-frame on the three marks
— it is not imported from SU(3).** The coincidence with the SU(3) flavor-weight triangle is
**recognition, not input**: established physics was pointing at this object; the object is the
marks' own no-privilege configuration. **[Registered, not Sealed — same standing as the Item 4
no-privileged-frame move it reuses; inherits the grade of the §1 walk and of Item 4's pairing.]**

**Falsification surface.** If "non-interchangeable" admitted a non-cyclic structure on three,
or if a fixed internal line were permitted, `u·d ≠ −1/2`. The result is exactly as strong as
no-privileged-frame, and no stronger.

## 3. The neutron structure

**3.1 Three faces, cleanly separated.** Both nucleons are a single baryonic closure — three
relata gathered into one color singlet.

- **Interior / color (This-face).** *Identical* for proton and neutron — the same singlet.
  **Blind** to the difference. Interior capacity is the closure-inherited measure `2π⁵`,
  spatialized across three isotropic degrees to `6π⁵` (the exact With-This factor, not the complete proton/electron ratio). **[Registered.]**
- **Spin (selected comparison premise).** The displayed mixed-mark model holds proton and
  neutron spin contribution equal in order to compare the mark term. P12 supplies spinorial
  representation structure but no particle-species, spin-statistics, or nucleon assignment;
  the Pauli/spin-`SU(2)` nucleon construction used by the older derivation is external or
  unconstructed at current native scope. Equal contribution is therefore selected and
  model-conditional, not Secured or Registered native nucleon content.
- **Flavor / marks (With-face).** On the shared color closure, P14 positively distinguishes
  the registered roles by **mark-configuration** (§1, §2): `P = uud`, `N = udd`.
  **[Registered at P14's differentiation scope; not an exhaustive physical-difference claim.]**

**3.2 Candidate sign mechanism.** Both configurations are realizable standings. P15 supplies
the proton's satisfiable outward valence and the neutron's lack of net outward valence; P17
supplies a qualitative maintenance-load order. This page proposed that satisfiable outward
valence relieves part of the proton's intrinsic maintenance while the neutron bears the
corresponding load internally. If that additional bridge holds, the proton is the lower-load
standing and `m_n - m_p > 0`.

P15 and P17 do not construct that bridge. The existence or possible occupancy of an electron
counterpart does not by itself show an occupancy-independent reduction in the isolated proton's
intrinsic maintenance. The relief mechanism and framework-derived positive sign are therefore
**Conjectured under P20**. The observed sign corroborates the proposal but does not warrant it.
The wrong-sign charge-as-interior-cost route remains negative evidence only.

**3.3 P21-governed decay proposal: inverse closure (This → With + From).** This subsection
retains supporting source material; it is not a current P20 result and its former Registered
labels do not govern. [P21](with-to-this-closure.md) Registers the generic decay classifier but
grades the office-form inverse and candidate `d → u` passage Conjectured. A forward With closes
into a This; the proposal reads neutron decay as partial reopening into a With (successor
standings in external relation) plus released From. No inverse map or office-complete output
theorem is yet constructed. The older one-directional drain, zero return amplitude, and
monotonic accumulation-to-certainty claims are Open. The proposed passage would settle two
ledgers at once:

- **With / charge ledger →** the **electron** as a proposed output. P21 grades electron
  production Open internally and empirical observationally; inverse-equal role recognition does
  not force production.
- **From-With / chiral receipt ledger →** the **antineutrino** (near-pure open From-With, the
  receipt and almost nothing else — the framework's missing-partner object). **[Conjectured —
  the antineutrino identification is the corpus's Conjectured From-With reading.]**
- **Surplus → pure From: kinetic energy**, Conjectured as a structural reading and empirical
  quantitatively.

Energy balances empirically in the dialect: `m_n = m_p + m_e + m_ν + KE` (939.565 = 938.272 +
0.511 + 0 + 0.782 MeV). The structural conservation condition is inherited; the numerical
ledger is observation, not a derivation of products or partition.

## 4. The mass constraint: how flavor gives `1/4`

**4.1 The foundational measures.** The electron has trivial color interior (the point orbit) —
the bare exterior turn, volume `2π`. The proton is that bare turn carrying the full interior
closure: `2π × 6π⁵ = 12π⁶`. So `m_p/m_e = 6π⁵[1 + c(3π⁴)⁻²]`, with `3/2 ≤ c ≤ 9/4`, and `m_e = 1` is `2π/2π`. The interior-only
normalization is `3π⁴ = 6π⁵/2π`. The splitting parameter is `f`, with
`(m_n − m_p)/m_p = f / (3π⁴)`. **[Registered, modulo the single 1.9×10⁻⁵ proton residual that
`6π⁵` already carries.]**

**4.2 The squared mark overlap is `1/4`; physical access remains a conjecture.** With the
registered mark-metric `u·d = -1/2`:

```
P = 2u + d,   N = u + 2d
|P|² = |N|² = 3,   P·N = 3/2
cos θ = (3/2)/3 = 1/2   →   θ = π/3
squared normalized mark overlap = cos²(π/3) = 1/4
```

The norms, inner product, normalized overlap, and square are Sealed mathematics given the P14
mark geometry. The further identification of this inner product with a physical access
amplitude is not supplied by the algebra. It still requires an amplitude-space location,
licensed access pairing, normalized frame of alternatives, and a map from aggregate mark
overlap to transition reach. **The `1/4` is Conjectured as a physical access weight.**

**4.3 Reconciliation with the isospin ladder (access ≠ readout).** The isospin lowering
coefficient `⟨n|I₋|p⟩ = 1` — equivalently the superallowed Fermi matrix element `|M_F|² = 1`
— is external mature physics. The proposal reads it as normalization in an orthonormal
doublet/readout space and treats the mark overlap as a distinct candidate access object. This
distinction prevents the two numbers from being identified, but it does not construct the
missing physical access space or weighting map. Readout `1` remains comparison; physical
access `1/4` remains Conjectured.
The third mark (`s`) fixes the u–d angle even though neither nucleon contains it, because the
three marks are one cyclic geometry (§2) — justified, not smuggled.

**4.4 Candidate splitting formula.** The selected ledger partitions in `f`-units:

```
f_total = f_electron + f_Q
f_electron = 1/(2π)                          # electron's bare standing, paid once, linearly
f_Q        = (1/4) · [1 − 1/(2π)²]           # Born access weight × (advance = complement of
                                             #   the reserved two-standing receipt channel)
f_total    = 1/(2π) + (1/4)·[1 − 1/(2π)²]
```

Numerically: `f_total = 0.402822`, `f_total/(3π⁴) = 1.37846×10⁻³` against measured
`(m_n−m_p)/m_p = 1.37843×10⁻³` — agreement at **~2×10⁻⁵**, with no fitted continuous constant
(every term is a named object built from `2π` and the mark-metric).

**Honest leverage note.** The candidate leading structure `1/(2π) + 1/4` lands within
**~1.6%**. The further factor `[1 - 1/(2π)^2]` carries the last refinement and reuses `2π`, but
the physical `1/4`, linear addition, one-time electron payment, and complement partition all
remain unforced selections. The complete magnitude is **Conjectured**; numerical agreement is
an empirical check rather than framework warrant.
