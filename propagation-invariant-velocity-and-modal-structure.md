# Propagation, the Invariant Velocity, and the Modal Structure of Quantum Evolution

### A self-contained derivation, within the From/With/This framework, of what spatial propagation is, why there is a finite invariant velocity (the role played in physics by the speed of light), why it is unique and constant, and how the continuous Schrödinger evolution and the discrete collapse of the wavefunction sit as two distinct modal registers. The treatment is architectonic: each concept is built from the framework's primitives rather than imported from physics in its mature form.

## 0. Orientation and primitives

This document assumes the following framework primitives, used below without re-derivation:

- **The three offices (From, With, This).** Every content is constituted as three signature-distinct factors: From (oriented conditioning — the conditioning of a later state by an earlier), With (symmetric co-standing — the holding of a multiplicity together as a structured, differentiated whole), This (closure — standing as one). The three are *qualitatively distinct* and non-interchangeable on the constitutive (vertical) axis.
- **Causation as constitutive dependency**, in three office-kinds: From-causation (oriented succession-dependency), With-causation (symmetric co-determination among co-standing contents), This-causation (the closure conditioning its constituents). Causation is not a relation between independent things but the constitution-relation by which the conditioned is the determinate thing it is.
- **The physics domain constraint** is threefold: a physical content is *self-transforming* (From), *invariant* (This — a conserved structure persists across the transformation), and *coherent* (With — the contents are held together in mutual relation). Causation is this constraint read from inside.
- **A physical state** is a self-transforming object carrying three intrinsic characteristics: an **invariant** (its conserved kind), an intrinsic **generator** (its available transformations), and intrinsic **coupling-slots** (capacities for its transformation to be co-conditioned with other self-transformers, conditioned on slot-compatibility, not on sameness of kind).
- **The Hamiltonian** (established prior): the conserved coherent total generator of the field's joint self-transformation; it generates temporal succession, and its conservation is time-translation invariance. It is the signature of the physics subdomain in the modal register (see §6).
- **The modal triad**: admissibility / realizability / actuality. Realizability is the coherent constraint of the accumulated standing actuals on the next actual; it is continuous and develops coherently. Actuality is the standing of actuals, which accumulate monotonically; each actual, once standing, re-constrains the subsequent realizability.
- **Structured coherence (locality in germ).** With holds multiplicity *as* multiplicity — in a *differentiated* relatedness, not an undifferentiated all-to-all heap. Each co-standing content is directly related only within a bounded neighborhood; "near" and "far" are real because the relatedness is structured.

The temporal metric is the count of conditioning-steps (one self-transformation = one step). The spatial metric is the count of co-standing positions between contents. These are introduced prior; this document explains how they are commensurated.

## 1. The problem

A temporal step is clean: one conditioning, one recursion, the self-transformation advancing once. Spatial *propagation* is not clean on its face. "A cause propagates through space" appears to require a thing that travels from position to position — which smuggles in a traveler crossing a container, exactly the picture the framework refuses (no independent substance moving through a pre-given space).

To put the temporal and spatial metrics into a single structure — and so to have a spacetime at all — we must say what propagation *is* with no traveler, and we must find the relationship that commensurates a temporal step with a spatial step. That relationship is the role played in physics by the invariant velocity *c*. The questions are sharp:

1. Is the propagation rate **finite** (forced)?
2. Is it the **unique** commensuration of the temporal and spatial metrics?
3. Is it **constant** (the same everywhere and in every frame)?
4. What *is* propagation, with no traveler?

## 2. Neighbor, defined objectively

Coupling is conditioning: by the intrinsic coupling-slot, one self-transformer's state conditions another's. Crucially, conditioning is **From-oriented and costs a step**: A's state at step *n* conditions B's state at step *n+1*. The conditioning is *across* a temporal step, not within one.

Define: **B is a neighbor of A** iff A directly conditions B in one temporal step (one application of the coupling). This is objective — it is read off the conditioning structure (which operators directly condition which, in one step), not stipulated. No unit length is imposed; the neighbor-relation *is* the one-step conditioning relation.

**Distance** is then derived, not posited: the distance from A to a content D is the number of conditioning-links on the shortest conditioning-chain from A to D. If A conditions B, B conditions C, C conditions D, then D is three links from A.

## 3. Why propagation is finite, and intransitive

The decisive structural fact is that conditioning costs a step, which makes **one-step reach intransitive**.

Suppose A conditions B and B conditions C. Then A's state at step *n* conditions B at step *n+1*, and B at *n+1* conditions C at *n+2*. Therefore a change at A reaches C at step *n+2* — **two steps**, not one. C is *not* affected at step *n+1*. The one-step reach of A is exactly its immediate neighbors and no further.

This is what forbids instantaneous propagation across the spread. To reach a content *d* links away requires traversing *d* conditioning-links, which requires *d* temporal steps, because each link-traversal *is* one conditioning *is* one step. Instantaneous reach across a gap would require a direct conditioning between non-neighbors — but "non-neighbor" *means* "not directly conditioned in one step." So:

- **Finiteness is forced.** A change propagates at most one conditioning-link per temporal step. (Structured coherence independently forbids the all-to-all heap that instantaneous propagation would require; the conditioning-costs-a-step fact is the same locality stated dynamically.)
- **Intransitivity is secured.** Coherence over time is preserved (A, B, C are mutually coherent across steps), but the *one-step* reach is strictly the immediate neighbors. This is what was missing from any account that treats coupling as instantaneous mutual constraint: such an account makes coherence transitively global and collapses all distance. Conditioning-across-a-step restores genuine distance.

## 4. Why it is the unique commensuration

The commensuration we seek is the relationship between one temporal step and one spatial step. The propagation *is* this relationship: it is precisely how much spatial displacement (links) one temporal step (conditioning) yields.

Could there be a different commensuration? Only if there were another structural bridge between the temporal register (From-succession) and the spatial register (With-spread). But conditioning is the *sole* bridge: a conditioning at one position-and-step is exactly what links a temporal step to a spatial displacement (it conditions a neighboring position at the next step). Time and space are otherwise distinct registers — oriented From versus unoriented With — with no other relationship between their counts. Therefore the propagation rate is the **unique** commensuration: it is the only structural relationship capable of putting the two metrics into common terms.

## 5. Why it is constant — and why this *is* the invariant velocity

The relevant rate is the **direct neighbor-to-neighbor propagation of causal coherence**, and it is **one conditioning-link per one conditioning-step**. This is a *maximal*, *null* rate — an upper bound — not a universal speed:

- It **cannot exceed** one link per step: that would require traversing two links in one step, i.e. conditioning a non-neighbor directly — forbidden (a non-neighbor is by definition not directly conditioned in one step). This is the ceiling, and it is sharp.
- It is **attained** only by direct null propagation: a coherence-difference passed straight from a content to its immediate neighbor, with nothing else interposed.

Effective motion and most causal processes are **slower** than this bound. A massive body, a bound structure, a signal through a medium, or any composite re-coupling carries internal transformation, waiting, binding, path curvature, or non-null re-coupling — each of which consumes steps without advancing a link, so the effective rate falls below one. The invariant velocity is therefore the **upper bound and the null rate**: writing the rate as *v*, every propagation satisfies *v ≤ 1*, with *v = 1* attained only by direct null propagation.

The cleaner statement: *the direct neighbor-to-neighbor propagation of causal coherence occurs at one link per step; effective motion may be slower, because it may include internal transformation, waiting, binding, path curvature, or non-null re-coupling; but no propagation can exceed one link per step.*

What is fixed, then, is the **bound** — and the bound is fixed because it is **not a magnitude that happens to be set** but a structural **identity**. The spatial unit (one link) and the temporal unit (one step) are *the same act* — conditioning — counted two ways: as link (the spatial relation it establishes) and as step (the temporal advance it costs). One link **is** one step because traversing a link *is* taking a step; nothing can traverse more than one link per step because nothing can perform more than one conditioning per conditioning.

Consequences (all concerning the **bound**, the null rate):

- **The bound is constant**: an identity between two namings of the same act cannot vary. There is no room for the maximal rate to be other than one-per-one.
- **The bound is frame-invariant**: a "frame" is a way of counting steps and links. The identity between them is *prior to* any frame — it is what every frame is counting. Therefore every frame finds the same maximal propagation rate. This is the structural origin of the empirically puzzling fact that the invariant velocity is the same in all frames: it is not a fast thing whose measured speed happens to be constant; it is the identity between the spatial and temporal units, which no frame can alter because frames presuppose it.
- **The bound is naturally unity**: because it is the identity of the unit, the maximal rate *is the unit*. Its numerical value in any other system (e.g. metres per second) is an artifact of unrelated, arbitrarily-sized units of length and time; that number carries no fundamental content. Set against its own structure, the invariant velocity is **one**, and all other quantities take their numbers as ratios to it.

This is what the speed of light *is*, fundamentally: the identity between the spatial and temporal units, both being the single act of conditioning counted two ways — the finite, unique, constant, frame-invariant upper bound on all propagation, attained by direct null propagation, equal to one by nature.

## 6. What propagation *is* — no traveler

Propagation can now be stated with no traveler, using the Hamiltonian as the unified coherent standing.

Take the total field as the **single coherent standing** the Hamiltonian generates. It advances: one recursion, one temporal step, the whole moving from standing-1 to standing-2. That is the temporal step of the *whole*.

Now resolve the whole into its differentiated sub-standings — the diverse local conditions across the field. These are bound into *one* coherent standing by the conservation laws (that mutual coherence is what makes them one Hamiltonian and not many). When the whole advances one step, every sub-standing must advance *in coherence with its neighbors* — and neighbor-coherence is local (a sub-standing is directly related only to its neighbors). Therefore a local difference can be brought into coherence only with the *next* step's condition of its *immediate* neighbors, and with more distant sub-standings only at later steps, one neighbor outward per step.

**Propagation is this**: the neighbor-by-neighbor resolution of local coherence-differences across the differentiated field as the unified standing advances. Nothing travels. A "cause propagating through space" is a local coherence-difference being brought into coherence with successively more distant sub-standings — one conditioning-link per temporal step — because (a) the whole must remain coherent (conservation), (b) coherence is local (structured With), so (c) a difference can be resolved only one neighbor outward per advance.

The unified standing's single temporal advance and the propagation of coherence across the sub-standings are therefore **the same event at two grains**: as the whole, it is one temporal step; as the differentiated field, it is propagation across space. The invariant velocity is simply the grain-conversion between these two readings of one event — which is why it is the identity (one), constant, and frame-invariant.

## 7. Motion — how neighborhood relations change

If distance is conditioning-chain-length, motion cannot be a thing sliding through fixed positions; it must be the **conditioning structure itself re-forming**.

Which coupling-slots are filled is part of the standing, and the standing advances each step. So at each step the conditioning-network can re-form: which operator directly conditions which can change as states advance. A content D at three links from A comes to be at two links from A when the couplings re-form so that a shorter conditioning-chain from A to D exists. Motion is **re-coupling** — the change in who directly conditions whom — which alters the chain-length distances.

This re-coupling is itself governed by the one-step rule. A coupling can re-form only between contents that are *already* neighbors, because forming a coupling is a conditioning, which costs a step and reaches one link. Hence the geometry changes **locally and incrementally**: at each step, distances change by at most one link. This is a speed limit on motion itself — nothing approaches anything faster than one link per step — because motion *is* a propagation (of coupling-structure), bounded by the same identity as all propagation.

Two consequences worth noting:

- The geometry is **dynamical**: the spatial structure (the conditioning-network) changes as the standing advances. It is not a fixed stage.
- This dynamical re-forming, driven by where the field's content (energy) is concentrated, is a **strong lead toward** the gravitational character of the metric: it is plausibly the same structure as the mutual conditioning of energy and spatial geometry, with motion and gravitation two aspects of one fact — the conditioning-geometry re-forming. This is marked as a lead, not a sealed result. The step from "motion is re-coupling" to gravitational metric dynamics is not trivial and requires a dedicated walk: it must show how energy concentration constrains the re-coupling rules, how the local causal cones tilt and curve as a result, and how this recovers geodesic behavior and, in later articulation, field equations. Those are not established here.

## 8. Relation to the discrete-causal-network picture

The structure derived here is a **discrete causal network with a minimum causal event**: one conditioning is the fundamental event (the minimum temporal unit), one link is the fundamental spatial relation (the minimum spatial unit), and the invariant velocity is the ratio of the fundamental length to the fundamental time. This coincides in form with independently developed approaches in physics that treat spacetime as a fundamentally discrete causal structure from which smooth geometry is emergent at large scales. The framework arrives at this structure as *forced* rather than postulated.

A note on "smoothness." It is not obvious that reality is smooth at the level of events at all. What is continuous is the field of the *possible* (§9); what is *actual* is organized into discrete, quantized events; and registration by an observer is itself sampled at finite rates far coarser than the fundamental scale. A world that is discrete at the level of causal events, continuous at the level of the realizable field, and coarsely sampled at the level of observation, is fully consistent with what is observed. The recovery of the smooth large-scale geometry from the discrete substructure is a genuine open problem (shared with the discrete-causal-network programs in physics) and is not claimed here; only the substructure and the status of the invariant velocity within it are established.

## 9. The Schrödinger equation and the collapse of the wavefunction as two modal registers

The framework's modal triad gives a clean account of the two faces of quantum evolution and dissolves the apparent conflict between continuous evolution and discrete events.

**The Schrödinger field is the realizability field.** The wavefunction is not a thing in space; it is the structured field of *what is realizable* — the coherent constraint of the accumulated actuals on the next actual. Its evolution is generated by the Hamiltonian (the signature of the physics subdomain in the modal register). The Schrödinger equation is, in framework terms, the statement that **the realizability field evolves under the total coherent generator**. It is continuous and deterministic because *realizability* is the coherent, recursive constraint developing — the field of the coherent possible — not an actual occurring. The continuity of Schrödinger evolution is the continuity of realizability.

**Collapse is the realizability-to-actuality transition.** The realizability field carries multiple realizable forks coherently together (the superposition). Collapse is the occurrence of one **actual**: one fork is selected and stands. The discreteness of collapse is the discreteness of *actualization* — an actual is a This, a closure standing as one, hence discrete and quantized. Once standing, the actual accumulates monotonically and **re-constrains** the subsequent realizability field (realizability is the constraint of the accumulated actuals on the next actual). So the post-collapse evolution proceeds from a realizability field reshaped by the new actual.

**The measurement problem is dissolved, not solved.** The apparent puzzle — that continuous deterministic evolution sometimes jumps discontinuously to a definite outcome — arises from forcing two distinct modal registers onto one. Realizability (the coherent possible) is continuous; actuality (the standing actual) is discrete; they are not the same kind of thing, so there is no mystery in one being continuous and the other discrete. "Collapse" is simply the modal transition from realizability to actuality, which *must* be discrete because actuals are Thises. Treating the wavefunction as itself an actual that must then collapse is the category error; separating the registers removes the puzzle.

**Observation** is the registration of accumulated actuals — coarsely sampled, discrete, and at rates far above the fundamental scale. It is not a separate mechanism that forces actualization; it is the standing-actual registered.

**Selection weighting.** The structure of the transition is supplied here: a realizability field of multiple forks, from which one actual is selected and stands. The measure on that selection is supplied by the Registered Realizability Weighting Law and its Born instance, `|h(σ,k)|²`. No separate frequency bridge is required. Absolute local transition rates still require the possibility-space and rate structure of the process.
