# Gauge Structure Result

## Mature status

Status: **Registered, not Sealed** for the P9 alternating-form and native SU(3) bridge.

P8 now supplies the upstream carrier package at its own grade: one same-kind complex carrier `C^3`, primitive scalar verdict type, fixed scalar scale, and Hermitian registration pairing. P9 must not re-prove those claims through the alternating form or SU(3).

## P8 input boundary

P9 may inherit:

- one same-kind carrier;
- complex representation type;
- dimension exactly three;
- scalar closure-verdict type;
- fixed verdict scale;
- Hermitian registration pairing.

Each carries the named P8 seal residuals. P9 may not back-spend its center, stabilizer, determinant, or epsilon structure as P8 warrant.

## P9 claim

The P9 load is narrower:

`P8 carrier package + full ternary closure registration => primitive nonzero alternating complex 3-form; stabilizer and pairing preservation => SU(3)`.

The mathematical step is clean: given a fixed nonzero alternating complex 3-form on `C^3`, its stabilizer in `GL(3,C)` is `SL(3,C)`; intersecting with the pairing-preserving `U(3)` gives `SU(3)`. This step is **Sealed as mathematics**.

The framework bridge remains **Registered, not Sealed** because alternation is not yet fully grounded.

## What stands

- The internal distinction is the This-flattening / interior triadic distinction, not the old mediation-carrier framing.
- P8 independently supplies `C^3`; P9 does not derive dimension from epsilon.
- The closure-form is scalar-valued and normalized at the P8 interface, but its being a fixed alternating 3-form is a P9 result.
- Connectedness and irreducibility retain their owning P9 standing.
- The stabilizer theorem yields native SU(3) once the P9 form is granted.

## The live seal gate

The load-bearing premise is:

> the ternary closure-floor vanishes when two office contributions coincide.

Over characteristic not equal to two, vanishing on coincidence gives alternation. This excludes the symmetric cubic/trilinear countermodel. The premise is well motivated by triadic non-degeneracy but is not yet fully derived without assuming the antisymmetry it is meant to establish.

The remaining P9 countermodel set therefore includes:

- a symmetric cubic or symmetric trilinear invariant;
- any non-alternating scalar trilinear closure verdict compatible with the P8 carrier package;
- projective or phase-relaxed form variants not excluded by the precise P9 normalization argument.

Role-sorted lower-dimensional carriers, primitive vector/operator verdicts, and real-carrier alternatives belong to P8 and must not be counted again as P9 debts.

## Not derived

- Electroweak mixing angle.
- Exact hypercharge assignments.
- Exact chiral fermion module.
- Generation count.
- Masses or Yukawa couplings.
- Completeness of `SU(3) x SU(2) x U(1)` as the entire internal gauge structure.
