# Physics Registration Rule

This page is the canonical P1 control for spending content within the physics subdomain and for deciding when a familiar physics name may be used. The historical title called this a “theorem.” Its operative content is a rule of boundary-membership, claim-recording, and semantic control; it contributes no warrant to a particular downstream physics claim.

P2 establishes the physics boundary as the valid nonempty constraint selecting self-transforming / invariant / coherent mathematical structure. The familiar name **physics** is Registered for that bounded domain core, with mature physics content quarantined. P1 neither adds a transition into that domain nor upgrades any later claim made within it.

## The operative preflight

A physics expenditure must answer the questions relevant to the load it bears.

1. **Inherited-grade integrity.** Which exact mathematical or foundational content is spent, and at what state, warrant, scope, condition, and debt? The prior closure is inherited whole: no certified distinction may be contradicted or silently dropped. Inheritance does **not** require every feature to be active in every later construction.
2. **Boundary membership.** Does the content satisfy the established self-transforming / invariant / coherent physics constraint, or is it being cited only as mathematics, comparison, or a later proposal? Membership is this satisfaction; no second physical-occupancy test follows it.
3. **Additional claim.** What, if anything, is being added beyond boundary membership and inherited structure? Only that addition owes a new construction. Do not invent a failure mode or proof demand without identifying an operation that could produce it.
4. **Structural trace where load-bearing.** If From, With, This, vertical constitution, or horizontal build does inferential work, where does it do so? Do not demand a redundant office-by-office display when no such structure is being newly claimed.
5. **Source and claim record.** Which content is framework-derived, mathematics-as-mathematics, empirically observed, externally cited, selected, or comparison-only? Record framework state and warrant independently from semantic registration. `Native` is a semantic state, not shorthand for “physically established.”
6. **Name boundary.** If a mature physics name is used, what Secured framework core licenses it, what exact properties are mapped, and what ordinary surplus remains quarantined? Registered–Sealed is available only where scoped bidirectional content-identity has actually been shown.

Failure of a relevant question fixes the strongest honest use: mathematics outside the physics boundary, empirical observation, external comparison, a conditional later proposal, Conjectured/Open framework claim, or an Unregistered legacy name. These labels answer different questions and may not be arranged on one ladder.

## Whole inheritance without automatic deployment

The successor does not choose a convenient mathematical handle and discard the rest of the gate-package. Every living, relevant prior constraint remains binding at its existing grade. Each physics expenditure must therefore record the inherited item as **bites / does not bite / unresolved**.

This is not a demand that every inherited feature appear actively in every physical content. M6, for example, makes conditional rule-presented scalar constructions available; it does not thereby construct a continuum carrier, direction topology, manifold, positive-definite pairing, or Hilbert space. Those are genuinely additional structures and retain item-specific work. Constitutive inherited structure, by contrast, is not re-admitted merely because the domain has changed.

## Mathematics, framework construction, and observation

Physics is not made by adding occurrence-stuff to mathematics. The same possible / realizable / actual registers govern both. What changes is the selecting boundary and the characteristics of the contents admitted beneath it.

Three source classes may contribute to a physics page, provided their roles remain explicit:

1. **Framework construction** may establish a claim about content satisfying the physics boundary or may add later domain structure.
2. **Mathematics as mathematics** may establish formal consequences. When the object satisfies the physics boundary, those consequences apply within the physics domain at their exact scope; resemblance to a different mature physics object does not establish that different claim.
3. **Empirical observation as observation** may empirically secure an observed claim or supply a measured input. It cannot be relabeled as an internal framework derivation.

Source class is not epistemic state. A mathematical theorem can be theorem-Secured as mathematics while its proposed physical expenditure remains conditional. An observation can be empirically Secured while the corresponding framework-native derivation remains Open.

## Mature physics names are bundles

Registration proceeds property by property. A licensed use of `electron`, for example, does not automatically license its spin, charge value, chirality, representation, mass origin, or decay role. The same rule applies to `particle`, `field`, `mass`, `charge`, `interaction`, `quark`, `lepton`, `neutrino`, `proton`, `neutron`, `gauge`, `spacetime`, and every other inherited physics term.

For a merely Registered name, each downstream use must verify that it spends only the mapped core. Registered–Sealed removes that surplus audit only within the sealed scope; contextual associations outside that scope remain excluded.

The map is routing and control, not a blanket regrade. A consumer that needs more than P1 supplies must retain its own condition, handback, quarantine, or frontier. P1 may not be cited as compact proof of a downstream physical result.

## Verification gate

For any physics expenditure, a fresh reviewer asks:

1. What exact mathematical content is being spent, at what grade and conditions?
2. How does the content satisfy the established physics boundary?
3. What genuinely additional structure is claimed, and what construction establishes it?
4. Which statements are framework construction, mathematics, observation, citation, selection, or comparison?
5. What are the independent framework and registration records?
6. What legacy properties remain quarantined?
7. Which later frontier item or physical construction is still required?

If the record cannot answer these questions, the physical use is not licensed at the claimed reach.

## P1 disposition

P1 **PASSES as a control and dependency-map unit**. The pass means only that boundary membership, additional-claim burdens, and the current downstream load have been made explicit. It does not mean that all mapped consumers pass or that any mature physical name has been newly Registered.
