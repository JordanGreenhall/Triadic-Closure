# Physics Registration Rule

This page is the canonical P1 control for deciding when mathematical content may be spent inside the physics subdomain and when a familiar physics name may be used. The historical title called this a “theorem.” Its operative content is a rule of admission, claim-recording, and semantic control; it does not derive a physical object or close the physics gate.

The current proposed physics boundary selects self-transforming / invariant / coherent mathematical structure. P1 does not upgrade that proposal, construct the positive mathematics→physics gate, force occupancy, or certify any later physics object. Those claims retain their owning states, conditions, and debts.

## The operative preflight

A candidate is admissible for framework-physics use only when its owning record answers all six questions.

1. **Inherited-grade integrity.** Which exact mathematical or foundational content is spent, and at what state, warrant, scope, condition, and debt? The prior closure is inherited whole: no certified distinction may be contradicted or silently dropped. Inheritance does **not** require every feature to instantiate in every candidate.
2. **Boundary and constraint.** What exhibited physics boundary admits the candidate, and how does it satisfy the selected self-transforming / invariant / coherent constraint? If the positive gate is still conditional, that condition travels.
3. **Occupancy and participation.** What construction shows that this mathematical content actually occupies the physics subdomain or participates in its lineage? Formal resemblance, availability, or mature-physics expectation is insufficient.
4. **Two-axis structural trace.** What is From, With, and This in the candidate, and how does it stand in the horizontal build? An office may not be treated as an external base, and vertical constitution may not substitute for horizontal occupancy.
5. **Source and claim record.** Which content is framework-derived, mathematics-as-mathematics, empirically observed, externally cited, selected, or comparison-only? Record framework state and warrant independently from semantic registration. `Native` is a semantic state, not shorthand for “physically established.”
6. **Name boundary.** If a mature physics name is used, what Secured framework core licenses it, what exact properties are mapped, and what ordinary surplus remains quarantined? Registered–Sealed is available only where scoped bidirectional content-identity has actually been shown.

Failure of a question does not erase the candidate. It fixes the strongest honest use: mathematics-as-mathematics, empirical observation, external comparison, a conditional physical proposal, Conjectured/Open framework claim, or an Unregistered legacy name. These labels answer different questions and may not be arranged on one ladder.

## Whole inheritance without automatic deployment

The successor does not choose a convenient mathematical handle and discard the rest of the gate-package. Every living, relevant prior constraint remains binding at its existing grade. Each physics expenditure must therefore record the inherited item as **bites / does not bite / unresolved**.

This is not a demand that every gate feature appear actively in every physical content. M6, for example, makes conditional rule-presented scalar constructions available; it does not thereby instantiate a physical continuum, direction carrier, topology, manifold, positive-definite pairing, or Hilbert space. Active deployment and closure of the selected rule calculus remain item-specific physics work.

## Mathematics, framework construction, and observation

Physics is not made by adding occurrence-stuff to mathematics. The same possible / realizable / actual registers govern both. What changes is the selecting boundary and the characteristics of the contents admitted beneath it.

Three source classes may contribute to a physics page, provided their roles remain explicit:

1. **Framework construction** may establish a physical-domain claim through the P1 trace.
2. **Mathematics as mathematics** may establish formal consequences about an already licensed mathematical object. It cannot establish physical occupancy merely from isomorphism or resemblance.
3. **Empirical observation as observation** may empirically secure an observed claim or supply a measured input. It cannot be relabeled as an internal framework derivation.

Source class is not epistemic state. A mathematical theorem can be theorem-Secured as mathematics while its proposed physical expenditure remains conditional. An observation can be empirically Secured while the corresponding framework-native derivation remains Open.

## Mature physics names are bundles

Registration proceeds property by property. A licensed use of `electron`, for example, does not automatically license its spin, charge value, chirality, representation, mass origin, or decay role. The same rule applies to `particle`, `field`, `mass`, `charge`, `interaction`, `quark`, `lepton`, `neutrino`, `proton`, `neutron`, `gauge`, `spacetime`, and every other inherited physics term.

For a merely Registered name, each downstream use must verify that it spends only the mapped core. Registered–Sealed removes that surplus audit only within the sealed scope; contextual associations outside that scope remain excluded.

## Verification gate

For any physics expenditure, a fresh reviewer asks:

1. What exact mathematical content is being spent, at what grade and conditions?
2. Where is the physics boundary and candidate occupancy exhibited?
3. What is the vertical and horizontal trace?
4. Which statements are framework construction, mathematics, observation, citation, selection, or comparison?
5. What are the independent framework and registration records?
6. What legacy properties remain quarantined?
7. Which later frontier item or physical construction is still required?

If the record cannot answer these questions, the physical use is not licensed at the claimed reach.

## P1 disposition

P1 **PASSES as a control and dependency-map unit**. The pass means only that the admission rule and its current downstream load have been made explicit. It does not mean that all mapped consumers pass, that the proposed physics gate is Secured, or that any mature physical name has been newly Registered.
