# Physics Registration Theorem

This page records the first cleanup loop from the SM-content smuggle frontier. It
states the rule for when a mathematical structure may be treated as a physical
standing inside the framework.

## Mature status

Status: **Registered as a control rule; not a sealed derivation of every later
physical object**.

The rule is strong enough to govern downstream pages: a page may not treat a
mature physics name as framework-native unless the content of that name has passed
this registration rule, or unless the page explicitly marks it as empirical
observation or external comparison.

## The rule

A candidate structure has **framework-native physical standing** only when all five
conditions are met.

1. **From-whole inheritance.** The structure is reached from the inherited prior
   object as a whole, not from a selected handle chosen because mature physics
   wants that handle.
2. **Physics-domain constraint.** It satisfies the physics-entry constraint:
   self-transforming / invariant / coherent structure.
3. **Vertical constitution.** Its office-composition is stated: what in it is From,
   With, and This, without treating one office as an external base.
4. **Physics-subdomain participation.** It has a rule for how it belongs to the
   physics subdomain's self-transforming / invariant / coherent lineage, rather
   than remaining only an unregistered formal resemblance.
5. **Status and boundary declaration.** The page names which content is
   framework-derived, which is mathematics-as-mathematics, which is empirical
   observation, and which mature-physics content is only comparison.

If any condition fails, the candidate may still appear in the wiki, but only under
a weaker label: **mathematical comparison**, **empirical observation**,
**conditional result**, **Conjectured**, or **Open**.

## Mathematics / physics relation: no difference in modal kind

This rule must not be read as importing a non-framework topology between
mathematics and physics. The source discipline is the opposite: **physics is wholly
a subset of mathematics; realizability is realizability and actuality is actuality
across mathematics, topology, algebra, and physics.** Physics adds no Cartesian
ingredient, occurrence-stuff, or special kind of actuality to mathematics.

What differentiates physics is not a new mode of being, but the particular
characteristics by which the physics subdomain self-selects: its actuals develop
under the physics-entry signature of self-transforming / invariant / coherent
structure. Registration therefore asks whether a mathematical object is being
read as belonging to that subdomain under that signature, not whether mathematics
has somehow become non-mathematical.

## What is preserved from mathematics

Mathematics may supply formal consequences once the framework has licensed the
mathematical object. For example, if a fixed nonzero alternating form on a
complex 3-carrier is licensed, its stabilizer facts may be used as mathematics.

But a formal resemblance or a selected mathematical handle does not by itself
license a mature **physics-subdomain** standing. A formal object is not a
framework-native particle, field, mass, charge, interaction, or decay channel
merely because there is a mathematical structure that resembles one. It must be
shown to belong to the physics subdomain under that domain's signature, or else be
marked as mathematics-as-mathematics, empirical observation, or comparison.

## What the physics domain adds

The physics domain adds the demand that the structure stand as
self-transforming / invariant / coherent. In practice this means:

- it can participate in a transformation without losing the invariant that makes
  it the same structure;
- it has a coherence condition rather than a loose association of labels;
- its actuality-status is named: actual, realizable, constraint, measure, state,
  event, or empirical observation.

This is the minimal anti-smuggle gate. It prevents a result page from silently
moving from “there is a useful mathematical analogy” to “the framework has derived
a mature physical entity.”

## Three permitted bases

A load-bearing claim in a physics page must come from one of three bases:

1. **Framework derivation** — built from the foundational stack and physics-domain
   entry rule.
2. **Mathematics as mathematics** — a theorem or computation about a licensed
   mathematical object.
3. **Empirical observation as observation** — allowed as an anchor or check, but
   not relabeled as a framework derivation.

The verifier should reject only claims that cross these boundaries without saying
so. It should not reject legitimate mathematics or legitimate empirical anchoring
when those are correctly labeled.

## Consequence for mature physics names

A mature physics name is a bundle. Registration is property-by-property, not
name-by-name.

Thus `electron` may be licensed as a native mass ruler only to the degree that
`electron bare repeat` has been registered. That does not automatically license
spin, charge value, chirality, Yukawa origin, weak representation, or decay role.

Likewise `proton`, `neutron`, `quark`, `lepton`, `neutrino`, `field`, `charge`,
`mass`, and `interaction` must be decomposed into licensed content, empirical
content, and unlicensed mature-physics content before being used downstream.

## Verification gate

For any future physics result, the verifier asks:

1. What is the candidate physical standing?
2. Which exact content is framework-derived?
3. Which exact content is mathematics-as-mathematics?
4. Which exact content is empirical observation?
5. Which mature-physics content is only comparison or remains Open?
6. Does the argument rely on a later unpassed frontier item?

If those questions cannot be answered, the page must be downgraded or quarantined.

## Loop 1 decision

This page does not seal the whole physics domain. It registers the control rule by
which later physical standings are admitted. It is a pre-flight gate for the
constructive program, not a substitute for that program. Later loops must not stop
at saying “this was smuggled”; they must identify the function the smuggle served
and attempt to rebuild that function from the framework basis, characteristic by
characteristic.

The right grade is therefore:

**Registered control rule.**

Not:

- “all later physical vocabulary is sealed”;
- “formal resemblance alone licenses mature physics-subdomain standing”;
- “empirical labels are framework derivations.”
