## Exact mathematics and countermodels

### Plane count

For spatial dimension `d`, the number of unordered coordinate two-planes is

`C(d,2)=d(d-1)/2`.

At `d=3`, this is exactly `3`. This proves only the count. A family

`Lambda_alpha=alpha R_H^-2`

has the same dimensions and macro scaling for every dimensionless `alpha`. Choosing `alpha=1`, `2`, `3`, or any other value is an exact countermodel to the claim that dimensional scaling alone forces the coefficient. The coefficient proposal therefore bears its scalar-budget and per-plane-unit premises explicitly.

### Present-state identity

From

`Omega_Lambda=Lambda c^2/(3H_0^2)`

and

`R_H=c/H_0`,

pure substitution yields

`Omega_Lambda=Lambda R_H^2/3`

and hence

`Lambda=3 Omega_Lambda R_H^-2`.

This algebra is exact and model-independent once those definitions are selected. Because `Omega_Lambda` already contains `Lambda`, substituting measured values back into the identity is not an independent prediction.

The AUD-014 route is an exclusion argument, not a numerical calculation. It has the logical form

`(no arena carrier) and (no third conditioning mode) -> no unsourced background-curvature mode`.

If either premise is not established at cosmological scope, `k=0` does not follow from this route. Sourced curvature and perturbation-scale observables remain separate.
