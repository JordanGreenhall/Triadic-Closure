# Appendix — Foundation Floor and Engine

This appendix supplies the detailed arguments supporting document 01, sections 0–5. Document 01 governs every claim’s standing and scope. The appendix preserves explanatory depth without creating a second authority surface.

## 1. The foundation and its warrant

The floor has four components with two different warrant structures.

First, relating is ineliminable. Suppose relating were assembled from prior unrelated parts. The act that assembles the parts must connect them, and connecting is already relating. The proposed construction therefore deploys what it claims to construct from non-relation. What is secured is non-compositeness in the relevant sense: relating is not built from prior unrelated units. This does not make relating a bare numerical one. Its unity is already articulated as one act with three offices.

Second, any determinate assertion deploys at least three conditions. It distinguishes something from what it is not; it holds the distinguished terms together without collapsing them into unity or scattering them into unrelated plurality; and it stands as a determinate assertion. A denial of distinction distinguishes. A denial of holding holds its own terms together. A denial of determinate standing stands as a determinate claim. These three conditions are the offices later named From, With, and This. Retorsion therefore blocks collapse to one or two.

The remaining two components are completeness claims. That relating is primitive says that nothing stands beneath it. That the offices are exactly three says that the ultimate office inventory contains no fourth. In both cases the completeness diagnostic applies: an act cannot certify the exhaustiveness of its own ultimate inventory without deploying that inventory in the certification. These claims therefore stand as defended posits rather than as conclusions of an unavailable further proof.

Exactly-three carries maximal warrant for a claim of its kind. It has a forced floor: at least three offices are retorsion-secured. Candidate fourths—modality, direction, negation, plurality, agency—return as a status, texture, relatum, or second-order articulation of the three. The claim is reflexively stable because its defense itself distinguishes candidates, holds them against the offices, and stands as a verdict. A genuine irreducible fourth office would refute it. What is unavailable is not refutation but absolute certification, and the same ceiling binds every rival ultimate count.

## 2. Architectonic discourse

Formal proof operates inside a fixed inventory of terms and moves. Architectonic rigor operates at the level where that inventory is being located. The discourse used to state the foundation is conducted from within developed language and inference, but the logic of the exposition does not generate the floor. It displays dependency traces from the order of knowing back toward what is prior in the order of being.

The method also passes a reflexive office check. Dependency reads conditioning asymmetry and therefore maps to From. Retorsion reads the ineliminable office-composition deployed in every determinate act. Construction is horizontal generation from established standing. Selection is constraint operated as a licensing boundary. Recognition attaches an inherited name after the structure stands. This mapping does not provide the method’s original warrant; it confirms that the method introduces no fourth kind of operation foreign to the architecture.

## 3. The unbounded possible and constraint

The unbounded possible is not a largest object, empty container, or metaphysical material. It is the refusal to begin from a bounded actuality. Nothing stands outside it from which a later content or constraint could be imported.

Constraint therefore does not add. It selects. A boundary excludes what fails it and thereby locates a region of admissibility. This reverses the ordinary burden of a building-up ontology. The question is not where a novel substance came from, but whether the proposed content lies within the possible and which constraint selects it. Selection is auditable because the boundary is explicit.

The operational use of admissibility appears only after a particular boundary stands. The unbounded possible is admissibility in the limit-case sense: no prior boundary has yet narrowed it. The two senses must not be silently exchanged.

## 4. The three offices

The offices are gerunds—conditioning-from, holding-with, arriving-as-this—not substances or independent components.

### 4.1 From

From is determinate distinction and one-way conditioning. It has two inseparable directions: distinction-from and orientation-toward. A cut distinguishes what is admitted from what is excluded; an oriented conditioning points toward what may stand. Directional asymmetry is therefore not a later fourth ingredient. It is From under its one-way character.

### 4.2 With

With is coherent holding without unification. It allows distinct standings to co-stand without becoming one item and without remaining unrelated. Every With has a basis: the registered respect under which its occupants are held.

The held exceeds the registered. The occupants are individuated by their actual lineages and standings; the basis registers only a determinate respect. A basis that registered everything true of what it held would exclude along no respect and would therefore cease to be a determinate boundary. Every determinate basis under-registers.

With is consequently not an empty container and not a thin set. A standing may participate in several holdings. The field conditions its occupants, and the occupants in turn texture the field. Co-conditioning does not abolish their distinction.

### 4.3 This

This is actuality at full strength: the arrived determinate standing. It has four faces.

- **Determinateness:** it has a checkable edge and bounded specificity.
- **Composedness:** vertically it is the one act of From and With; horizontally it may contain accumulated sub-standings.
- **Composability:** once actual, it is available for further holdings.
- **Conditionedness:** it bears both the source-conditioning by which it stands and the field-conditioning in which it realizes.

This is a genuine unit without being a self-grounding substance. Closure separates the legitimate work of substance—bearer, subject, persistent unit—from existential independence.

## 5. Closure and the two dimensions

A closure is From-With-This standing as one. Read under arrival it is an actual; read under availability for further determination it is a standing. Relative stability means that the closure remains intelligible as the same standing across an admissible range of variation. Stability is maintained persistence, not frozen simplicity.

The architecture has two dimensions of composition.

The **vertical** is synchronic constitution. Every relating at every point is the three offices composed through one another. It does not accumulate and has no history. Read modally, it is the admissibility cascade of nested constraints. J, orientation reversal within a stage, belongs here.

The **horizontal** is diachronic accumulation. Actuals accrue; Realizability(S) is re-textured; completed closures become units at a new altitude; lineage and address grow. Rho-coherence belongs here.

A layer is a horizontal nesting unit, not a deeper vertical constitution. Cell, tissue, organ, organism, and ecology occupy different horizontal altitudes while participating identically in the vertical office-composition. The standing and building faces of a stride are readings of one horizontal event: standing reads the result against the vertical admissibility cascade, while building reads the changed state and changed realizability field.

## 6. The modal engine

The three registers are admissible, realizable, and actual.

- **Admissible:** permitted under the standing boundary.
- **Realizable:** available from the actual state S by an admissible passage.
- **Actual:** a realizable content that has arrived as This.

A possible With is merely permitted. A realizable With is available from S. An actual With is a realized holding borne by a closure. Realizability itself is an actual With: the field of what can co-stand from S is not an abstract list outside reality but a determinate field conditioned by the current standing.

A move is an admissible passage from S. Adjacency means realizable-from-S; it is primitive to the engine and does not presuppose a spatial metric. The validity rule requires three things simultaneously: admissibility under the boundary, realizability from the standing stock, and expression without importing content from a later register or domain.

Each stride has the same closure-form and a different condition. Nothing foreign is added, because the result was admissible; something genuinely changes, because an actual has joined S and altered Realizability(S). Form repeats while history advances.

The registers have different costs. Actualization is per-stride. Admissibility is field-like: once a region’s generating structure is actualized, its closure rules are shown admissible, and its construction spends no excluded content, the region is available at admissibility grade. Particular contents are then finite pulls of certified moves rather than independent foundational audits.

## 7. Determinate content and recognition

A determinate content has an edge, facets, a trace, and a basis. Facetness means that the content can be surveyed under several respects without dissolving into unrelated fragments. Trace-back locates the constraints and passages through which it stands. The basis states the respect currently registered.

Recognition attaches a mature name only after the framework structure is present. The name does no work in the construction, and every association not earned by the construction remains excluded. Recognition is therefore a mapping at a stated scope, not a license to import the later discipline whole.

## 8. The primordial unfolding

The primordial section shows how the engine begins without smuggling a prior inventory of actual contents.

### Step 0 — Commingled closure

The offices are one act without separately standing. No domain-internal horizontal stock exists.

### Step 1 — Separation of the offices

The offices become distinguishable without becoming independent entities. The separation is articulation within one act, not the assembly of three prior things.

### Step 2 — The first determinate content

The first This stands at the determinate/indeterminate edge. Its horizontal ledger is empty because no earlier actual exists. Its composition is therefore vertical: From and With close as one determinate standing. It is determinate in the limit-case sense of being the first edge; composed through the offices; composable because every actual can enter later holdings; and conditioned by the primordial office-articulation and realizability field.

### Step 3 — The second standing

Once the first This stands, the condition has changed. A further stride begins from S rather than from an empty horizontal ledger. The first content may be taken as a unit for a new determination. The engine’s repeatability is thus established without making the second stride identical to the first.

The primordial unfolding has a double output. It produces a standing and, simultaneously, a newly textured field of realizability. This double output is the source of horizontal accumulation: each actual both is and modifies what may next become actual.

## 9. Boundaries

This appendix does not add a fourth office, prove absolute completeness, define realizability from an external reachability relation, establish literal numerical self-holding, or settle any domain’s D-series occupancy. Those standings remain exactly as stated in document 01.
