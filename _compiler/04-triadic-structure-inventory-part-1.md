# Triadic Structure Semantic Inventory — Part 1, Corpus-Adjudicated

Source locus: §§0.1–1.2 of the original `triadic-structure-of-relating-rev-canonical.md`.

Method: each unit was searched across the original Drive corpus using its principal terms, earlier versions, conversation-ledger audits, later control pages, and downstream uses. Later summaries were not allowed to override detailed work. No later detailed source reverses the claims retained below. The principal corrections are separation of claim standing from warrant route, removal of obsolete “next obligation” language, and explicit preservation of the discourse-position and exact-count limitations identified by later audits.

## TS-001 — Architectonic argument precedes a fixed proof system
The treatise examines the conditions under which proof, derivation, domain, object, and determinate content can appear. It therefore uses architectonic dependency rather than reasoning only inside a fixed formal system.

**Parents:** AR-001, AR-002.
**Adjudication:** retained; corroborated by `architectonic-rigor-complete.md` and all later domain-entry work.
**Claim standing:** Registered.
**Warrant route:** methodological construction.

## TS-002 — Minimal dependency placement
A claim belongs at the first level where all of its dependencies are available. Later disciplinary names, analogies, and content remain quarantined until earned.

**Parents:** AR-003, AR-004, AR-012, AR-014.
**Spent by:** the ordering of the treatise and later domain walks.
**Adjudication:** retained; no later source licenses aesthetic or document-order placement in place of dependency placement.
**Claim standing:** Registered.
**Warrant route:** control rule.

## TS-003 — Source tags report route, not confidence or live physics status
`Posited`, `Forced`, `Recognition`, `Selected`, and `Open` in the foundational treatise primarily indicate the architectonic route and its stopping point. They are not ordinary confidence labels and may not be silently substituted for the four live physics statuses.

**Parents:** AR-012 through AR-016; claim-status control.
**Adjudication:** retained with correction. `Open` can also function as a live claim status where the treatise explicitly marks an unresolved joint; the other route terms remain separate metadata.
**Claim standing:** Registered control distinction.

## TS-004 — The foundation has secured roots and defended completeness posits
Relating is ineliminable; determination requires at least distinction, holding, and standing. Relating's architectonic primitiveness is Secured by dependency plus retorsion, while the exact completeness of the three-office inventory carries a defended completeness warrant.

**Parents:** AR-007 through AR-010, AR-015, AR-016.
**Adjudication:** retained. Later corpus work preserves the distinction between “at least three” and “exactly three.”
**Claim standing:** Registered as the framework’s warrant profile.

## TS-005 — Relating cannot be assembled from prior unrelated parts
Any assembly of supposedly unrelated parts already connects them; connecting is relating. Further, every determinate assertion that a candidate is prior to, beneath, or grounds relating places that candidate in an asymmetric relation to relating and thereby deploys relating. The candidate cannot be intelligibly asymmetrically prior; at most it is co-constitutive. This secures architectonic primitiveness without asserting an unrestricted inventory beyond intelligibility.

**Parents:** TS-004.
**Adjudication:** retained with scope made explicit.
**Claim standing:** Registered.
**Warrant route:** retorsion.

## TS-006 — Determination requires at least three offices
Every determinate claim performs distinction, holding, and standing. Attempts to eliminate one of these still deploy it. The result blocks reduction to one or two offices but does not establish exact completeness.

**Parents:** TS-004.
**Spent by:** From / With / This.
**Adjudication:** retained; this is the minimum-count result consistently spent downstream.
**Claim standing:** Registered.
**Warrant route:** retorsion.

## TS-007 — Exactly-three is a defended posit
The office inventory cannot certify its own exhaustive completeness from wholly outside itself. Exactly-three therefore carries a defense profile: no surviving counterexample, examined rivals return as subdivisions or second-order statuses of the three, and the inventory remains reflexively stable when applied to its own defense.

**Parents:** TS-006, AR-015, AR-016.
**Adjudication:** retained. Later dimensionality work spends exactly-three at this architectonic strength; it does not convert the claim into a formal theorem.
**Claim standing:** Registered and Sealed at defended-posit strength within the framework.
**Warrant route:** defended posit under the totalization ceiling.

## TS-008 — Order of being differs from order of knowing
The floor is prior to developed logic in the order of being. The treatise is written from a late domain and uses developed inference to display backward dependency traces; its discourse does not generate the floor.

**Parents:** AR-011, TS-001, TS-002.
**Adjudication:** retained, but the later corpus audit correctly identifies an articulation debt: the treatise must explicitly state the standing of the discourse it uses.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-010 — Opening orientation: the unbounded possible under constraint
The account begins from the unbounded possible rather than a largest object, container, or prior substance. Successive constraints articulate admissibility, realizability, and actuality.

**Parents:** TS-004, TS-007.
**Adjudication:** retained as the framework’s opening orientation. Earlier “largest whole” or substance-like readings are excluded.
**Claim standing:** Registered within the framework.
**Warrant route:** defended opening posit and construction.

## TS-011 — Constraint selects rather than adds
A constraint does not import foreign content into the unbounded possible; it excludes what fails the boundary. This is spent by the modal engine, anti-smuggling discipline, primordial unfolding, domain phases, and domain entry.

**Parents:** TS-010, AR-013.
**Adjudication:** retained. Later domain-entry work confirms the rule, while also clarifying that a successor-domain constraint selects the domain without adding a mature domain ontology.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## Adjudicated limitations

1. `Forced`, `Recognition`, `Selected`, and `defended posit` remain warrant-route metadata and may not replace live status metadata.
2. “At least three” and “exactly three” must remain distinct.
3. Exact completeness is not upgraded to formal theoremhood.
4. The discourse-position debt remains visible rather than being silently treated as solved.
5. No later physics result may retroactively define the foundational offices.
