# Triadic Structure Semantic Inventory — Part 5, Corpus-Adjudicated

Source locus: §§6.1–6.6 of the original `triadic-structure-of-relating-rev-canonical.md`.

Method: every floor claim was checked against the later mathematics gate, conversation-ledger audit, quantity/quality uses, and downstream domain work. No later detailed source reverses the held/registered distinction or the floor limitations. Corrections below make explicit where later mathematics may extend the floor without retroactively importing cardinality, magnitude, or literal reflexivity into it.

## TSR-053 — Floor demonstrations use state, adjacency, move, arrived content
Each derivation must identify what stands, what is adjacent, what valid move occurs, and what content arrives.

**Adjudication:** retained as execution protocol.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-054 — Self- and other-relation arise from different bases over the same standings
The first This and With-as-This can be held under a basis registering same-kind standing or under a basis registering lineage/kind difference.

**Adjudication:** retained. The distinction is basis-relative, not a division into two stocks of entities.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-055 — Other-relation registers difference of kind
A holding is other-relational when its basis positively registers kind-difference across the held positions.

**Adjudication:** retained.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-056 — Self-relation is same-under-basis, not numerical self-identity
Distinct actual standings are held as the same in registered kind.

**Adjudication:** retained. Literal reflexive holding remains unavailable unless separately constructed.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-057 — Held distinctness and registered content are different layers
Actual positions are distinct by genesis, lineage, and address even when the basis registers only same-kind multiplicity.

**Adjudication:** retained as the load-bearing distinction for quantity.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-058 — `W(A,A)` and `W(A,B)` index registered character
The notation records what the basis registers, not the ontological identity or total inventory of what is held.

**Adjudication:** retained as notation control.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-059 — Quantity is quality-blind holding of distinct actuals
Quantity-structured content registers multiplicity without registering qualitative difference among the positions.

**Adjudication:** retained. The positions remain genuinely distinct in the build.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-060 — Quality is basis-registered difference
Quality-structured content arises when the basis positively registers difference in kind.

**Adjudication:** retained.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-061 — Quantity and quality are holdings of one standing ground
The same actual standings may yield quantity- or quality-structured content under different bases.

**Adjudication:** retained. The split is generated by basis, not by two ontological substances.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-062 — Ordinal succession comes from same-kind repetition plus lineage
Another-of-the-same, combined with one-way genesis, yields first/next/again.

**Adjudication:** retained. The ordering comes from lineage and does not inject quality into the registered quantity-content.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-063 — The floor yields ordinal succession and potential infinity only
Repeatability provides successor without an internally generated last, but not completed infinity, cardinality, equinumerosity, or magnitude.

**Adjudication:** retained as a hard scope boundary. Later mathematics must construct any stronger structure independently.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-064 — Containment order follows exhibited basis-entailment
Where satisfying `B2` entails satisfying `B1`, the `B2` holding is contained in the `B1` holding.

**Adjudication:** retained conditionally on an exhibited entailment route.
**Claim standing:** Registered under the stated condition.

## TSR-065 — Arbitrary basis-entailment is unavailable at the floor
Only lineage-exhibited entailments are licensed; no general decision procedure or lattice is yet earned.

**Adjudication:** retained as a live limitation.
**Claim standing:** Open beyond exhibited cases.

## TSR-066 — Holdings can be compared through shared actual standings
Because holdings do not absorb members, one standing may participate in multiple holdings, allowing overlap and inclusion comparisons without extraction to a neutral space.

**Adjudication:** retained at qualitative strength.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-067 — Floor comparison does not yield correspondence or magnitude
Overlap, inclusion, and exclusion do not by themselves provide one-to-one pairing, cardinal equality, or quantitative measure.

**Adjudication:** retained as a hard anti-smuggling boundary.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## Adjudicated limitations

1. Self-relation is never literal numerical reflexivity at this floor.
2. Quantity depends on actual distinctness plus basis-level quality blindness.
3. Potential infinity is not completed infinity.
4. Containment is available only where basis-entailment is exhibited.
5. Cardinality, correspondence, and magnitude require later independent construction.
