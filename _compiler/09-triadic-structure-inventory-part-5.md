Compile only living semantic structure. Examples are retained only where they perform a live clarifying or delimiting function. Deprecated routes and withdrawn claims are not promoted into semantic units.

## TSR-053 — Floor demonstrations must proceed by standing state, adjacency, move, and arrived content

**Kind:** execution protocol

**Content:** Each floor-level derivation begins from what already stands, identifies what is adjacent in `Realizability(S)`, performs a valid determining move, and states the content that has newly arrived. Familiar names, analogies, or later disciplinary structures cannot substitute for this four-part trace.

**Parents:** modal engine; adjacency; anti-smuggling rule.

**Spent by:** every floor-generated structure in §§6.2 onward.

**Standing:** forced method.

---

## TSR-054 — Self-relation and other-relation arise from two bases over the same pair of standings

**Kind:** generated distinction

**Content:** After the second standing, the first This and the first With both stand at unit-altitude as Thises. The same two actual standings may then be held under two factively available bases: a lower-level basis registering their different lineages and kinds, and a unit-altitude basis registering both simply as standings of the same kind.

**Parents:** second standing; With as holding-on-a-basis; held/registered distinction; altitude shift.

**Spent by:** quantity/quality split; W-notation; later binary holdings.

**Standing:** forced from the unfolding.

---

## TSR-055 — Other-relation is relation under a basis that registers difference of kind

**Kind:** definition from genesis

**Content:** A holding is other-relational when its basis registers the held positions as different in kind. Its content is difference-across-a-registered-cut.

**Parents:** TSR-054.

**Spent by:** quality-structured content; containment and comparison structures.

**Standing:** forced.

---

## TSR-056 — Self-relation is same-under-basis relation, not numerical reflexivity

**Kind:** definition and delimitation

**Content:** A holding is self-relational when its basis registers distinct actual standings as the same in kind. The relation is between two standings and returns only in registered character; it is not a standing held with numerically itself.

**Parents:** TSR-054; actual individuation by genesis.

**Spent by:** quantity-structured content; ordinal succession.

**Standing:** forced. Literal reflexive holding remains excluded from downstream use unless independently established.

---

## TSR-057 — Held distinctness and basis-registered content are different semantic layers

**Kind:** categorical distinction

**Content:** The positions in a holding are individuated by their distinct geneses, lineages, and addresses. The basis may register only that they are distinct, while remaining blind to what distinguishes them. Constitution comes from the build; content is what the basis registers.

**Parents:** With held/registered distinction; lineage; TSR-054.

**Spent by:** defense of quantity against the charge of smuggled quality; W-notation; later mathematical gate.

**Standing:** forced.

---

## TSR-058 — W-notation indexes registered character, not the inventory of held standings

**Kind:** notation control

**Content:** `W(A,A)` denotes two standings held under a basis that registers them as the same in kind. `W(A,B)` denotes a holding under a basis that registers a difference in kind. The letters identify basis-registered character, not the numerical or ontological identity of the held.

**Parents:** TSR-054–057.

**Spent by:** later floor logic and mathematical-domain constructions.

**Standing:** canonical notation rule.

---

## TSR-059 — Quantity-structured content is quality-blind holding of genuinely distinct actuals

**Kind:** generated content distinction

**Content:** Quantity arises where the holding registers multiplicity but no character-difference among the held positions. The positions remain genuinely distinct in the order of being; the quantity-content arises because the basis does not register the qualitative difference supplied by their distinct builds.

**Parents:** self-relation; held/registered distinction.

**Spent by:** succession; counting-in-sequence; later mathematical quantity.

**Standing:** forced within the framework.

---

## TSR-060 — Quality-structured content is basis-registered difference among held positions

**Kind:** generated content distinction

**Content:** Quality arises where the basis positively registers a difference in kind among the held positions. The relevant distinction is not mere numerical plurality but difference visible under the governing basis.

**Parents:** other-relation; held/registered distinction.

**Spent by:** predication; containment; qualitative comparison.

**Standing:** forced within the framework.

---

## TSR-061 — Quantity and quality are two holdings of one standing ground, not two stocks of relata

**Kind:** structural synthesis

**Content:** The same actual standings can support quantity-structured or quality-structured content depending on the basis under which they are held. The split is generated by With as holding-on-a-basis, not by dividing reality into inherently quantitative and inherently qualitative objects.

**Parents:** TSR-054–060.

**Spent by:** all later claims that quantity and quality diverge at the floor.

**Standing:** forced.

---

## TSR-062 — Ordinal succession is generated from same-kind repetition plus inherited lineage order

**Kind:** derivation

**Content:** Self-relation yields another-of-the-same. Because the positions are actual standings with one-way, non-commutative genesis, their lineage supplies an order: first, next, again. Succession therefore articulates build-order under a quality-blind basis without importing difference of kind into the quantity-content.

**Parents:** quantity-structured content; lineage; one-way From.

**Spent by:** potential infinity; mathematical counting-in-sequence.

**Standing:** forced.

---

## TSR-063 — The floor yields ordinal succession and potential infinity, but not cardinality or magnitude

**Kind:** scope boundary

**Content:** The repeatability of another-of-the-same gives a first, a successor, and no internally generated last. It does not yet give completed infinity, unordered cardinality, equality of size across collections, or magnitude.

**Parents:** TSR-062.

**Spent by:** prohibition on importing counting-in-a-bag or measure at the floor.

**Standing:** forced delimitation.

---

## TSR-064 — Containment order is generated by entailment between bases

**Kind:** derivation

**Content:** If satisfying basis `B2` entails satisfying basis `B1`, then the `B2`-holding is contained within the `B1`-holding. This yields directed and transitive order from basis-entailment without counting members or measuring holdings.

**Parents:** With as basis-holding; From as directed conditioning.

**Spent by:** sub-holding structure; later order by inclusion.

**Standing:** forced conditionally on exhibited basis-entailment.

---

## TSR-065 — Arbitrary basis-entailment is not available at the floor

**Kind:** scope boundary

**Content:** At floor grade, entailment between bases is certified only where the deeper basis has an exhibited compound lineage through the shallower. No general decision procedure for arbitrary basis pairs has been earned.

**Parents:** TSR-064; compound-basis construction downstream.

**Spent by:** prevention of premature lattice or topology claims.

**Standing:** live limitation.

---

## TSR-066 — Comparison of holdings proceeds through shared world-standing members without extraction

**Kind:** operation

**Content:** Because a With does not absorb its members, the same actual standing can participate in multiple holdings. Two holdings can therefore be compared by whether a standing is held by one, the other, both, or neither, without extracting members into a neutral comparison-space.

**Parents:** With as non-absorptive holding; composability of actual standings.

**Spent by:** overlap, inclusion, exclusion, and basis comparison.

**Standing:** forced at qualitative strength.

---

## TSR-067 — Floor-level comparison does not supply correspondence, cardinal equality, or magnitude

**Kind:** scope boundary

**Content:** Shared-member comparison can show overlap, non-overlap, inclusion, exclusion, and same/different holding. It does not generate a one-to-one correspondence between distinct members of different holdings, equal cardinality, or quantitative magnitude.

**Parents:** TSR-066.

**Spent by:** blocking any route from §6.6 directly to cardinality.

**Standing:** forced delimitation.

---

## Dependency and preservation effects

1. The quantity/quality distinction must preserve the held/registered distinction as its load-bearing parent.
2. `Self-relation` may not be rewritten as literal reflexivity.
3. Ordinal order at the floor comes from lineage; this lineage does not automatically cross later domain boundaries.
4. Containment and comparison remain qualitative unless a later domain independently builds quantitative correspondence or measure.
5. No later compiler pass may cite §6.6 as a source of cardinality or one-to-one pairing.
