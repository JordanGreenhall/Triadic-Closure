# CH3 Authority Adjudication

Semantic unit: CH3 — Maintenance relief, energetic ordering, and bonding interpretation.

## Claim 1 — chemistry compares closure-maintenance relief

The supplemental chemistry source proposes that chemical passages compare differences in closure-maintenance load rather than importing teleological minimization. P17 governs maintenance as the structural meaning of mass but does not derive a chemical interaction or energy functional. CHEM-006 retains the proposal as a structural interpretation and leaves quantitative magnitudes Open.

**Standing:** Conjectured structural interpretation; Unregistered as a quantitative chemistry law.

## Claim 2 — the symmetric mode is bonding and the antisymmetric mode antibonding

CH2 Secures only the mode decomposition. The labels require a selected native functional whose comparison shows that one mode reduces joint maintenance relative to separated standings and the other raises it or fails to relieve it. No such functional is currently constructed.

**Standing:** Conjectured conditionally as the intended interpretation; Unregistered as a derived physical ordering.

## Claim 3 — bond formation lowers energy

A numerical energy difference requires a common state space, interaction/maintenance functional, normalization, coupling, and comparison regime. None is supplied by P17, CH1, or CH2.

**Standing:** Open and Unregistered quantitatively.

## Governing formulation

CH3 may say that bonding is proposed as joint-maintenance relief and antibonding as the corresponding maintenance burden, but it may not infer ordering from symmetry alone or report a derived binding energy.
