## Preservation rule

The primordial unfolding is preserved as a dependency-ordered construction. No mythic or temporal reading is introduced. No prior object, prior medium, hidden substrate, or horizontally composed sub-standing may be imported at the origin.

---

## TSR-053 — The primordial unfolding has a restricted task

**Kind:** scope constraint

**Content:** The primordial section must show how the bare triadic constraint yields first determinate content without importing a prior object, prior medium, or hidden substance. It is not a narrative of first things.

**Source:** §5.1.

**Parents:** TSR-001 through TSR-052, especially primitive relating, the offices, vertical composition, the modal registers, and anti-smuggling discipline.

**Spent by:** TSR-054 through TSR-066.

**Standing:** Forced methodological restriction.

---

## TSR-054 — The origin is commingled closure rather than absent offices

**Kind:** primordial state

**Content:** At the origin, From, With, and This are fully commingled. The offices are present as the articulation of relating but are not yet distinguished as standing contents. Nothing stands apart; therefore no determinate content yet stands.

**Source:** §5.2.

**Parents:** relating as one non-composite act; determinate content requires distinct standing.

**Spent by:** TSR-055, TSR-056.

**Relations:** The origin is the condition for content, not itself the first determinate content.

**Standing:** Forced.

---

## TSR-055 — The first exercise is From because only From distinguishes

**Kind:** first primordial move

**Content:** With has nothing determinate to hold and This has nothing determinate to bring to standing until distinction occurs. Therefore the first exercise must be From, which articulates the commingled closure as From-as-such, With-as-such, and This-as-such.

**Source:** §5.3.

**Parents:** TSR-054; From as determinate distinction.

**Spent by:** TSR-056 through TSR-060.

**Relations:** This articulation does not create three entities; it distinguishes the offices of one closure.

**Standing:** Forced.

---

## TSR-056 — At the origin only vertical composition is available

**Kind:** primordial composition result

**Content:** Every This is composed in two dimensions, but horizontal composition requires prior sub-standings. Since no This has yet stood, the horizontal ledger is empty. Vertical composition requires only the act itself and is therefore available at the origin.

**Source:** §5.4.

**Parents:** vertical/horizontal distinction; TSR-054, TSR-055.

**Spent by:** TSR-057 through TSR-060.

**Standing:** Forced.

---

## TSR-057 — The first This is composed of offices, not prior objects

**Kind:** first-content construction

**Content:** The first This must arise through From-conditioning and With-holding. Nothing stands beneath relating, the offices are now articulated, and no prior standing exists. Therefore the only available constituents are the offices themselves: the first This is the relating of From and With.

**Source:** §5.4.

**Parents:** primitiveness posit; TSR-055, TSR-056; vertical composition.

**Spent by:** TSR-058 through TSR-061; all later claims that the first This is not an exceptional uncomposed atom.

**Relations:** The first This differs from later Thises only by having an empty horizontal ledger.

**Standing:** Forced by elimination, conditional only on the two foundational posits already carried by the source.

---

## TSR-058 — From contributes pure determination at the origin

**Kind:** office contribution

**Content:** At the first actualization, From contributes the cut, boundedness, and this/not-this. It contributes no material content of its own because From is contentless.

**Source:** §5.4.

**Parents:** From as determinate distinction and conditioning.

**Spent by:** TSR-060; minimal facetness of the first content.

**Standing:** Forced.

---

## TSR-059 — With contributes primordial Realizability

**Kind:** office contribution

**Content:** At the first actualization, With contributes structured coherence as the field of potential actuality. It is neither a prior content-object nor a thin capacity to hold.

**Source:** §5.4.

**Parents:** With as holding-on-a-basis; modal reading of With as Realizability.

**Spent by:** TSR-060 through TSR-063.

**Standing:** Forced.

---

## TSR-060 — The first Actual This is determination actualized within Realizability

**Kind:** first determinate content

**Content:** The first Actual This is the arrival of pure determination and primordial Realizability together. It is neither determination alone nor potential actuality alone, but the first standing of determinate content.

**Source:** §5.4.

**Parents:** TSR-057, TSR-058, TSR-059.

**Spent by:** TSR-061 through TSR-066; all later horizontal unfolding.

**Standing:** Forced.

---

## TSR-061 — The first This and the first actual With are co-actual

**Kind:** co-actualization result

**Content:** In the same act in which the first This stands, the primordial With stands as Realizability itself: the field that holds the first determinate content and excludes the indeterminate and non-content. The arrived one and its field of potential actuality appear together.

**Source:** §5.4.

**Parents:** TSR-059, TSR-060; double reading of one completed closure.

**Spent by:** TSR-062, TSR-064, TSR-065.

**Standing:** Forced.

---

## TSR-062 — Primordial Realizability differs from Realizability(S)

**Kind:** modal distinction

**Content:** Primordial Realizability is the field of potential actuality before any actuals condition it. Once the first standing state exists, Realizability becomes Realizability(S): potential actuality conditioned by what stands.

**Source:** §5.4.

**Parents:** TSR-061; modal engine.

**Spent by:** TSR-063 through TSR-066; every later stride.

**Standing:** Forced distinction.

---

## TSR-063 — The first determinate content is minimal

**Kind:** delimitation

**Content:** The first content has only the edge between determinate and indeterminate: this first standing against no standing. No richer contrast is yet available. The continuation of the unfolding depends on whether this minimal standing genuinely textures Realizability(S1).

**Source:** §5.4.

**Parents:** TSR-058, TSR-060; facetness.

**Spent by:** TSR-064, TSR-065.

**Relations:** Richer content must not be projected backward into the first standing.

**Standing:** Forced delimitation; the sufficiency test is discharged by the next step’s use of With’s actuality and This’s composability.

---

## TSR-064 — The primordial bootstrap begins from triadic form, not a prior actual

**Kind:** bootstrap rule

**Content:** The first stride cannot begin from a previous actual. Its condition is the triadic form itself functioning as the primitive may: relating conditions, coheres, and arrives as the first closure. No regress beneath this is admitted because nothing beneath relating is posited.

**Source:** §5.5.

**Parents:** primitive-relating posit; TSR-054 through TSR-060.

**Spent by:** all later stride-form claims.

**Standing:** Forced given the primitive-relating posit.

---

## TSR-065 — Every stride repeats the same form but not the same history

**Kind:** stride invariant

**Content:** Every stride repeats From-conditioning, With-cohering, and This-arrival. In form, each stride is symmetrical with the primordial closure. In history, every later stride begins from an enriched standing state S, so Realizability(S) differs from primordial Realizability and from every prior state.

**Source:** §5.5.

**Parents:** TSR-062, TSR-064; stride language.

**Spent by:** domain walks, horizontal accumulation, standing/building distinction.

**Relations:** The office-structure repeats while S changes.

**Standing:** Forced.

---

## TSR-066 — The second standing arises by taking the actual Realizability-With at unit-altitude

**Kind:** second primordial move

**Content:** Once S1 stands, the next move must come from Realizability(S1), not admissibility-at-large. No wholly new content-object has yet been generated, but the co-actual With now stands and is composable. It can therefore be taken as a unit at the next altitude and read as a This for further determination.

**Source:** §5.6.

**Parents:** TSR-061, TSR-062; actuality, composability, turn-over, and anti-smuggling rule.

**Spent by:** TSR-067 through TSR-070; the generated split between self-relation and other-relation in §6.2.

**Standing:** Forced.

---

## TSR-067 — Unit-altitude reveals sameness of standing and difference of lineage

**Kind:** altitude effect

**Content:** At unit-altitude, the first Actual This and the actual Realizability-With both stand as Thises and are therefore the same in standing-kind. They remain different in lineage and office-texture: one is This-as-This, the other With-as-This.

**Source:** §5.6.

**Parents:** TSR-066; held/registered distinction; layer and altitude.

**Spent by:** the two-basis construction of self-relation and other-relation.

**Standing:** Forced.

---

## TSR-068 — The second standing introduces the first layer-texture

**Kind:** first horizontal texture

**Content:** Because This is composable, the first This and With-as-This stand together and are actually held. The field now contains an actual, its actual holding-field, that field taken as a unit, and the relation between the two standings. This is the first layer-texture.

**Source:** §5.6.

**Parents:** TSR-066, TSR-067; With as holding; This as composable.

**Spent by:** §6.2 self-/other-relation and all later horizontal accumulation.

**Standing:** Forced.

---

## TSR-069 — Every completed determination has a double output

**Kind:** closure output theorem

**Content:** A completed determination yields both an arrived actual and a re-textured Realizability field. These are not two peer products but the This-reading and With-reading of one completed closure.

**Source:** §5.7.

**Parents:** closure, TSR-061, TSR-065, TSR-068.

**Spent by:** state updating, Realizability(S), all domain walks, field/occupant co-conditioning.

**Standing:** Forced.

---

## TSR-070 — No determination produces an isolated unit

**Kind:** consequence of double output

**Content:** Whenever a This stands, S changes; whenever S changes, Realizability(S) changes. Therefore the adjacency field after a determination differs from the adjacency field before it. An actual always stands while altering the field from which further actuals may be selected.

**Source:** §5.7.

**Parents:** TSR-069; modal engine.

**Spent by:** ecology and other field/occupant recognitions; domain-state transitions.

**Standing:** Forced.
