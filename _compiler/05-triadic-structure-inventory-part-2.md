# Triadic Structure Semantic Inventory — Part 2, Corpus-Adjudicated

Source locus: §§1.3–2.8 of the original `triadic-structure-of-relating-rev-canonical.md`.

Method: every semantic unit was checked against later control pages, the vertical/horizontal restatement, domain-entry work, the conversation-ledger audit, and downstream physics/chemistry usage. No later detailed work reverses these units. Corrections below separate warrant route from claim standing, prevent examples from carrying derivational load, and preserve the pressed links rather than presenting them as already discharged.

## TS-012 — Noise/sound/grammar is illustration only
Successive narrowing from noise to sound to articulated sound to grammar illustrates constraint as selection without addition.

**Adjudication:** retained solely as exposition; it carries no forcing load.
**Claim standing:** Registered as an example of TS-011.
**Warrant route:** recognition example.

## TS-013 — The offices are gerundive
From, With, and This are offices of an act—conditioning-from, holding-with, arriving-as-this—not static entities in a stock.

**Adjudication:** retained; later corpus work consistently depends on the verbal reading and rejects substrate-like office interpretations.
**Claim standing:** Registered.
**Warrant route:** construction from the primitiveness of relating.

## TS-014 — Closure is the triadic act standing as one
Closure is one act with three irreducible offices, not three independent relata assembled into a relation.

**Adjudication:** retained as the foundational meaning of closure. Later uses of “closure” in atoms, domains, and higher structures are applications of this same concept, not additional senses.
**Claim standing:** Registered and Sealed within the framework.
**Warrant route:** construction from the office triad.

## TS-015 — Each office is vertically composed through the other two
From is the relating of With and This; With is the relating of From and This; This is the relating of From and With.

**Adjudication:** retained. The corpus treats this as composition at full strength, not mutual implication or metaphor.
**Claim standing:** Registered.
**Warrant route:** construction from the gerundive offices and non-compositeness of relating.

## TS-016 — Vertical composition is mutual and baseless
No office is assembled from prior non-relational parts. Each is composed through the other two without a non-relational base.

**Adjudication:** retained. Later coinductive vocabulary is a recognition of this structure, not an additional premise.
**Claim standing:** Registered.
**Warrant route:** structural construction; coinduction is sealed recognition.

## TS-017 — Vertical composition is invariant across closures
Every closure at every horizontal stage is constituted by the same office relation. Vertical constitution does not accumulate or acquire history.

**Adjudication:** retained and corroborated by the later `vertical-and-horizontal` control page.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-018 — From is determinate distinction and conditioning
From marks a difference, preserves the distinguished sides as distinguished, and conditions what may follow. It contributes cut, position, and direction rather than independent content.

**Adjudication:** retained; later domain gates, lineage, polarity, and orientation all spend this formulation.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-019 — From is one-way and has no independent content
Conditioning proceeds from one side toward another; an inverse is not built into the cut. Independent content in From would turn the office into another standing thing.

**Adjudication:** retained with scope: reversibility may later arise in a constructed relation, but it is not primitive in From itself.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-020 — Polarity and non-degenerate pairing are two faces of From
Distinction yields side/complement; holding the distinction in relation yields pairing. These are not separately imported structures.

**Adjudication:** retained as a foundational structural consequence. Later uses of `J` and polarity are recognitions of this inherited structure.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-021 — From has two native directions
From→This conditions a successor standing; From→With conditions the medium in which realization is possible.

**Adjudication:** retained. Later domain-handoff and field-conditioning work depends on keeping these directions distinct.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-022 — From→With is relational medium-conditioning
Suitability or fertility belongs to the relation between a determinate possibility and its medium, not to the medium in isolation.

**Adjudication:** retained. Seed/soil and similar cases remain examples only.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-023 — With holds multiplicity under a basis
With holds a multiplicity as multiplicity under a rule, criterion, or coherence condition. It is neither an empty container nor a thin set.

**Adjudication:** retained and repeatedly spent by domains, predicates, bases, and realizability fields.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-024 — Held standings and basis-registered character are distinct
A With holds actual standings individuated by genesis and lineage; its basis registers only the respect under which they are included, compared, same, or different.

**Adjudication:** retained. This distinction is indispensable to the later held/registered discipline, quantity/quality split, and probability work.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-025 — Every determinate basis under-registers what it holds
A determinate basis is one respect rather than every respect. A basis that registered everything would exclude nothing and cease to be a determinate constraint.

**Adjudication:** retained, but the pressed dependency on facetness remains explicit. The compiler must not present the application of facetness to bases as independently re-proved here.
**Claim standing:** Registered conditional on the facetness argument.
**Open joint:** exact formalization of the pressed link.

## TS-026 — With and occupants co-condition one another
A realizability field is altered by what stands in it, and a standing is partly conditioned by the field in which it realizes.

**Adjudication:** retained at framework level. Ecosystem and gravitational examples do not independently prove it.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-027 — This is actuality at full strength
This is the arrived determinate standing: the “does,” rather than the merely admissible or realizable-given-what-stands.

**Adjudication:** retained; later actuality, closure, state, and occupancy claims spend this full-strength sense.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-028 — Every actual This is determinate, composed, composable, and conditioned
Determinateness gives bounded specificity; composedness includes vertical constitution and possible horizontal composition; composability makes the closure available to later holdings; conditionedness carries lineage and context.

**Adjudication:** retained. No later work removes any of the four faces.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-029 — A This is a genuine unit without being a substance
A This stands as one because closure has arrived as one. It is not simple, independent, or self-grounding.

**Adjudication:** retained as the framework’s replacement for substance-unit ontology.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-030 — Actual, standing, and closure are readings of one triadic unit
“Closure” reads the triadic act as one; “actual” reads it under arrival; “standing” reads it under availability for further determination.

**Adjudication:** retained as a controlled relation among terms, not as three distinct ontological objects.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TS-031 — Relatively stable closure replaces substance
A relatively stable closure can bear properties, support predication, remain individuated, and persist through change without existential independence.

**Adjudication:** retained. The closure structure is framework-native; “replacement of substance” is the later interpretive recognition.
**Claim standing:** Registered.
**Warrant route:** construction plus sealed recognition.

## TS-032 — Composition has vertical and horizontal dimensions
Vertical is synchronic office-constitution; horizontal is diachronic accumulation, re-texturing of realizability, strides, turn-overs, and nesting.

**Adjudication:** retained as canonical control. Later `vertical-and-horizontal.md` clarifies rather than supersedes it.
**Claim standing:** Registered and Sealed as framework vocabulary.

## TS-033 — Layer and altitude are horizontal
A layer is a completed closure taken as a unit at a later horizontal stage; altitude is position in that layer tower. Apparent spatial up/down does not make these vertical.

**Adjudication:** retained. Later domain descriptions must not use “vertical” for scale hierarchy.
**Claim standing:** Registered control rule.

## TS-034 — Modal nesting, layer nesting, and reading stances are distinct
Actual-within-realizable-within-admissible is modal nesting; closure-over-closure is horizontal layer nesting; standing/building are readings of horizontal stride. The vertical has no diachronic reading.

**Adjudication:** retained. This prevents false `J`/`rho`, emergence, and primordial-history problems.
**Claim standing:** Registered control distinction.

## Adjudicated limitations

1. Recognition examples carry no forcing load.
2. The facetness-to-basis link remains an explicit pressed dependency.
3. Co-conditioning does not collapse field and occupant into one object.
4. Closure has one foundational meaning across scales.
5. Vertical composition and horizontal accumulation remain categorically distinct.
