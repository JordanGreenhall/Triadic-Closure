## TSR-001 — The opening orientation begins from the absolutely unbounded possible

**Kind:** opening posit

**Content:** The account begins from the absolutely unbounded possible: everything admissible and wholly non-actualized. This is not a largest object, total set, empty container, or metaphysical stuff. It is the refusal to begin from any bounded actuality.

**Source:** §1.1, “The beginning: the unbounded under constraint.”

**Parents:** architectonic rigor and its prohibition on smuggled primitives.

**Spent by:** TSR-002, TSR-003, TSR-008; all subsequent constraint walks.

**Relations:** “admissible” here is used in a limit-case sense and must not be silently exchanged with the operational admissibility opened by a particular constraint.

**Standing:** posited in the source.

## TSR-002 — The first move is constraint, not creation from nothing

**Kind:** opening structural posit
