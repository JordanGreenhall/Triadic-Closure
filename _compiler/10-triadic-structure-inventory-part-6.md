Predication occurs when an actual basis is read as a criterion of inclusion and exclusion.

**Adjudication:** retained; linguistic `A is B` is recognition only.
**Claim standing:** Registered.

## TSR-061 — Predication may spend only an earned basis

**Adjudication:** retained as anti-smuggling control.
**Claim standing:** Registered.
