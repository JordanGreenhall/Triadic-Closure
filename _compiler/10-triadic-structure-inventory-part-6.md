## Preservation rule

Compile only living semantic content. Do not create semantic units for deprecated or eliminated formulations. Recognition labels are preserved only where they remain active constraints on use.

---

## TSR-060 — Predication is criterion-reading of an actual With

**Kind:** generated operation

**Content:** Predication first appears when an actual With is read as a criterion. A standing is held if it satisfies the basis and excluded if it does not. The later linguistic form `A is B` is recognition of this structure, not its source.

**Parents:** With as holding-on-a-basis; actual With; inclusion/exclusion.

**Spent by:** predicates, classification, basis-governed one-place questions.

**Standing:** forced.

---

## TSR-061 — A predicate may spend only an earned basis

**Kind:** anti-smuggling constraint

**Content:** A standing may be tested only against a basis already actual at the current level. A predicate requiring unbuilt magnitude, metric, or later-domain content cannot be used merely because it is admissible elsewhere.

**Parents:** TSR-060; trace-back; modal validity rule.

**Spent by:** all downstream predication and classification.

**Standing:** forced.

---

## TSR-062 — Conjunctive compound bases are generated from composable basis-standings

**Kind:** construction

**Content:** Deployed bases are standings and therefore composable. From can distinguish what satisfies both bases, producing a new basis whose holding is conjunctive. AND-like structure is generated from basis composability and overlap rather than imported from propositional logic.

**Parents:** composability of standings; From as distinction; With as basis-holding.

**Spent by:** compound criteria, structural entailment, containment order.

**Standing:** forced for actually deployed bases.

---

## TSR-063 — Disjunctive compound bases remain open at the floor

**Kind:** live boundary

**Content:** The floor construction forces conjunctive compounds but does not force disjunctive compounds. Joins and topology-like closure under unions cannot be spent at this level unless independently selected or later generated.

**Parents:** TSR-062.

**Spent by:** prohibition on premature OR, join, and topology claims.

**Standing:** Open.

---

## TSR-064 — Genuine binary holdings must depend on both positions

**Kind:** pruning rule

**Content:** Constant operations discard both positions, and projections depend on only one position. Neither is a genuine binary holding of the pair. The living binary family contains only structures whose result depends on both positions.

**Parents:** With as holding; two-position survey.

**Spent by:** floor-level binary structure and later logical recognition.

**Standing:** forced.

---

## TSR-065 — Genuine binary holdings divide into symmetric and asymmetric families

**Kind:** structural bifurcation

**Content:** Symmetric holdings register only agreement or difference and require no privileged value. Asymmetric holdings require an orientation supplied by a further determination of From's one-wayness.

**Parents:** TSR-064; From as oriented conditioning.

**Spent by:** XOR/XNOR recognition; conditional AND/OR route; later exchange-sector analysis.

**Standing:** forced at family level.

---

## TSR-066 — Logical names are sealed recognition over earned binary structures

**Kind:** recognition constraint

**Content:** XNOR and XOR may name the symmetric roots once those structures have been earned. AND, OR, NOT, and De Morgan structure require the additional orientation and complement conditions stated in the source. The names contribute no extra structure.

**Parents:** TSR-065; sealed recognition.

**Spent by:** later logic exposition.

**Standing:** recognition; structural roots retain their own forced standing.

---

## TSR-067 — Designated-value cashing of orientation is not derived at the floor

**Kind:** live hinge

**Content:** From supplies one-way orientation, but identifying that orientation with a privileged binary value is an identification rather than a derivation. If that identification is refused, the symmetric roots survive while the AND/OR reading falls.

**Parents:** TSR-065.

**Spent by:** limits on asymmetric logical interpretation.

**Standing:** Open as identification.

---

## TSR-068 — The floor-level sorter remains unbuilt

**Kind:** live operational gap

**Content:** The floor earns the pruned binary families and their symmetric/asymmetric distinction, but does not yet generate an internal operation whose office is to sort self-relation from other-relation.

**Parents:** self/other-relation split; TSR-065.

**Spent by:** prohibition on claiming a completed floor-level computation of the split.

**Standing:** Open.

---

## TSR-069 — Address is a record of recursive turn-over lineage

**Kind:** generated bookkeeping structure

**Content:** A closure may be surveyed under conditioning, holding, and arrived aspects. Taking an aspect as a unit for further determination is a turn-over. Iterated turn-overs generate an address recording the lineage of takings.

**Parents:** closure; composability; turn-over; horizontal accumulation.

**Spent by:** lineage tracking, recursive path structure, individuation by genesis.

**Standing:** forced.

---

## TSR-070 — Address generation is non-commutative because From is one-way

**Kind:** generated structural property

**Content:** The order of turn-overs matters. Reversing `FT` into `TF` would require inverting a one-way From and re-taking the sequence. Non-commutativity is therefore a lineage effect of one-way conditioning, not an imported algebraic axiom.

**Parents:** TSR-069; From as one-way.

**Spent by:** distinct path histories, territory differentiation, later algebraic recognition.

**Standing:** forced.

---

## TSR-071 — Unary finite addresses are reachable and remain distinct

**Kind:** reachability result

**Content:** In recursion taking one aspect at a time, every finite address can be walked, and distinct addresses remain distinct under non-commutative generation.

**Parents:** TSR-069, TSR-070.

**Spent by:** unary address space and path-structure inheritance.

**Standing:** forced.

---

## TSR-072 — Compound-address injectivity remains open

**Kind:** live boundary

**Content:** Once holdings themselves enter addresses as compounds, nesting is no longer merely string extension. The source does not establish that the walk-to-address map remains injective under arbitrary compound nesting.

**Parents:** TSR-071; compound holdings.

**Spent by:** prohibition on general compound-address uniqueness claims.

**Standing:** Open.

---

## TSR-073 — Territories are regenerative regions of generated addresses

**Kind:** generated regional structure

**Content:** A territory is a region of generated addresses sharing a regenerative signature under further turn-over. Distinguishability of such regions follows from non-commutativity.

**Parents:** TSR-069, TSR-070.

**Spent by:** later regional organization of recursive structures.

**Standing:** forced at the level of regenerative address regions.

---

## TSR-074 — Disciplinary names for territories remain recognition

**Kind:** recognition constraint

**Content:** Calling a generated territory topology, algebra, logic, or another established discipline is permitted only as sealed recognition after containment has been checked. The disciplinary name cannot contribute unearned structure.

**Parents:** TSR-073; sealed recognition.

**Spent by:** later domain and stratum naming.

**Standing:** recognition.

## Dependency and preservation effects

1. Predication, compound bases, binary holdings, and recursive addresses are distinct generated structures and must not be merged into a generic account of logic.
2. Conjunctive composition is living forced content; disjunctive composition remains open at the floor.
3. The symmetric binary roots survive independently of the unresolved designated-value identification.
4. Address lineage does not itself cross the mathematics gate; only its registered path-structure may cross.
5. No later rendering may claim arbitrary compound-address injectivity or a floor-level sorter unless a separate living source closes those joints.
