# Corpus Lineage — Adjudicated Semantic Inventory

Status: adjudicated governing compiler artifact.

## Canonical lineage rule

The corpus is a developmental lineage, not a stack of documents to aggregate. Documents are evidence-bearing containers. Authority attaches to semantic claims through their detailed derivation, repair history, downstream use, and explicit adjudication.

---

## CL-001 — The corpus must be read by lineage, not indiscriminate aggregation

**Canonical formulation:** Canonical, current, superseded, corrective, summary, process, and historical materials may not be averaged into one doctrine. Each semantic claim must be reconstructed through its complete lineage.

**Standing:** current governing rule.

**Spent by:** every compiler pass.

---

## CL-002 — Foundational dependency precedes domain expenditure

**Canonical formulation:** Architectonic rigor and triadic structure establish the prior whole; the domain-entry method specifies how that whole enters a domain; domain results spend and extend it under additional constraints. A downstream result may expose an ambiguity or required repair, but it may not silently redefine the foundation.

**Standing:** current governing order.

---

## CL-003 — Foundation and domain results have different roles, not absolute document ranks

**Canonical formulation:** Foundational sources establish offices, closure, modal structure, method, and handoff. Domain-result sources apply those structures and may sharpen what their domain use requires. Conflict is adjudicated claim by claim: a downstream result cannot silently override a foundational claim, while a detailed later repair may correct an earlier domain result or reveal that foundational wording needs clarification.

**Standing:** current control distinction.

---

## CL-004 — Historical conversation ledgers are evidence; explicit current rulings are authority for the adjudicated point

**Canonical formulation:** Historical conversation ledgers record critique, approval, rejection, repair, and developmental context but do not automatically convert all contained prose into theory. A direct present ruling by Jordan Hall governs the precise claim expressly adjudicated and must be propagated through affected compiler and corpus artifacts.

**Standing:** current source-role rule.

**Excluded error:** treating all conversation content as canonical, or excluding an explicit present ruling merely because it was delivered conversationally.

---

## CL-005 — Historical and superseded documents remain provenance

**Canonical formulation:** Material classified as superseded, historical, cautionary, task-forming, or provenance-only may explain development but may not supply living premises unless the semantic unit is independently retained or re-established by the current lineage.

**Standing:** current governing rule.

---

## CL-006 — `Current` is metadata, not conclusive semantic authority

**Canonical formulation:** A page labeled current is eligible evidence, not a guarantee that every sentence expresses the most advanced living result. Detailed derivations, later repairs, explicit adjudications, and downstream successful uses must be compared before a claim is compiled as current.

**Standing:** current compiler rule.

**Adjudication:** this replaces the weaker earlier rule that allowed a current result page to operate unless contradicted by a control page. The proton/electron mass-ratio failure demonstrated that current detailed and control pages can both retain obsolete formulations.

**Spent by:** all source adjudication.

---

## CL-007 — Process and verification artifacts are distinct from theory claims

**Canonical formulation:** Checklists, scripts, finite checks, and verification companions may test consequences, record failures, or govern execution. They do not establish the architectonic premises they presuppose. A run result is tied to a specific artifact and version.

**Standing:** current compiler rule.

---

## CL-008 — Supersession may be explicit or established by a demonstrated replacement lineage

**Canonical formulation:** Age, filename, and summary status do not establish supersession. Supersession is established by an explicit ruling or by a later detailed lineage that demonstrably repairs and replaces the earlier semantic claim. Partial supersession preserves unaffected living units.

**Standing:** current migration rule.

**Adjudication:** explicit control metadata is sufficient but not necessary when the detailed work itself clearly discharges, refutes, or replaces the older formulation.

---

## CL-009 — Detailed work outranks lossy summary for the claim summarized

**Canonical formulation:** A summary, status matrix, reading guide, or supersession overview may route the compiler to relevant material. It cannot override the most advanced coherent detailed derivation merely by being newer or labeled canonical/current.

**Standing:** current authority rule.

**Scope:** a summary may govern an explicit status ruling when that ruling is itself the substantive adjudication; it may not erase qualifications or replace mathematical and conceptual work it merely abbreviates.

---

## CL-010 — Downstream expenditure is adjudicative evidence

**Canonical formulation:** Later work that necessarily spends a semantic claim may sharpen its meaning, reveal hidden scope conditions, or show that an older formulation is unusable. Successful downstream expenditure is evidence in adjudication, though it does not automatically prove every premise it consumes.

**Standing:** current compiler rule.

---

## CL-011 — The target is one living semantic object with its unresolved joints

**Canonical formulation:** The compiler is neither an archive nor a consensus average. For each semantic object it must produce one current formulation, exact standing, warrant route, conditions, dependencies, expenditures, excluded revivals, open joints, propagation targets, and evidence ledger.

**Standing:** current governing objective.

---

## CL-012 — Absence from an incomplete compiler proves nothing about the framework

**Canonical formulation:** Until full adjudication is complete, failure to find a claim in `_compiler` may reflect incomplete ingestion, obsolete notation, or an unsearched replacement lineage. The compiler must return to the original corpus rather than infer abandonment.

**Standing:** current error-prevention rule.

---

## Source-specific cautions

1. Historical package reading orders are provenance, not present authority.
2. `phi-forward-reconstruction.md` is non-current unless a specific semantic unit is independently retained elsewhere.
3. A `verify.py 9/9` statement is a historical run report unless the exact target and script are rerun.
4. Tier lists and source maps are routing aids dated to their creation; every named file requires claim-level adjudication.
5. Overview pages may themselves contain stale doctrine and cannot be used as a substitute for reading the detailed source family.

## Compiler obligation

Every pre-existing `_compiler` inventory must be re-reviewed under CL-001 through CL-012. No unit is compiler-current merely because it has already been extracted.
