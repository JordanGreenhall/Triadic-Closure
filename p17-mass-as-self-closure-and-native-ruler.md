# P17 Mass as Self-Closure and Native Ruler

## Scope

P17 owns the nature of mass, the stay/advance decomposition, scoped recurrence as a mass-bearing standing, and the status of the electron bare repeat as a native ratio ruler.

P17 does not own the proton/electron numerical ratio, the three-face factorization, neutron splitting, decay energetics, absolute calibration, the full mass hierarchy, Higgs/Yukawa mass generation, or the numerical particle spectrum.

## Essential construction

A standing persists only by being re-selected through the passage that could otherwise advance beyond it. The portion of a passage borne in maintaining the same closure is the **stay** component; the portion expressed as onward change is the **advance** component.

Mass is the burden of self-maintenance: the standing's own closure repeatedly directed back through itself so that the identity-constituting invariant is re-instantiated step by step.

This is not a substance added to a particle and not an imported energy-compression law. It is the registered physical name for self-directed closure-maintenance within the displayed recurrence construction.

## Grades

### Mass as self-closure

Framework state: **Secured at structural scope**.

Semantic registration: **Registered, not Sealed** as mass.

Warrant: P6 persistence form plus exhibited recurrence in which a closure is re-selected as the same standing.

Reach: the nature of mass as self-maintenance and the stay/advance distinction.

Debt: no universal theorem that every physically massive standing is exhausted by this construction.

### Electron bare repeat

The color-trivial electron role can recur without the additional internal color-closure burden carried by the modeled baryon. Within the framework's ratio calculus, this bare repeat is the minimal displayed mass-bearing recurrence.

Framework state: **Secured at the displayed recurrence scope**.

Semantic registration: **Registered, not Sealed** as the native electron ruler `m_e = 1`.

This is an internal normalization, not a derivation of kilograms, electron-volts, or the measured electron mass.

### Persistence and stability

Per-step recurrence supplies a scoped P6 persistence witness. Recurrence alone does not supply perturbative stability. A broader claim that the electron is the ontologically basic stable ruler requires an explicit variation-class and return construction.

### Form of a value

A mass value is natively a ratio of self-maintenance counts or measures relative to a selected ruler. P17 Registers that ratio-form program. It does not provide a step/whole calibration anchor connecting native ratios to external physical units.

## Quarantine

P17 does not derive:

- the complete proton/electron mass ratio;
- the `6 pi^5` color-seating factor or its correction bracket;
- neutron/proton splitting;
- binding-energy decomposition;
- absolute masses or conversion to external units;
- the ordered stable-closure cascade;
- Yukawa couplings, Higgs vacuum structure, or the fermion mass hierarchy.

These remain with P18–P21 or P29.
