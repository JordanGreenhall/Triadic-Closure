# Triadic Closure — Spine and Protocol

## 1. What this corpus is

Triadic Closure is a foundational architectonics: a construction of mathematics, physics, and chemistry from three offices of one act of relating — **From, With, and This**. It begins from the unbounded possible under constraint, secures its floor by retorsion, carries the primitiveness of relating and the exactly-three count as defended posits at the maximal warrant available to claims of their kind, and enters each successor domain by narrowing rather than importing.

The live corpus states the best current form of the framework. It contains current claims, their arguments, their exact standings, and their open boundaries. It does not carry provenance, supersession history, process logs, or memorials of abandoned routes.

## 3. Claim standing and warrant

A claim's **live status** and the **instrument that warrants it** are distinct.

### Live statuses

- **Open** — explicitly not closed; a genuine work frontier.
- **Lead** — a direction with stated warrant and a named owed check; not yet a conjecture.
- **Conjectured** — a plausible proposal not yet registered.
- **Conjectured-strong** — the structural form or ingredients are substantially constrained, but the native construction or magnitude remains incomplete.
- **Registered-candidate** — a new result on registered parents awaiting adversarial review before it enters standing use.
- **Registered** — the framework reaches the item in essence or under stated conditions, while some closure remains incomplete.
- **Registered and Sealed** — at the stated scope, the item carries no content beyond the registered structure and the registered structure carries no unaccounted remainder.
- **Dissolved** — a purported problem or demand is removed as a category error rather than answered.

### Warrant instruments

- **Retorsion-secured** — denial necessarily deploys the denied condition.
- **Forced** — secured by an exhibited dependency or construction.
- **Selected** — admitted by an exhibited boundary but not uniquely compelled.
- **Recognition** — a downstream name attached only after the structure is earned; surplus remains quarantined.
- **Defended posit** — a completeness claim at the universal certification ceiling, carried by a forced floor, survived rival examination, and reflexive stability.

A defended posit is not an unfinished theorem or a low-confidence status. It is the maximal warrant available to any exhaustive foundational inventory. It remains refutable by a genuine counterexample, not open as an owed proof task.

**Standing rule:** never strengthen or weaken a claim beyond the reach of its warrant. A check is not a proof; a selection is not forced; a measure is not an observed frequency; a structural relation is not a first-principles derivation of every mature particular.

## 4. Vertical and horizontal

- **Vertical** — synchronic office-composition: each office composed through the other two; admissibility; closure-as-closure; the home of J. It does not accrue and does not vary by historical depth.
- **Horizontal** — diachronic accumulation: actuals accumulate; Realizability(S) is re-textured; strides, layers, nesting, and the home of rho.

Layers and nesting are horizontal even when their ordinary presentation feels hierarchical. The standing face is the vertical reading of a horizontal stride. Collapsing the two creates false tensions between J and rho, between primordial constitution and accumulated structure, and between the three offices and the D-series.

## 5. Execution discipline

1. **Use the canonical owner.** Work from the principal document and any named technical appendix for the topic. Do not average parallel formulations.
2. **No theory invention.** Do not manufacture proof requirements, infer missing constructions, create frontiers, or reconcile a genuine unresolved conflict from outside the corpus.
3. **No content smuggling.** Mature disciplinary vocabulary may be attached only after its framework structure is earned. External constants and observations enter only through an explicit translation bridge.
4. **No smuggled demands.** A question that presupposes an ontology the framework has already refused is tested before it is accepted as an obligation. If the distinction is category-invalid, the result is Dissolution.
5. **Load-first reasoning.** Determine the exact downstream job of a concept before demanding more of it. A claim need not reproduce every mature association of its later name.
6. **Whole inheritance.** A successor domain receives every constraint of the inherited object; it may not select convenient features and ignore the rest.
7. **Three moves at every stage.** Locate the dependency position; construct from the standing stock; grade the result and record its exact downstream license.
8. **Real computation only.** A computation must be independently specified and capable of returning against the claim. Mechanical string, link, and arithmetic checks have no semantic authority.
9. **Exact-result protection.** High-risk expressions must occur identically in their canonical owner and in `EXACT-RESULTS.md`.
10. **Open boundaries remain visible.** Every principal treatment states what is current, what conditions it inherits, what remains open, and what may not yet be spent.

## 6. Failure controls

- Do not treat vertical constitution as horizontal accumulation.
- Do not attach a mature name before the earned structure is stated.
- Do not convert argued into proved, candidate into standing, selection into force, or weighting into frequency.
- Do not ask a concept to bear content that its actual downstream use does not require.
- Do not hard-code a desired result and report the output as verification.
- Do not inherit G1 outside the spatial manifold/order sector.
- Do not truncate exact formulas. In particular,

\[
\frac{m_p}{m_e}
=
6\pi^5\left[1+c(3\pi^4)^{-2}\right],
\qquad
\frac32\le c\le\frac94.
\]

The factor \(6\pi^5\) is the exact With–This seating factor. It is not the proton/electron mass ratio.

## 7. Maintenance rule

New work enters the document that owns the claim. Useful technical detail is placed in the relevant appendix only when absorbing it would materially impair the principal document. Once current value is absorbed, the source container is removed. The live corpus remains a statement of the framework, not an account of how the statement evolved.
