## 1. Invariant additive maintenance

Let a closure-orbit \(X\) carry a transitive action by its closure symmetry \(G\), and let \(\mu\) be the normalized invariant measure. A maintenance assignment \(M\) must be positive, additive on disjoint registered contributions, and invariant under \(G\):

\[
M(A\sqcup B)=M(A)+M(B),\qquad M(gA)=M(A).
\]

The normalized invariant measure on a compact homogeneous space is unique. Hence

\[
M(A)=\alpha\mu(A).
\]

Choosing the native electron closure as unit fixes \(\alpha\). Therefore mass ratios are ratios of invariant closure measures. The proof does not identify mass with an arbitrary geometric volume; it identifies maintenance with the unique invariant additive measure on the closure space generated by the standing.
