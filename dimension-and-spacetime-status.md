1. **With-flattening:** horizontal flattening removes the offices' qualitative marking while conservation retains their exact count. The result is three distinct but interchangeable degrees along which co-standing extent may be articulated.

4. **Signature germ:** the temporal member is orientation-distinguished from the three spatial members. That difference supplies the `1|3` germ which P5 may later commensurate as a Lorentzian metric.

Nothing in this construction requires the P3 exchange sectors. Nothing requires a second proof that the inherited images remain present under the physics boundary: P2 already carries them whole, and P4 introduces no operation that removes or alters them.
